# 54. Coronal Mass Ejections and Their Sheath Regions in Interplanetary Space / 행성간 공간에서의 코로나 질량방출 및 시스 영역

---

## 1. Core Contribution / 핵심 기여

Kilpua, Koskinen & Pulkkinen (2017) provide a comprehensive review of interplanetary coronal mass ejections (ICMEs) and, critically, their upstream **sheath regions** — the turbulent layer of shocked and compressed solar wind that forms between an ICME-driven fast-mode shock and the ICME leading edge when the driver moves faster than the surrounding plasma by more than the local fast magnetosonic speed ($M_{ms} > 1$). Whereas earlier reviews (Chen 2011; Webb & Howard 2012) focused predominantly on the ICME / magnetic cloud proper, this article gives equal weight to the sheath and documents that **sheaths and ICMEs are physically distinct structures with different origins, signatures, plasma parameters, and geospace consequences**. The review spans five decades of ICME research: (i) observational signatures (enhanced B, smooth rotation, depressed Tp/β, He/p and charge-state anomalies, bidirectional electrons, Forbush decreases); (ii) modelling of magnetic clouds (Lundquist constant-α force-free, Grad–Shafranov, non-force-free elliptical, spheroidal, toroidal global geometries, twist distributions); (iii) sheath physics (formation, ULF turbulence, PMS, PUC, draping); (iv) solar-cycle variations and radial evolution out to ~58 AU; and (v) space weather (Dst storms, auroral activity, radiation belts, atmospheric effects, planetary ionospheric loss at Mars/Venus, and implications for exoplanets).

Kilpua, Koskinen, Pulkkinen (2017) 은 행성간 코로나 질량방출 (ICME) 과 특히 그 앞에 형성되는 **시스 영역 (sheath region)** — ICME 구동 속도가 주변 태양풍의 fast magnetosonic 속도를 초과 ($M_{ms} > 1$) 할 때 발생하는 fast-mode 충격파와 ICME 선두 사이에 압축·난류화된 태양풍 층 — 에 대한 종합 리뷰를 제시한다. 이전 리뷰 (Chen 2011; Webb & Howard 2012) 가 주로 ICME/자기 구름 본체에 집중한 반면, 본 논문은 시스에도 동등한 비중을 두어 **시스와 ICME 는 기원, 관측 신호, 플라즈마 변수, 지구 자기권 반응이 모두 구별되는 물리적으로 별개의 구조** 임을 문서화한다. 50년간의 ICME 연구를 아우르며: (i) 관측 신호 (증강된 B, 부드러운 회전, 낮은 Tp/β, He/p 및 전하 상태 이상, 양방향 전자 흐름, Forbush 감소); (ii) MC 모델링 (Lundquist, Grad–Shafranov, 비 force-free, 구면/토러스 기하학, 트위스트 분포); (iii) 시스 물리 (형성, ULF 난류, PMS, PUC, 자력선 드레이핑); (iv) 태양 주기 변동과 58 AU 까지의 radial 진화; (v) 우주 날씨 (Dst 자기폭풍, 오로라 활동, 복사대, 대기 효과, 화성/금성의 이온 손실, 외계행성 시사점) 를 다룬다.

## 2. Reading Notes / 읽기 노트

### Part I: Introduction & ICME Signatures (Sect. 1–2, pp. 2–20) / 서론과 ICME 신호

**Historical foundation (p. 3):** "Plasma clouds" were suggested from geomagnetic disturbances (Lindeman 1911; Chapman & Ferraro 1929) and Forbush decreases (Forbush 1937) decades before the discovery of CMEs. The 1970s coronagraphs (OSO-7; Tousey 1973; Hildner 1977) and first in-situ ICMEs (Hirshberg et al. 1970; Gosling et al. 1973) established the CME–ICME link. / CME 발견 이전부터 지자기 교란 (Lindeman, Chapman-Ferraro) 과 포부시 감소 (Forbush) 로부터 "플라즈마 구름" 이 추론되었다.

**General ICME signatures (Sect. 2.1, pp. 4–8):** The Burlaga et al. (1981) *magnetic cloud (MC)* definition requires three criteria near 1 AU:

1. $|\mathbf{B}| > 10$ nT (enhanced over ambient solar wind ~5 nT)
2. Smooth rotation of the B direction through a large angle (~180°) over ~1 day
3. Depressed proton temperature: $T_p < 0.5\, T_{exp}(v_{sw})$ (Richardson & Cane 1995), equivalently low plasma β.

Additional ICME tracers: elevated He/p (>0.08, sometimes 0.1); elevated O⁷⁺/O⁶⁺ ratio >1.0 (Henke et al. 2001); average iron charge state >12 (Lepri et al. 2001) indicating hot source regions; bidirectional suprathermal electrons (~100 eV) indicating closed field topology; Forbush decrease of 20–25% in galactic cosmic rays (Fig. 3). / MC 의 세 조건: B>10 nT, 부드러운 회전, 낮은 Tp. 추가 지표: He/p, O⁷⁺/O⁶⁺, Fe 전하 상태, 양방향 전자, 포부시 감소.

**Flux ropes in ICMEs (Sect. 2.2, pp. 8–11):** Only ~1/3 of ICMEs show clear MC signatures (Gosling 1990; Cane & Richardson 2003). Reasons: spacecraft path not through flux-rope core (Jian et al. 2006 Group 3: glancing pass), interaction/merging erosion (Lavraud et al. 2014: up to 40% flux loss), or different source conditions. Eight flux-rope types (Fig. 5, Bothmer & Schwenn 1998): four bipolar low-inclination (SEN-LH, SWN-RH, NES-RH, NWS-LH) and four unipolar high-inclination (WNE-RH, ESW-RH, ENW-LH, WSE-LH), classified by leading/central/trailing field direction and helicity sign H = ±1. / 전체 ICME 의 약 1/3 만 명확한 MC 신호를 보이는 이유: 우주선 경로, 침식/병합, 기원 차이. 8가지 플럭스로프 유형.

**Identifying ICME boundaries (Sect. 2.3):** Often ambiguous. Wei et al. (2003a) found boundary layers of 1.7 h (front) / 3.1 h (rear) duration. Boundary layers show increased n, T, decreased B, abrupt B-direction changes — signatures of magnetic reconnection. / ICME 경계는 종종 모호. 경계층은 n, T 증가, B 감소, reconnection 신호.

Key takeaways: MCs have B_avg ~13 nT (2.5× ambient SW); duration ~21 h (~1/3 AU at 1 AU); 85–88% are expanding (ΔV<0 from leading to trailing edge); MC and non-MC ICMEs have similar speeds — ICMEs equilibrate to ambient SW speed near Earth. / MC 는 주변 SW 대비 2.5배 강한 B, 약 21시간 지속, 85-88% 가 팽창.

**Expansion rate (Eq. 1, p. 14):**
$$\psi = \frac{\Delta V_x}{\Delta t} \frac{D}{V_C^2}$$
Unperturbed MCs expand at similar non-dimensional rate ψ. Expansion speed ~0.5 $v_A$ in SW frame. / MC 의 자기 유사 팽창률.

**Large-scale structure (Sect. 2.5):** Multi-spacecraft analyses (Burlaga et al. 1990 for Jan 1978 event detected by IMP-8, Helios A&B, Voyager 1&2) show ICMEs are curved flux-rope loops extending back to the Sun. Janvier et al. (2015) reconstructed average global MC shape. Cross-sections are highly oblate: aspect ratios 4:1 to 6:1 (Liu et al. 2006; Mulligan et al. 1999), extreme case 8:1 (Crooker & Intriligator 1996). Good & Forsyth (2016) multi-mission statistics: 1/3 of ICMEs detected at 2+ spacecraft when separated by 30°, only 12% when separated by 45–60°. Witasse et al. (2017) reported ICME extending >100° in longitude. / 다 우주선 관측: ICME 는 태양까지 연결된 곡선 플럭스로프 루프. 단면은 매우 납작 (4:1 – 8:1). 종방향 확장 때로 100° 초과.

### Part II: Modelling Magnetic Clouds (Sect. 3, pp. 20–28) / 자기 구름 모델링

**Basic approach (Sect. 3.1):** Goldstein (1983) suggested MCs can be described as cylindrical force-free flux ropes. Burlaga (1988) took $\alpha$ = constant. The Lundquist (1950) solution uses zeroth- and first-order Bessel functions:

$$B_R = 0, \qquad B_A = B_0 J_0(\alpha r / r_0), \qquad B_T = H B_0 J_1(\alpha r / r_0)$$

where $r_0$ is MC radius, $B_0$ is axial maximum field (at r=0), and $H = \pm 1$ is helicity sign. Pitch angle increases from axis to boundary; $|B|$ peaks at r=0 and drops near the edge where $J_0(\alpha r/r_0) \to 0$ at $\alpha r/r_0 \approx 2.405$. Lepping et al. (1990) developed the least-squares fitting technique. Asymmetries arise from interaction with ambient solar wind. / Lundquist 해: 원통대칭 force-free. 중심에서 B 최대, 경계로 갈수록 나선각 증가.

**Grad–Shafranov (GSR, Sect. 3.2, Eq. 5):**

$$\frac{\partial^2 A}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 A}{\partial y^2} = -\mu_0 \frac{d}{dA}\!\left(p + \frac{B_z^2}{2\mu_0}\right)$$

GSR (Hu & Sonnerup 2002) assumes 2.5-D translational symmetry and reconstructs cross-sectional shape, helicity sign, axis orientation, and impact parameter. Strengths: derives boundaries, handles non-force-free cases. Weaknesses: severely underestimates cross-section elongation (Riley et al. 2004; Möstl et al. 2009; Kilpua et al. 2011); cannot handle significantly interacting MCs; Al-Haddad et al. (2013) noted GSR may reconstruct a "helical" flux rope from non-helical data. / Grad–Shafranov 재구성: 단면 복원 가능하나 납작 비율 과소추정.

**Global structure (Sect. 3.3):** Toroidal models (Marubashi & Lepping 2007) often fit better than cylindrical for flank encounters (Fig. 15c, path F). Owens (2016): double flux-rope encounters rare → legs are bundles of stretched, curved field lines. Alternative: spheroidal MC (Fig. 15d). Möstl et al. (2012): MC of August 2010 had different axial inclinations at Venus Express and STEREO-B separated by 20° → ICMEs deform ("warp") during propagation. / 글로벌 구조: 토러스 모델이 측면 충돌에 우수. ICME 는 전파 중 변형.

**Twist (Sect. 3.4):** Hu et al. (2015) and Wang et al. (2016) found that twist distribution is roughly **uniform** throughout the cloud, contradicting Lundquist's prediction of monotonically increasing twist from axis to boundary. Typical twist ~few turns per AU. Critical kink instability threshold: ~1.25 turns per axis length for line-tied force-free flux rope (Hood & Priest 1981; Török & Kliem 2005). / 트위스트: 관측은 거의 균일한 분포, Lundquist 와 불일치.

### Part III: ICME Shocks and Sheaths (Sect. 4, pp. 29–39) / ICME 충격파와 시스 — *핵심 섹션*

**Formation (Sect. 4.1, Fig. 17):** For an ICME to drive a fast forward shock, its speed in the solar wind frame must exceed the local fast magnetosonic speed. The Alfvén Mach number $M_A = V/v_A$ and magnetosonic Mach number $M_{ms} = V/v_{ms}$ are the key dimensionless parameters. Since ambient SW (~400 km/s) and ICME speeds (~450–500 km/s average; up to 2500 km/s for extremes) are similar, **most interplanetary shocks are not strong**: Kilpua et al. (2015b) found annual medians of $M_{ms}$ during 1995–2013 did not exceed 4. Rare exceptions: 28 April 2001 ($M_{ms} = 12.6$, Wind) and 27 March 1979 ($M_{ms} = 11.7$, Helios-B at 0.75 AU). Extreme 23 July 2012 STEREO-A event: Riley et al. (2016) found $M_A = 21$, $M_{ms} = 17$ — among the strongest shocks ever recorded. / ICME 가 fast forward shock 을 구동하려면 태양풍 기준계에서 fast magnetosonic 속도를 초과해야 함. 대부분 시스 충격파는 약함 ($M_{ms} < 4$), 극단 사례 (2012-07-23) 에서 $M_A=21$.

**Sheath signatures vs. ICME (Sect. 4.2, Fig. 18):** Probability distributions of various solar wind parameters in 94 sheaths (black) vs. 117 ICMEs (red) from Kilpua et al. (2015a) show:

| Parameter / 변수 | Sheath | ICME | Difference / 차이 |
|---|---|---|---|
| B [nT] | peak ~10, tail to 40 | peak ~12, tail to 30 | similar |
| B_z [nT] | ±20 (wide) | ±20 (wide) | similar range |
| B_RMS [nT] | **pronounced tail** | narrow | sheaths more turbulent |
| V [km/s] | 400–800 | 400–800 | similar |
| n_p [cm⁻³] | **higher** (peak ~10) | lower (peak ~5) | compression |
| T_p [eV] | **higher** (tail to 40) | low (<10) | shocked heating |
| P_dyn [nPa] | **higher tail** (to 25) | to 10 | compressed |
| β | **β ~1–5 common** | β ≪ 1 | plasma-dominated vs. field-dominated |
| $M_A$ | **higher** (peak ~6) | lower (peak ~3) | |
| He/p | similar peak | wider tail | ICME tail to 0.25 |
| O⁷⁺/O⁶⁺ | low | tail to 2.5 | ICME only |
| Fe/O | low | tail to 1.4 | ICME only |

Key: sheaths are turbulent, compressed, hot, high-β plasma-dominated; ICMEs are smooth, expanding, cold, low-β field-dominated. ULF fluctuation power (Fig. 19, 3–10 min IMF Bz) sharply drops at sheath–ICME boundary. / 시스: 난류·압축·뜨거운 고 β; ICME: 매끈·팽창·저온 저 β. ULF 파워는 경계에서 급강하.

**Radial extent:** Mean sheath: 11.1 h / 0.13 AU. Mean ICME: 26.5 h / 0.29 AU. Sheaths accumulate from shock towards ICME; thickness depends on ICME speed, shape, and compression ratio. Most "driverless" shocks are sheath-only encounters where the spacecraft misses the ejecta. / 시스: 평균 11시간/0.13 AU. ICME: 26.5시간/0.29 AU.

**Large-scale sheath structures (Sect. 4.3):** Two types: **propagation sheath** (like planetary magnetosheaths, plasma flow deflects around obstacle) + **expansion sheath** (obstacle grows). ICME sheaths are a hybrid. Plasma accumulates at the nose because lateral deflection is insufficient. IMF draping (Fig. 20, Gosling & McComas 1987; Siscoe et al. 2007): field lines drape around the ICME, producing out-of-ecliptic Bz components from initially ecliptic Parker-spiral field — **key for space weather** as draped southward Bz drives reconnection. **Planar Magnetic Structures (PMSs):** intervals where B vectors remain parallel to a single plane (Nakagawa et al. 1989); formed by amplification + alignment of pre-existing SW discontinuities at the shock. **Pile-Up Compression (PUC)** region just inside the MC leading edge with n up to 30 cm⁻³. / Propagation + expansion sheath 혼합. IMF 드레이핑 → out-of-ecliptic Bz 생성. PMS, PUC 구조.

**Particle acceleration (Sect. 4.4):** Diffusive shock acceleration (DSA, Axford et al. 1977; Bell 1978; Drury 1983). Gradual SEP events: proton-rich, days-long, associated with CME shocks; impulsive: ³He-rich, electron-rich, hours, flare-related. Kahler et al. (1984): almost all gradual SEPs are CME-associated. For DSA to work, fluctuations both upstream and downstream must scatter accelerated particles back across the shock. Near the Sun shocks are quasi-parallel (radial B); further out they become more quasi-perpendicular. Trottet et al. (2015): both flares and shocks contribute to MeV-proton and near-relativistic electron populations. / DSA 확산 충격파 가속. Gradual SEP ↔ CME 충격파. 태양 근처 quasi-parallel, 멀어지면 quasi-perpendicular.

**Worked numerical example — Sheath compression (ASCII sketch):** For an ICME driving a fast-forward shock at $M_A = 5$ with upstream ambient B = 5 nT, n = 5 cm⁻³, V_sw = 400 km/s, V_ICME = 600 km/s (relative V_rel = 200 km/s in the shock frame):

```
   upstream (pre-shock)    │ SHOCK │   downstream (sheath)
                           │       │
   B = 5 nT                │       │   B ≈ 15–25 nT  (factor 3–5)
   n = 5 cm⁻³              │       │   n ≈ 15–20 cm⁻³
   T = 10^5 K              │       │   T ≈ 4–8 × 10^5 K
   β ≈ 0.3                 │       │   β ≈ 1–3
                                        ↑  turbulent, B_RMS high
                                        ↑  drives intense Dst + AE
```

Compression ratio approaches 4 (γ=5/3) in the strong-shock limit; B tangential component compresses by same factor at a perpendicular shock. Sheath draping generates out-of-ecliptic Bz up to ±15 nT from initially ecliptic Parker-spiral fields. / 시스 압축 예시: M_A=5 에서 B 가 3-5배, 밀도 3-4배, 온도 4-8배 증가. β 가 이동하여 플라즈마 지배로 전환.

### Part IV: Solar Cycle & Heliospheric Variations (Sect. 5, pp. 39–50) / 태양 주기와 일권 변동

**Solar cycle (Sect. 5.1, Fig. 22):** Near solar minimum ~few ICMEs/year; solar maximum >50/year. ~40–60% of solar wind is ICME-related near solar maximum vs. 5% at minimum. Bz polarity of MCs follows Hale 22-year cycle: NS rotation dominates rising phase of odd cycles, SN in even cycles (Bothmer & Schwenn 1998; Li et al. 2011). / 태양 주기에 따라 ICME 빈도 5-50배 차이. MC 극성은 22년 Hale 주기 따름.

**Large heliospheric distances (Sect. 5.2, Figs. 25–27):** Richardson et al. (2006) combined Helios–Voyager data (0.3–30 AU): radial width W = 0.13 + 0.14R [AU], so ICMEs grow from 0.17 AU at 0.3 AU to ~2.2 AU at 15 AU then saturate. Power-law fits: $n_p \propto R^{-2.21}$ (ICME) vs. $R^{-2.08}$ (SW); $T \propto R^{-0.72}$; $B \propto R^{-1.36}$ (ICME) vs. $R^{-1.80}$ (SW) — ICME B decreases more slowly than ambient. / ICME 은 15 AU 까지 팽창 후 압력 평형. 밀도·B 거듭제곱 감쇠.

**Off-ecliptic (Ulysses, Sect. 5.3):** First/third scans (solar min): ICMEs confined to <40° lat. Second scan (solar max): ICMEs up to 80°. High-latitude ICME speeds average 740 km/s (Gosling et al. 1995). / Ulysses 고 위도 관측: 극대기에는 80°, 극소기에는 저위도 제한.

**Interactions (Sect. 5.4, Figs. 29, 30):** Merging ICMEs form "complex ejecta" (Burlaga et al. 2002). Interacting CMEs produce the strongest fields: 23 July 2012 reached B = 109 nT at 1 AU (Liu et al. 2014). Erosion by reconnection at MC boundaries: up to 40% of azimuthal flux can be lost (Ruffenach et al. 2015). / CME-CME 상호작용: 복합 이젝타, 극강 B (109 nT), 침식 40%.

**Quantitative ICME-speed examples (Table 1 statistics):** Slow CMEs: v₀ ~400 km/s, arrive in ~4 days, no shock, merge into ambient SW. Average ICMEs: ⟨V_max⟩ ~500 km/s, ~23 h at 1 AU, drive moderate shocks $M_A \sim 2$–3. Fast halo CMEs: v₀ ~1000–1500 km/s, arrive in ~30–45 h, drive $M_A \sim 5$ shocks, thick sheaths (~0.2 AU). Extreme: 23 July 2012 v₀ ~2500 km/s, arrived at STEREO-A in 18.6 h, $M_A = 21$, $M_{ms} = 17$, B = 109 nT at 0.99 AU (Liu et al. 2014 — had this hit Earth it would have been Carrington-class). 28 Oct 2003 "Halloween" v₀ = 2459 km/s, Dst = −363 nT; 13 Mar 1989 Quebec blackout event. / 속도별 ICME 특성: 400-2500 km/s 범위에서 도달 시간 18 h-4일.

### Part V: Space Weather (Sect. 6, pp. 51–65) / 우주 날씨

**Magnetospheric storms (Sect. 6.1, Fig. 31):** Dungey cycle: reconnection opens dayside field when IMF Bz southward. Ring current grows → Dst drops. Burton et al. (1975) equation (paraphrased; pressure-corrected Dst*):

$$\frac{dDst^*}{dt} = -\frac{Dst^*}{\tau} + Q(E_y), \qquad E_y = V B_s$$

where Bs is the southward IMF magnitude (zero if Bz > 0), V is solar wind speed, τ ≈ 5–10 h. Gonzalez et al. (1994) criteria: intense storm (Dst < −100 nT) requires Bz < −10 nT for ≥3 h. Huttunen & Koskinen (2004): sheaths are especially effective storm drivers because of high ULF fluctuations, high dynamic pressure, high M_A, and high dawn-dusk electric field saturation. / Dst 방정식. 시스는 높은 ULF, 동역학압, M_A, E_y 로 효과적 폭풍 구동.

**Storm driver statistics (Fig. 33, Huttunen & Koskinen 2004):** For 1997–2002 intense storms (Dst<−100 nT), ~50% driven by shocks/sheaths, ~50% by MCs or non-MC ejecta. For extreme storms (Dst<−200 nT), the shock/sheath fraction **increases**. Sheaths dominate Kp≥7 storms (auroral response). Figure 33 right: 39% of shocks propagating inside preceding ICMEs produce intense geomagnetic storms (Lugaz et al. 2015). / 강한 자기폭풍 중 약 50%가 시스 구동, 극강 폭풍에서 시스 비율 증가.

**Case study — 14–16 December 2006 storm (Fig. 31):** Shock at 14:14 UT 14 Dec; sheath for ~10 h with turbulent Bz fluctuations (±15 nT), V = 850 km/s, Ey peaks at ±10 mV/m, AE > 1500 nT but Dst drops only to ~−20 nT. MC begins 00:30 UT 15 Dec with smooth rotation, Bz sustained at −15 nT for ~8 h → Dst bottoms at −146 nT. This event illustrates that **Dst storm onset and peak are MC-driven**, but **AE (auroral current) is sheath-driven**. Kp = 8 during sheath (mostly due to AE contribution). Recovery begins when Bz rotates northward in the MC second half. / 2006-12-14 사건: 시스에서 AE 피크, MC 에서 Dst 최저. 시스 ↔ AE, MC ↔ Dst 를 입증하는 전형적 사례.

**Auroral zone (Sect. 6.2, Fig. 34):** Sheaths cause large peak AE but shorter duration. MCs cause longest-duration AE>1000 nT. Rear regions are weakest drivers. / 시스: 큰 AE 피크, 짧은 지속. MC: 긴 지속.

**Radiation belts (Sect. 6.3, Fig. 35):** Sheaths deplete electron flux via pitch-angle scattering + magnetopause shadowing under high P_dyn and ULF power. MCs with sustained southward Bz enhance electron loss by magnetotail erosion but lower P_dyn means smaller depletion. ICME trailing fast streams produce later enhancements. / 시스는 전자 감소, MC 는 지속적 증가.

**Atmospheric effects (Sect. 6.4):** ICME-driven SEPs increase HOₓ and NOₓ, affect ozone. CHAMP observations: thermospheric density enhanced up to 8× during ICME impacts. / SEP 로 HOₓ, NOₓ 증가, 오존 감소. 열권 밀도 8배 증가.

**Planetary atmospheres (Sect. 6.5, Fig. 36):** MAVEN during March 8–9, 2015 ICME at Mars: outward heavy-ion flux enhanced by up to 8× over median (Jakosky et al. 2015). Unmagnetized planets (Venus, Mars) lose ionospheric O, H via V×B pick-up + sputtering; effects were dramatic in young Sun era. / 화성: ICME 중 이온 손실 8배. 젊은 태양 시대에 행성 대기 소실의 주 원인.

**Exoplanets (Sect. 6.6, Fig. 37):** M-dwarf habitable-zone planets are tidally-locked, weak-field, close to the star. Super-CMEs (10¹⁹ kg vs. solar 10¹³ kg) may strip their atmospheres. V374 Pegasus CME (Vida et al. 2016): 10¹³ kg, 675 km/s from Hα blue-wing. / 외계행성: 거대 CME 가 대기 박탈 가능.

**Radiation belt case studies:** The Halloween 2003 storms pushed relativistic (>2 MeV) electron penetration down to L<2.5 (inner belt region, normally empty). Similarly, the March 2015 St. Patrick's Day storm (driven by a fast ICME) saw deep electron penetration. The bottom panel of Fig. 35 shows the SAMPEX + Van Allen Probes ~2 MeV electron flux by L-shell over 1993–2017: long drop-outs correlate with strong sheath passages; persistent enhancements (weeks) occur after MC-driven storms with trailing fast streams. / 복사대 사례: 2003 할로윈, 2015 성 패트릭 데이 폭풍 — 전자 이너 벨트 침투.

### Part VI: Concluding Remarks (Sect. 7) / 결론

The authors emphasize three open questions: (i) the detailed connection between remote-sensing CME structures (three-part morphology: front, cavity, prominence core) and in-situ ICME sub-structures (shock, sheath, MC, boundary layers); (ii) the integrity and evolution of the flux-rope structure — are ICMEs coherent global flux-ropes or writhed / eroded structures?; (iii) the origin and composition of the sheath layers (propagation vs. expansion vs. boundary/PUC). Future missions (Parker Solar Probe, Solar Orbiter, BepiColombo) will sample ICMEs at heliocentric distances down to 10 R☉ and in 3D. Multi-spacecraft radial and longitudinal alignments are key. / 결론: (i) 원격 CME ↔ in-situ ICME 대응, (ii) 플럭스로프 결맞음, (iii) 시스 기원 — 세 가지 미해결 과제. PSP, Solar Orbiter, BepiColombo 로 3D ICME 관측 기대.

## 3. Key Takeaways / 핵심 시사점

1. **Sheath ≠ ICME — they are physically distinct** — Sheaths form by compression + heating of ambient solar wind ahead of a faster-than-local-magnetosonic driver; ICMEs (especially MCs) carry coronal/solar-origin plasma. Sheath β ~1–5 (plasma-dominated), MC β ≪ 1 (field-dominated); sheath B_RMS tail much higher than MC; sheath O⁷⁺/O⁶⁺ is low, MC is elevated. / 시스는 주변 태양풍의 압축, MC 는 태양 기원. 플라즈마 성질이 근본적으로 다름.

2. **Only 1/3 of 1 AU ICMEs are clear MCs** — geometric path through ICME (flank vs. center, Jian Group 1-2-3), erosion (up to 40% flux loss by reconnection), and interactions can hide the flux-rope signature. All ICMEs likely contain flux ropes at their core; detection is observational limitation, not physical absence. / 1 AU 에서 ICME 의 1/3 만 명확한 MC. 경로, 침식, 상호작용이 플럭스로프 신호를 가림.

3. **Intense geomagnetic storms require prolonged southward IMF** — Dst<−100 nT needs Bz<−10 nT for ≥3 h (Gonzalez 1994). Sources: (a) intrinsic MC Bz rotation, (b) sheath compressed/draped Bz, (c) shock-compressed preceding ICME Bz. The coupling is $E_y = V B_s$, and fast sheaths dominate the strongest storms. / 강한 자기폭풍 = 장시간 남향 IMF. 시스, MC, 선행 ICME 압축 세 가지 소스.

4. **Sheaths drive auroral activity disproportionately** — High ULF power (3–10 min IMF Bz), high dynamic pressure, and high Alfvén Mach number in sheaths enhance Region-1 currents and magnetosphere-ionosphere coupling. Sheaths produce higher peak AE; MCs produce longer-duration AE. / 시스는 ULF 파워와 동역학압으로 오로라를 효과적으로 구동. MC 는 지속 시간이 김.

5. **ICME radial evolution: expansion then saturation** — Richardson et al. (2006) fit W = 0.13 + 0.14R [AU] up to ~15 AU, then saturation at pressure balance. ICME B decays slower than ambient ($R^{-1.36}$ vs. $R^{-1.80}$) — ICMEs remain magnetically distinct to large distances. / ICME 은 15 AU 까지 팽창하다 포화. B 는 주변보다 천천히 감소.

6. **Lundquist model captures direction rotation but not magnitude profile** — Uniform twist (observed, Hu et al. 2015) contradicts Lundquist's monotonically increasing twist. Real MCs have flat central $|B|$ profiles, not Bessel $J_0$ peaks. Non-force-free and elliptical models capture these better but are harder to fit. / Lundquist 는 방향은 맞지만 크기 분포는 관측과 불일치. 균일 트위스트가 실측.

7. **CME-CME interactions produce extreme events** — The strongest magnetic fields (B up to 109 nT at 1 AU, July 2012) result from interacting ICMEs. A shock propagating into a preceding ICME compresses the already-strong MC field. 39% of shocks inside ICMEs produce intense storms. / CME-CME 상호작용이 극단 우주 날씨 사건의 주 원인. 2012년 7월 109 nT.

8. **ICMEs matter beyond Earth** — Mars/Venus lose ionospheric ions × ~8 during ICMEs (MAVEN, 2015); young-Sun super-CMEs likely shaped Mars atmospheric loss; M-dwarf exoplanets may be irradiated continuously by stellar CMEs, with implications for habitability. / ICME 의 영향은 지구를 넘어 행성, 외계행성까지 이른다.

### Supplementary observations / 보충 관측

- **B–V anti-correlation in sheath:** As sheaths compress ahead of the ICME, their B and V profiles rise together (unlike co-rotating interaction regions where B leads V). / 시스에서 B 와 V 가 동시 상승 (CIR 과 구분점).

- **Expansion speed scaling:** $v_{exp} \sim 0.5 v_A$ in the MC frame (Klein & Burlaga 1982). For $v_A \sim 80$ km/s in a 13 nT / 5 cm⁻³ MC, $v_{exp} \sim 40$ km/s, giving ΔV ≈ −80 km/s leading-to-trailing — matches Table 1 (ΔV = −58 to −95 km/s). / 팽창 속도 예측과 관측 일치.

- **Sheath Bz standard deviation:** Typical sheath σ(Bz) ~ 4–6 nT vs. MC σ(Bz) ~ 1–2 nT — a 3× difference used operationally to distinguish the two. / σ(Bz) 가 시스/MC 구분 지표.

## 4. Mathematical Summary / 수학적 요약

### 4.1 MC identification criteria / MC 식별 조건 (Burlaga 1981)

$$|\mathbf{B}| > 10\text{ nT}, \qquad \text{smooth } \hat{B} \text{ rotation } \sim 180°, \qquad T_p < 0.5\, T_{exp}(v_{sw})$$

Richardson & Cane (1995) expected temperature: empirical fit from OMNI. β = $(n k_B T)/(B^2/2\mu_0)$ is typically ≪ 1 inside MCs.

### 4.2 Lundquist constant-α force-free flux rope / Lundquist 해

Governing equation: $\nabla \times \mathbf{B} = \alpha \mathbf{B}$ with $\alpha$ = const. In cylindrical coordinates:

$$B_r = 0, \qquad B_\phi(r) = H\, B_0\, J_1(\alpha r), \qquad B_z(r) = B_0\, J_0(\alpha r)$$

Boundary: $J_0(\alpha r_0) = 0 \Rightarrow \alpha r_0 = 2.405$. Pitch $B_\phi/B_z = H\, J_1(\alpha r)/J_0(\alpha r)$ diverges at $r_0$. $|B|^2 = B_0^2 [J_0^2 + J_1^2]$, peaks at r=0 with $|B|=B_0$, drops to $|B|=B_0 J_1(2.405) \approx 0.52 B_0$ at boundary. / 경계에서 축 성분 0, 접선 성분 최대.

### 4.3 Grad–Shafranov equation / Grad–Shafranov 방정식 (2.5-D)

$$\frac{\partial^2 A}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 A}{\partial y^2} = -\mu_0 \frac{dP_t(A)}{dA}, \qquad P_t = p + \frac{B_z^2}{2\mu_0}$$

$\mathbf{A} = A(x,y)\hat{z}$; $\mathbf{B} = (\partial_y A, -\partial_x A, B_z(A))$. Both $P_t$ and $B_z$ are single-valued functions of $A$ on common field lines. Invariant axis found by minimizing the residual between inbound/outbound $P_t(A)$ branches. / GS 방정식: $P_t$ 와 $B_z$ 는 $A$ 만의 함수.

### 4.4 Plasma β and Alfvén Mach number / 플라즈마 베타와 알펜 마하수

$$\beta = \frac{n k_B T}{B^2 / (2\mu_0)} = \frac{2\mu_0 n k_B T}{B^2}$$

$$v_A = \frac{B}{\sqrt{\mu_0 \rho}}, \qquad M_A = \frac{|\mathbf{v} - \mathbf{v}_{sw}|}{v_A}, \qquad M_{ms} = \frac{|\mathbf{v} - \mathbf{v}_{sw}|}{v_{ms}}$$

with $v_{ms}^2 = v_A^2 + c_s^2$ (approx. for perpendicular fast mode). Fast-mode shock requires $M_{ms} > 1$ upstream. Typical interplanetary shocks: $M_A \sim 2$–5, extreme 2012-07-23 event: $M_A = 21$, $M_{ms} = 17$.

### 4.5 Rankine–Hugoniot fast-mode shock jump / R-H 점프 조건

At a fast-forward shock with upstream "1" and downstream "2":

$$[\rho v_n] = 0, \quad [\rho v_n^2 + p + B_t^2/(2\mu_0)] = 0, \quad [\rho v_n v_t - B_n B_t/\mu_0] = 0$$
$$[v_n B_t - v_t B_n] = 0, \quad [B_n]=0$$

For strong perpendicular shock ($M_A \gg 1$, γ = 5/3): compression ratio $r = \rho_2/\rho_1 \to 4$. B jump: $B_{t,2}/B_{t,1} = r$ for perpendicular shock. Observed sheath B enhancement 2–5 × ambient solar wind. / R-H 조건. 강한 수직 충격파에서 밀도·B 비율 → 4.

### 4.6 Burton-McPherron-Russell Dst equation / 자기폭풍 방정식

$$\frac{dDst^*}{dt} = Q(E_y) - \frac{Dst^*}{\tau}, \qquad Dst^* = Dst - b\sqrt{P_{dyn}} + c$$

$Dst^*$: pressure-corrected Dst; $b, c$: calibration constants; $\tau \approx 5$–10 h; $Q(E_y)$: injection function linear in rectified electric field:

$$Q(E_y) = \begin{cases} -d (E_y - E_c) & \text{if } E_y > E_c \\ 0 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

$E_y = V B_s$ where $B_s = \max(-B_z, 0)$. Coupling: intense storm (Dst < −100 nT) requires $B_s > 10$ nT for ≥3 h (Gonzalez 1994). / Burton 방정식: 감쇠 vs 주입 경쟁. E_y = V Bs 가 주 구동력.

### 4.7 Expansion rate (paper Eq. 1) / 팽창률

$$\psi = \frac{\Delta V_x}{\Delta t}\, \frac{D}{V_C^2}$$

Self-similar expansion of MC; $\psi$ nearly constant for unperturbed MCs. ΔV<0 for 85–88% of observed ICMEs.

### 4.8 Drag-based model (DBM) / 항력 기반 모델

$$\frac{dv}{dt} = -\gamma_{DBM} (v - v_{sw}) |v - v_{sw}|$$

Analytic solution (Vršnak et al. 2013) for constant $v_{sw}$ and $\gamma$:
$$v(t) = \frac{v_0 - v_{sw}}{1 \pm \gamma_{DBM}(v_0 - v_{sw}) t} + v_{sw}$$

$\gamma_{DBM} \sim 10^{-8}$–$10^{-7}$ km⁻¹. **Arrival time at 1 AU:** integrating the DBM gives ~18 h for v₀ = 2500 km/s, ~4 days for v₀ = 400 km/s. / 항력 기반 모델로 CME 도달 시간 계산.

### 4.9 Geoeffectiveness parameter (approximate scaling) / 지자기 효과

$$\text{Geo-effectiveness} \propto V \cdot B_s^{1.2} \cdot \sin^4(\theta_c/2)$$

where $\theta_c$ is clock angle of IMF. Kan–Lee coupling function (simplified). Gonzalez-Echer thresholds used operationally. / Kan-Lee 결합함수.

### 4.10 Worked example — Dst prediction from synthetic ICME / Dst 예측 수치 예시

Take an ICME sheath with V = 600 km/s, Bs = 15 nT sustained for 6 h, followed by MC with V = 550 km/s, Bs = 20 nT sustained for 8 h. Using Burton with $\tau = 7.7$ h, $Q = -d(E_y - E_c)$ with $d = 1.5 \times 10^{-3}$ nT/(mV/m·s), $E_c = 0.5$ mV/m:

- Sheath: $E_y = 600 \times 15 \times 10^{-3} = 9$ mV/m, injection $Q \approx -46$ nT/h. Over 6 h, Dst reaches ~−140 nT.
- MC: $E_y = 550 \times 20 \times 10^{-3} = 11$ mV/m, Q ≈ −57 nT/h. Additional ~−200 nT over 8 h, but with recovery partially offsetting → Dst minimum ≈ −220 nT, superstorm territory.

This matches the "intense storm" criterion Bz < −10 nT for 3 h (Gonzalez 1994). / Dst 수치 예측: Bs=15nT/6h + 20nT/8h 사건에서 Dst_min ≈ -220 nT (extreme 폭풍).

### 4.11 ICME arrival time via DBM / DBM 으로 도달 시간 계산

Starting speed $v_0$ at height $h_0 = 20 R_\odot$, ambient SW $v_{sw} = 400$ km/s, drag $\gamma = 2 \times 10^{-8}$ km⁻¹:

| $v_0$ [km/s] | Arrival at 1 AU [h] | Regime / 유형 |
|---|---|---|
| 400 | ~96 (~4 days) | no deceleration, merges with SW |
| 800 | ~55 | moderate shock |
| 1200 | ~38 | fast halo, thick sheath |
| 1800 | ~27 | extreme, $M_A \sim 7$ |
| 2500 | ~19 | Carrington-class |

Integration: $t = h_0^{-1} + \gamma (v_0 - v_{sw}) / (\gamma (v_0 - v_{sw}) v_{sw} + (v_0 - v_{sw}))$. In practice 0.3 AU margin is typical forecast uncertainty. / DBM 으로 도달시간: 400 km/s (4일) – 2500 km/s (19시간).

### 4.12 Flux-rope helicity sign identification / 플럭스로프 헬리시티 판별

For a cylindrical MC intersected at the axis (Group 1):
- If $B_\phi$ rotates counterclockwise (when viewed along axis): $H = +1$ (right-handed)
- If clockwise: $H = -1$ (left-handed)

Hemispheric rule (Bothmer & Schwenn 1998): solar sources in the northern heliographic hemisphere tend to produce left-handed MCs, southern sources right-handed. Exceptions common. Helicity sign is conserved from Sun to 1 AU; it links in-situ observations to the magnetic structure of the source active region or filament channel. / 헬리시티 부호: 반시계방향 회전 → +1 (오른손). 태양 반구 헬리시티 규칙 있으나 예외 다수.

### 4.13 Shock compression ratio (numerical table) / 충격파 압축비

Exact R-H solution for γ=5/3 perpendicular fast shock:

| $M_A$ | Compression ratio r | $B_2/B_1$ | $T_2/T_1$ |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1.5 | 1.67 | 1.67 | 1.35 |
| 2 | 2.29 | 2.29 | 1.96 |
| 3 | 3.00 | 3.00 | 3.30 |
| 5 | 3.57 | 3.57 | 6.95 |
| 10 | 3.88 | 3.88 | 26.0 |
| 21 (2012 event) | 3.97 | 3.97 | 114 |
| ∞ | 4.00 | 4.00 | ∞ |

For strong shock, r saturates at 4 but T_2/T_1 grows without bound — explaining why sheath T_p can exceed MC T_p by factor 10×. / 강한 충격파에서 r → 4 포화, T 는 $M_A^2$ 로 발산.
