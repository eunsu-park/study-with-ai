## Gender and Number Rules

### Gender

**Masculine Noun Markers:**
- Ends in -o: libro, gato, año
- Ends in -or: doctor, amor, color
- Days and months: lunes, enero
- Rivers, mountains, seas: río, monte, mar

**Feminine Noun Markers:**
- Ends in -a: casa, mesa, ventana
- Ends in -ción, -sión: nación, televisión
- Ends in -dad, -tad: ciudad, libertad
- Ends in -ez, -eza: vez, belleza

**Exceptions:**
- el día, el mapa, el problema (masculine)
- la mano, la foto, la moto (feminine)

### Number

**Plural Forms:**
- Vowel + s: casa → casas
- Consonant + es: papel → papeles
- -z → ces: lápiz → lápices

## Study Tips

1. **Memorize with Articles**: Always memorize el/la together with the noun
2. **Thematic Learning**: Group related nouns by topic
3. **Connect with Real Objects**: Label objects around you in Spanish
4. **Flashcards**: Use flashcards with pictures
5. **Learn in Sentences**: Use nouns in sentences rather than in isolation
6. **Practice Plurals**: Learn singular and plural forms together
7. **Gender Patterns**: Focus on -o/-a patterns and exceptions
