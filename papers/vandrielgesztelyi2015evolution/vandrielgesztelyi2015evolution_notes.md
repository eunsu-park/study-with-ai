# 40. Evolution of Active Regions / 활동영역의 진화

---

## 1. Core Contribution / 핵심 기여

**English**: This Living Review by van Driel-Gesztelyi and Green (2015) provides the most complete observational synthesis to date of the full life cycle of solar active regions (ARs): emergence, growth, maximum, decay, dispersal, and assimilation into the background network field. Building on the three pillars of dynamo-relevant empirical laws — Spörer's law (butterfly diagram), Joy's law (tilt–latitude), and Hale's polarity law — the authors systematically lay out the intrinsic characteristics (polarity separation, tilt evolution, asymmetric proper motion, inherent magnetic helicity, magnetic tongues, serpentine emergence, and nesting), and then trace the decay phase: moving magnetic features (MMFs), fragmentation via light bridges, supergranular dispersal, disconnection from toroidal roots, flux cancellation along the polarity inversion line (PIL), and the build-up of flux ropes and filaments that eventually erupt as CMEs. The final section showcases the long-term (six-rotation) analysis of AR 7978 — the "last best AR" of solar cycle 22 — which serves as a de facto Rosetta stone for AR long-term evolution, tying X-ray, EUV, microwave, flare, CME, and helicity-budget observations to the underlying magnetic evolution.

**한국어**: van Driel-Gesztelyi & Green (2015)의 이 Living Review는 현재까지 가장 완전한 관측 기반 종합으로, 태양 활동영역(AR)의 전 생애 — 부상(emergence), 성장(growth), 극대(maximum), 쇠퇴(decay), 분산(dispersal), 배경 네트워크로의 흡수 — 를 다룬다. dynamo 이론에 핵심적인 세 가지 경험 법칙 — Spörer 법칙(butterfly diagram), Joy 법칙(tilt-위도 관계), Hale 극성 법칙 — 을 기반으로, AR의 본질적 특성들(극성 분리, tilt 진화, 비대칭 고유운동, 고유 자기 헬리시티, 자기 혓바닥, serpentine 부상, nesting 경향)을 체계적으로 설명한다. 이어 쇠퇴 단계를 추적한다: MMF, light bridge에 의한 파편화, supergranular 분산, 토로이달 뿌리로부터의 분리, PIL에서의 자속 상쇄, 그리고 궁극적으로 CME로 폭발하는 flux rope와 필라멘트의 형성. 마지막 절은 태양 주기 22의 "마지막 최고 AR"인 AR 7978의 6 회전에 걸친 장기 분석을 집대성하는데, 이는 AR 장기 진화의 로제타 스톤 역할을 한다.

## 2. Reading Notes / 읽기 노트

### Part I: Introduction and AR Definition / 서론과 AR 정의 (§1, pp.5–13)

**English**: The paper begins by updating Kiepenheuer's (1968) definition from IAU Symposium 35: *"An AR is the totality of observable phenomena in a 3D volume represented by the extension of the magnetic field from photosphere to corona"*, with kG strength and unsigned flux Φ ≥ 10²⁰ Mx. In the photosphere we see sunspots, pores, and plage/faculae; in the chromosphere arch filament systems (AFS) and plage; and in the transition region and corona hot dense loops. ARs are the principal source of all solar activity — from small-scale brightenings and jets to the largest flares and CMEs. The level and type of activity depends on the evolutionary stage.

The key observational pillars are:
- **Spörer's law**: ARs emerge at 25°–35° at cycle start, migrating equatorward to produce the butterfly diagram.
- **Joy's law**: Leading polarity is closer to the equator; tilt increases with latitude.
- **Hale's law**: Leading polarity is opposite in N/S hemispheres and reverses every cycle (22-yr magnetic cycle).

Three additional intrinsic properties: (i) leading–following asymmetry (proper motion, magnetic tongues, helicity injection), (ii) flux emerges twisted carrying helicity and free energy, (iii) flux emergence is nested/clustered (Cassini 1729).

The authors then give a step-by-step narrative of AR evolution: (1) weak horizontal fields (200–600 G), granulation looks "anomalous", (2) AFS forms in Hα with rising tops at 10–20 km/s and downflows along legs at 20–25 km/s, (3) convective collapse concentrates fields from 200 G to 2000–2500 G, (4) after 24 h polarities separate, pores merge into sunspots at flux threshold Φ_crit ≤ 5×10²⁰ Mx for penumbra formation, (5) tilt relaxes to Joy's law within 1–3 days, (6) flux cancellation can remove up to 10%/day of total flux, (7) MMFs stream out of decaying spots, (8) spots fragment via light bridges (30–60 days), (9) filaments form along PIL, (10) the AR becomes an "enhanced network" that eventually fades into the background.

**한국어**: 논문은 Kiepenheuer(1968)가 IAU Symposium 35에서 제시한 정의를 현대화한다: *"AR은 광구에서 코로나까지 자기장 확장에 의해 표현되는 3D 체적 안의 관측 가능한 모든 현상의 총체이며, kG 강도 및 unsigned flux Φ ≥ 10²⁰ Mx"*. 광구에서는 흑점·기공(pore)·플라주/파큘라를, 채층에서는 arch filament system (AFS)과 플라주를, 전이층·코로나에서는 뜨겁고 조밀한 루프를 본다. AR은 모든 태양 활동의 근원이며 — 작은 밝기 증가·제트에서 가장 큰 flare·CME까지 — 활동의 양상은 진화 단계에 따라 다르다.

관측의 세 가지 기둥:
- **Spörer 법칙**: 주기 시작 시 25°–35°에서 부상, 적도로 이동하며 butterfly diagram을 만든다.
- **Joy 법칙**: leading 극성이 적도에 더 가깝고 tilt는 위도에 따라 증가.
- **Hale 법칙**: 남·북 반구의 leading 극성이 반대이고 매 주기마다 반전(22년 자기 주기).

추가로 세 가지 본질적 성질: (i) leading–following 비대칭(고유운동·자기 혓바닥·헬리시티 주입), (ii) 자속은 twist된 상태로 부상하며 헬리시티와 자유 자기에너지를 수반, (iii) 자속 부상은 nesting/군집을 보인다(Cassini 1729).

저자들은 AR 진화의 단계별 서술을 제공한다: (1) 약한 수평장(200–600 G), "anomalous" granulation, (2) Hα AFS 형성, 꼭대기 상승속도 10–20 km/s, 다리 하강속도 20–25 km/s, (3) convective collapse로 200 G → 2000–2500 G 집중, (4) 24시간 후 극성 분리, pore가 병합하여 흑점 형성 (Φ_crit ≤ 5×10²⁰ Mx에서 penumbra 발달), (5) 1–3일 내 tilt가 Joy 법칙에 수렴, (6) 자속 상쇄가 전체 자속의 최대 10%/day 제거, (7) 쇠퇴 흑점에서 MMF 방출, (8) light bridge로 파편화(30–60일), (9) PIL에 필라멘트 형성, (10) AR이 "강화 네트워크"가 되어 배경에 흡수.

### Part II: Lifetime and Flux Distribution / 수명과 자속 분포 (§2, pp.14–18)

**English**: Table 1 encapsulates the scaling:
| Region | Flux (Mx) | Lifetime | Rise/Lifetime |
|---|---|---|---|
| Large (with sunspots) | 5×10²¹ – 3×10²² | weeks – months | 15–3% |
| Small (pores, no spots) | 10²⁰ – 5×10²¹ | days – weeks | 15–27% |
| Ephemeral (ERs) | 3×10¹⁸ – 10²⁰ | hours – 1 day | ~30% |

Lifetime is roughly proportional to flux content; it also depends on surroundings (an AR at solar minimum may last 10 months; during maximum, < 4 months). The ratio rise/lifetime decreases with flux: large ARs emerge in a small fraction of their lifetime, while ERs spend ~30% of life emerging.

The flux distribution is a key dynamo diagnostic. Parnell et al. (2009) and Thornton & Parnell (2011), combining SOHO/MDI and Hinode/SOT, found a single power law n(Φ) ∝ Φ^(-1.85 ± 0.14) spanning 10^16 to 10^23 Mx — 18 orders of magnitude in frequency. This **scale-free** distribution suggests either (i) one unified mechanism (a combined tachocline + turbulent dynamo) or (ii) fragmentation/coalescence/cancellation dominating the surface distribution.

Harvey (1993) showed AR size distribution is essentially cycle-invariant, while AR *frequency* changes by a factor of 8 over the cycle (ER frequency only by factor 2). ERs emerge 2–3 years before sunspots at high latitudes 30°–35°, often with reversed orientations (preview of next cycle).

**한국어**: Table 1은 스케일링을 압축해 보여준다:
| 영역 | 자속 (Mx) | 수명 | 상승/수명 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 대형 (흑점 포함) | 5×10²¹ – 3×10²² | 주–개월 | 15–3% |
| 소형 (pore, 흑점 없음) | 10²⁰ – 5×10²¹ | 일–주 | 15–27% |
| 덧없는 (ER) | 3×10¹⁸ – 10²⁰ | 시간–1일 | ~30% |

수명은 대략 자속량에 비례하며, 주변 환경에도 영향을 받는다(태양 극소기에 10개월 지속 가능, 극대기엔 < 4개월). 상승/수명 비율은 자속이 클수록 감소한다.

자속 분포는 dynamo 진단의 핵심이다. Parnell et al.(2009)과 Thornton & Parnell(2011)이 SOHO/MDI + Hinode/SOT를 결합하여 얻은 단일 멱함수 n(Φ) ∝ Φ^(-1.85 ± 0.14)는 10^16–10^23 Mx 범위 (주파수로 18 자릿수) 전체에 걸쳐 있다. 이 **스케일 자유** 분포는 (i) 통합된 단일 메커니즘(tachocline + turbulent dynamo 결합) 또는 (ii) 파편화·병합·상쇄가 표면 분포를 지배함을 시사한다.

Harvey(1993)는 AR 크기 분포가 주기 불변임을 보였는데, 반면 AR *빈도*는 주기에 따라 8배 변화한다(ER는 2배만). ER은 흑점보다 2–3년 앞서 고위도 30°–35°에서 나타나며, 종종 반대 방향 tilt를 보인다(다음 주기 예고).

### Part III: Polarity Separation and Joy's Law / 극성 분리와 Joy 법칙 (§3.1–3.2, pp.19–27)

**English**: Wang & Sheeley (1989) analysed 2700 BMRs with Φ ≥ 3×10²⁰ Mx and found Φ(s) = 4×10²⁰ Mx·s^1.3, where s is polarity separation in heliographic degrees — **larger ARs separate farther**. Tian et al. (2003) confirmed Φ₂₀ ~ d^1.15 with Huairou data. Kosovichev & Stenflo (2008) found polarity separation peaks ~12 h after peak flux, then mildly decreases — suggesting the retraction of Ω-loop legs after emergence ceases.

**Joy's law** (Hale et al. 1919) is based on A.H. Joy's analysis of 2633 sunspot groups across 3.5 cycles from Carrington and Spörer drawings. The result: the following (f) spot sits farther from the equator than the preceding (p) spot. Wang & Sheeley (1991) derived from SOHO/MDI:
$$\sin\gamma = 0.48\sin\theta + 0.03$$
(Note: the commonly cited version "sin γ = 0.5 cos θ + 0.03" from Leighton 1969 is incorrect.) The modern, large-statistics version (Stenflo & Kosovichev 2012, 160,079 bipoles from SOHO/MDI) is γ = 32.1° sin b, which yields the easy rule-of-thumb: **sin(tilt) ≈ half of sin(latitude)**, so at b = 10° → γ ≈ 5°, at b = 20° → γ ≈ 10°.

Sources of tilt-angle scatter:
- **Age**: Orientations are random at birth; Joy's law is achieved only after 1–3 days.
- **Size**: Scatter decreases with flux content; small bipoles (< 300 MSH in Tlatov et al. 2010) may even *disobey* Joy's law.
- **Polarity separation**: Δα ~ 10°·(d/100 Mm)^(-3/4) (Fisher et al. 1995).
- **Magnetic twist / tongues**: Asymmetric tongues can mask the true tilt during emergence.
- **Deep vortices**: Sub-surface flows deform rising tubes (González Hernández et al. 2013 confirmed via ring-diagram helioseismology).

**Physical origin**: Babcock (1961) proposed spiral toroidal geometry; Leighton (1969) proposed kink deformation; Wang & Sheeley (1991) and D'Silva & Choudhuri (1993) advanced the Coriolis force on expanding Ω-loops. Kosovichev & Stenflo (2008) argued that since tilt does not relax to zero after the Coriolis force ceases, it must reflect the deep toroidal spiral. McClintock & Norton (2013) propose a combination of Coriolis and initial toroidal tilt.

**한국어**: Wang & Sheeley(1989)는 Φ ≥ 3×10²⁰ Mx인 2700 BMR을 분석하여 Φ(s) = 4×10²⁰ Mx·s^1.3 (s는 극성 분리 일면 각도)을 얻었다 — **큰 AR일수록 더 멀리 분리**. Tian et al.(2003)은 Huairou 데이터로 Φ₂₀ ~ d^1.15를 확인. Kosovichev & Stenflo(2008)는 극성 분리의 최대치가 최대 자속보다 약 12시간 늦게 오며, 그 이후 약간 감소함을 발견 — 부상이 끝난 뒤 Ω-loop 다리가 후퇴함을 시사.

**Joy 법칙**(Hale et al. 1919)은 A.H. Joy가 Carrington, Spörer의 그림으로부터 2633개 흑점군을 3.5 주기에 걸쳐 분석한 결과이다. 결과: following(f) 흑점이 preceding(p) 흑점보다 적도에서 더 멀다. Wang & Sheeley(1991)이 SOHO/MDI로 유도한 관계식:
$$\sin\gamma = 0.48\sin\theta + 0.03$$
(주의: Leighton 1969의 "sin γ = 0.5 cos θ + 0.03"는 틀린 공식). 최신 대통계 버전(Stenflo & Kosovichev 2012, 160,079 bipoles)은 γ = 32.1° sin b로, 쉬운 규칙: **sin(tilt) ≈ 절반 × sin(latitude)**, 따라서 b = 10° → γ ≈ 5°, b = 20° → γ ≈ 10°.

Tilt 산포의 원천:
- **나이**: 출생 시 방향은 임의이며 1–3일 후 Joy 법칙에 도달.
- **크기**: 산포는 자속에 따라 감소; 작은 bipole(Tlatov et al. 2010의 < 300 MSH)은 오히려 Joy 법칙을 *거스를* 수 있다.
- **극성 분리**: Δα ~ 10°·(d/100 Mm)^(-3/4) (Fisher et al. 1995).
- **자기 twist/tongue**: 부상 중 비대칭 tongue이 실제 tilt를 가릴 수 있다.
- **심층 vortex**: 표면 아래 흐름이 상승관을 변형 (González Hernández et al. 2013이 ring-diagram 일식론으로 확인).

**물리적 기원**: Babcock(1961) 나선형 toroidal 기하, Leighton(1969) kink 변형, Wang & Sheeley(1991)와 D'Silva & Choudhuri(1993)의 팽창 Ω-loop에 작용하는 Coriolis 힘. Kosovichev & Stenflo(2008)는 Coriolis가 멈춰도 tilt가 0으로 가지 않으므로 심층 toroidal 나선이 원인이라 주장.

### Part IV: Asymmetric Proper Motion and Magnetic Helicity / 비대칭 고유운동과 자기 헬리시티 (§3.3–3.4, pp.28–31)

**English**: Maunder (1919) described the pattern first: the leader moves forward for several days with diminishing speed, then halts; the follower moves eastward. Typical group length: 10°–12°. The leader becomes circular, compact, and long-lived; the follower fragments faster. The asymmetry biases rotation-rate measurements for young bipoles (Balthasar & Wöhl 1980), with recurrent long-lived groups showing higher initial rotational velocities by about 0.15°/day (Ružđjak et al. 2004).

**Magnetic helicity** — H = ∫A·B dV — measures shear, twist, and linkage. Leka et al. (1996) provided the first observational evidence that emerging flux is inherently twisted:
- Emerging bipoles had significant vertical currents at very early stages;
- Currents increased *together* with flux;
- Hα, X-ray morphology, and proper motions all consistent with the same sense of twist;
- Current increase was not consistent with generation by photospheric motions alone.

Wang & Abramenko (2000) found that in AR 7321 the α parameter (force-free field measure) remained nearly constant during emergence, implying the twist is a property of the sub-surface flux tube, not generated in situ.

**Magnetic tongues** (López Fuentes et al. 2000; Luoni et al. 2011) are tongue-like extensions of the LOS magnetograms caused by the azimuthal field component of the rising flux rope. Their orientation gives the sign of helicity: positive twist → "S"-like tongues, negative → "Z"-like. Tongues retract once the flux rope has fully crossed the photosphere, so they mask tilt only during emergence.

**한국어**: Maunder(1919)가 처음 패턴을 기술했다: leader는 며칠간 앞으로 이동하며 속도가 감소하다 멈춘다; follower는 동쪽으로 이동. 전형적 군집 길이: 10°–12°. Leader는 원형·밀집·장수명; follower는 더 빨리 파편화. 이 비대칭은 어린 bipole의 자전 속도 측정을 편향시킨다(Balthasar & Wöhl 1980).

**자기 헬리시티** — H = ∫A·B dV — 는 전단·꼬임·연결을 측정한다. Leka et al.(1996)은 부상 자속이 본질적으로 twist되어 있다는 최초의 관측적 증거를 제공했다:
- 부상 bipole이 초기 단계부터 유의한 수직 전류;
- 전류가 자속과 *동시에* 증가;
- Hα, X-ray 형태, 고유운동이 모두 같은 twist 방향과 일치;
- 전류 증가는 광구 운동으로 설명되지 않음.

Wang & Abramenko(2000)는 AR 7321에서 α 파라미터가 부상 동안 거의 일정함을 발견 — twist가 광구 아래 자속관의 고유 성질임을 의미.

**자기 혓바닥**(López Fuentes et al. 2000; Luoni et al. 2011)은 상승하는 flux rope의 방위각 자기장 성분이 LOS 자기그램에 만드는 혓바닥 모양 연장이다. 방향이 헬리시티 부호를 알려준다: 양의 twist → "S"-모양, 음의 → "Z"-모양. Tongue은 flux rope가 광구를 완전히 건넌 후 수축한다.

### Part V: Decay Phase / 쇠퇴 단계 (§4, pp.34–46)

**English**: Emergence lasts ≤ 5 days, but decay can last days to months. Once all flux has emerged, and often even before, ARs start decaying.

**Moving Magnetic Features (MMFs)**: Small (10³ km diameter) low-flux fragments (10¹⁸–10¹⁹ Mx) stream outward from decaying spots at ~1 km/s. Two types: unipolar (same polarity as spot, effective at removing flux) and bipolar (mixed polarity; may be U-loops or sea-serpent structures). Only unipolar MMFs clearly remove flux from the sunspot (Zhang et al. 2003).

**Fragmentation via light bridges**: Governed by the MHD induction equation ∂B/∂t = ∇×(v×B) + η∇²B. The magnetic diffusion timescale at 3000 km with η = 300 m²/s is ~1000 years, so decay is advective. For advection to work, plasma kinetic energy density (ρv²/2) must reach the magnetic energy density (B²/8π); at photospheric ρ = 4.9×10⁻⁶ kg/m³ and v = 1 km/s, this means field strengths must drop below ~615 G before convective break-up can proceed. Light bridges precede break-up; spot disappearance timing is 30–60 days (Bray & Loughhead 1964).

**Asymmetry in decay**: Karachik et al. (2010) found that the less compact following polarity dissipates faster than the compact leading polarity, creating a gradual flux imbalance and sometimes a coronal hole in place of the decayed following polarity. Table 3 shows the effective compactness r²_eff more than doubles during decay while flux shrinks to 40–17% of starting value.

**Dispersal and disconnection**: Once B weakens below equipartition, supergranular flows advect flux to cell edges. Schrijver & Title (1999) estimated the disconnection time from toroidal roots:
$$t_{\text{AR}}\,[\text{days}] = 15\,\Phi/10^{21}\,\text{Mx}$$
giving ~5 months for a major AR. Mechanism is either "explosion" below photosphere (Fan et al. 1994; Moreno-Insertis et al. 1995) or driven reconnection by converging convective flows (Schrijver & Title 1999).

**Flux cancellation along the PIL**: The van Ballegooijen & Martens (1989) mechanism: converging opposite-polarity fragments reconnect at low altitudes, producing a short loop that submerges and a long sheared loop that remains. This:
- Removes flux from photosphere (up to 10%/day for some ARs);
- Accumulates sheared axial field along PIL;
- Builds a flux rope through repeated cycles.
- Baker et al. (2012), Sterling et al. (2010): 20% of flux lost in just 2 days; Green et al. (2011): 34% of AR flux cancelled along internal PIL within 3.5 days post-emergence.

**Flux rope formation** (Figure 14, §4.8): three phases: (1) increasing shear via dispersal and cancellation; (2) accumulation of axial flux → double-J morphology; (3) formation of helical field wrapping axial flux → S-shaped sigmoid. Observational signatures: sigmoidal SXR/EUV, inverse crossing of PIL, bald patches, filament eruptions. Savcheva et al. (2012) found axial-to-total flux ratios of 18%, 16%, 58%, 50% for four flux ropes just before eruption.

**한국어**: 부상은 ≤ 5일이지만 쇠퇴는 며칠–몇 달이 걸릴 수 있다. 자속 완전 부상 이전에도 AR은 쇠퇴를 시작할 수 있다.

**MMF**: 쇠퇴 흑점에서 방사상으로 흘러나오는 소형(10³ km) 자속 파편(10¹⁸–10¹⁹ Mx), 속도 ~1 km/s. 두 종류: 단극(spot과 같은 극성, 자속 제거에 효과적)과 양극(혼합 극성). Zhang et al.(2003): 단극 MMF만 명확히 spot에서 자속을 제거.

**Light bridge 파편화**: MHD 유도 방정식 ∂B/∂t = ∇×(v×B) + η∇²B가 지배. 3000 km와 η = 300 m²/s에서 확산 시간은 ~1000년이므로 쇠퇴는 이류(advection)다. 이류가 작동하려면 운동 에너지 밀도(ρv²/2)가 자기 에너지 밀도(B²/8π)에 도달해야 한다; ρ = 4.9×10⁻⁶ kg/m³과 v = 1 km/s에서 B < ~615 G가 필요. Spot 소멸 30–60일(Bray & Loughhead 1964).

**쇠퇴의 비대칭**: Karachik et al.(2010): 덜 밀집된 following 극성이 leading 극성보다 빨리 소산되어 점진적 자속 불균형과 때로 코로나 구멍을 형성. Table 3: r²_eff가 쇠퇴 중 2배 이상 증가하는 반면 자속은 초기값의 40–17%로 감소.

**분산과 분리**: B가 등분배(equipartition) 이하로 떨어지면 supergranular 흐름이 자속을 세포 경계로 운반. Schrijver & Title(1999)의 토로이달 뿌리로부터의 분리 시간:
$$t_{\text{AR}}\,[\text{days}] = 15\,\Phi/10^{21}\,\text{Mx}$$
주요 AR에서 ~5개월. 메커니즘은 광구 아래 "폭발" 또는 수렴 대류에 의한 재결합.

**PIL에서의 자속 상쇄**: van Ballegooijen & Martens(1989) 메커니즘 — 수렴하는 반대 극성 파편이 저고도에서 재결합하여 짧은 루프는 잠기고 긴 전단 루프는 남는다. 결과: (1) 광구에서 자속 제거(최대 10%/day), (2) PIL을 따라 전단 축자기장 축적, (3) 반복적 사이클로 flux rope 형성. Green et al.(2011): 부상 후 3.5일 내 내부 PIL에서 AR 자속의 34%가 상쇄.

**Flux rope 형성**(Figure 14, §4.8): 3단계 — (1) 분산·상쇄로 전단 증가, (2) 축자속 축적 → double-J 형태, (3) 축자속을 감싸는 나선형 자기장 → S-형 sigmoid. Savcheva et al.(2012): 폭발 직전 flux rope의 축자속 비율 18%, 16%, 58%, 50%.

### Part V-bis: Serpentine Emergence and Nesting / Serpentine 부상과 Nesting (§3.5–3.6, pp.32–33)

**English**: At the beginning of flux emergence there is no single magnetic inversion line; instead small (~2 Mm) bipoles appear all over the nascent AR between growing magnetic concentrations (Wang & Zirin 1992; Bernasconi et al. 2002). This pattern is suggestive of **undulatory/serpentine flux** — an alternating series of small Ω- and U-loops breaking through the photosphere (Strous et al. 1996; Pariat et al. 2004; Watanabe et al. 2008). These small-scale emergence sites can be recurrent with a wavelength λ = 8 Mm (Strous & Zwaan 1999), and merge via magnetic cancellation/reconnection into large-scale AFS loops (Vargas Domínguez et al. 2012).

**Nesting** (Cassini 1729) is the tendency for new flux to emerge within, or immediately adjacent to, existing AR boundaries. Bumba & Howard (1965), Liggett & Zirin (1985), Harvey & Zwaan (1993) quantified this: **10 to 22-fold higher emergence rate inside existing ARs than in quiet Sun**. Consequences: (i) activity complex as a whole is long-lived due to repeated flux injection; (ii) individual bipole lifetimes are *shortened* due to intense flux cancellation with neighbouring flux; (iii) magnetic complexity and activity level increase compound-wise.

**한국어**: 자속 부상 초기에는 단일 자기 반전선이 존재하지 않고 대신 작은(~2 Mm) bipole들이 성장하는 자기 집중 사이에서 산발적으로 출현한다(Wang & Zirin 1992). 이 패턴은 **undulatory/serpentine 자속** — 작은 Ω-루프와 U-루프가 교대로 광구를 뚫고 나오는 — 을 시사한다(Strous et al. 1996). 이들 소규모 부상 지점은 파장 λ = 8 Mm로 재발할 수 있다(Strous & Zwaan 1999).

**Nesting**(Cassini 1729)은 기존 AR 경계 내부 또는 직접 인접한 곳에서 새 자속이 부상하는 경향이다. Bumba & Howard(1965), Liggett & Zirin(1985), Harvey & Zwaan(1993)이 정량화: **기존 AR 내부 부상률이 정온 태양 대비 10–22배 높다**. 결과: (i) 활동 복합체 전체는 반복적 자속 주입으로 장수명, (ii) 개별 bipole 수명은 강력한 국소 상쇄로 *단축*, (iii) 자기 복잡도와 활동 수준이 복합적으로 증가.

### Part VI: Modelling, Long-term Evolution, and Helicity Budget / 모델링·장기 진화·헬리시티 예산 (§5–§6, pp.47–77)

**English**: van Ballegooijen & Mackay (2007) constructed the seminal coupled emergence-through-decay MHD model: bipolar AR with initial field 1 kG, twist parameter q = 2πH/L = -0.5, pitch length L ≈ 0.5 R_sun, flux 1.231×10²² Mx per polarity, emerging at +20.2°. The model follows spreading, cancellation, and sub-surface submergence of short reconnected field lines. Shear accumulates along the PIL at low altitudes because submerging flux is perpendicular to the PIL (nearly potential), while long reconnected field lines inherit most of the shear. By day 20 the accumulating shear causes a CME-like lift-off (Figure 16). Mackay et al. (2011) measured a free-energy injection rate of (2.5–3)×10²⁵ erg/s — sufficient to balance radiative losses but too slow to power major eruptions alone.

**AR 7978 case study**: This AR emerged 18 Apr 1996 at Carrington longitude ~244°, was the 4th of 5 bipoles in an activity nest, and persisted 6 rotations. Peak flux ~1.5×10²² Mx. Unique because it was the only AR on-disc during cycle 22/23 minimum — allowing sun-as-a-star analyses of X-ray, EUV, microwave variability.

**Flare & CME evolution** (Table 6): rotation 1 (emergence): 1 X-class, 2 M-class, 14 C-class, 11 B-class + 8 CMEs; rotation 2: 16 B + 5 CMEs; rotations 4–6: no detectable flares but still 3–5 CMEs per rotation. **Flares peak during emergence; CMEs persist into decay phase** — because decay-phase CMEs occur via repeated filament eruptions driven by flux-rope rebuilding.

**Helicity budget** (§6.4): Uses Berger's (1984, 1988) formulation for photospheric helicity generation:
$$\frac{dH_r}{dt} = -2\oint (\mathbf{A}_p\cdot\mathbf{v})B_n \,dS$$
Photospheric motions inject two types: *twist* helicity (polarity rotation) and *writhe* helicity (relative motion of opposite polarities). These tend to cancel partially. Differential rotation injects only ~8.3 × 10⁴² Mx² total for AR 7978 over 6 rotations — a *minor contributor*. Most helicity (~55–241 × 10⁴² Mx² total) must come from emerging twisted flux. The AR produced 31 CMEs carrying away ~2×10⁴² Mx²/cloud × 31 = ~60 × 10⁴² Mx². Budget equation:
$$\Delta H_{\text{emergence}} = \Delta H_{\text{corona}} - \Delta H_{\text{diff rot}} + N\cdot H_{\text{CME}}$$
Conclusion: **sub-photospheric twisted flux emergence is the dominant helicity source**; differential rotation is always ineffective. AR 8100 (contrasting case): underwent strong clockwise rotation > 150°; differential rotation *depletes* coronal helicity after re-orientation N-S.

**Coronal scaling laws** (§6.5): For AR 7978, X-ray parameters follow power laws with mean B̄:
- Temperature T ∝ B̄^b, b ∈ [0.27, 0.55]
- Emission measure EM ∝ B̄^b, b ∈ [0.45, 1.96]
- Heating rate H_T ∝ B̄^b, b ~ 2 (quadratic!)

This supports coronal heating by stressed-field/current dissipation and MHD turbulence rather than wave heating.

**Multi-wavelength F–B relations** (§6.6): Démoulin (2004) found ΔF ∝ B̄^b with b = 1.1–1.2 across 19 bands (UV to SXR after instrument correction). Comparison: L_x ∝ Φ^1.15–1.19 across T Tauri stars and red dwarfs — a *universal* flux–magnetic relation spanning 12 magnitudes in stellar magnetic flux.

**한국어**: van Ballegooijen & Mackay(2007)는 부상-쇠퇴 통합 MHD 모델의 기념비적 작업이다: 초기 자기장 1 kG, twist 파라미터 q = 2πH/L = -0.5, 피치 길이 L ≈ 0.5 R_sun, 자속 1.231×10²² Mx, 위도 +20.2°. 분산·상쇄·짧은 재결합 루프의 잠김을 추적한다. PIL을 따라 전단이 축적되어 20일째에 CME와 같은 폭발이 일어난다(Figure 16). Mackay et al.(2011)은 자유 에너지 주입율 (2.5–3)×10²⁵ erg/s를 측정 — 복사 손실 상쇄에 충분하지만 주요 폭발에는 느리다.

**AR 7978 사례**: 1996년 4월 18일 Carrington 경도 ~244°에서 부상, 활동 nest의 5개 bipole 중 4번째, 6 회전 지속. 최대 자속 ~1.5×10²² Mx. 주기 22/23 극소기에 유일한 on-disc AR이라 sun-as-a-star 분석이 가능.

**Flare와 CME 진화**(Table 6): 회전 1(부상): X 1, M 2, C 14, B 11 + CME 8; 회전 2: B 16 + CME 5; 회전 4–6: flare 없음, CME 3–5/회전. **Flare는 부상 시 최대, CME는 쇠퇴까지 지속** — 쇠퇴기 CME는 flux rope 재구축에 의한 반복적 필라멘트 폭발.

**헬리시티 예산**: Berger(1984, 1988)의 공식 사용. 광구 운동은 *twist*(극성 회전)와 *writhe*(반대 극성 상대 운동) 두 헬리시티를 주입하며, 부분적으로 상쇄된다. AR 7978의 6 회전 동안 차등 자전은 총 ~8.3 × 10⁴² Mx²만 주입 — *미미한 기여자*. 대부분의 헬리시티(~55–241 × 10⁴² Mx²)는 twist된 자속의 부상에서 나온다. AR은 31 CME를 생산하여 ~60 × 10⁴² Mx² 운반. 결론: **광구 아래 twist 자속 부상이 지배적 헬리시티 원천**. AR 8100 대조 사례: > 150° 시계방향 회전; 재배향 후 차등 자전이 오히려 코로나 헬리시티를 *소진*.

**코로나 스케일링**(§6.5): T ∝ B̄^b (b 0.27–0.55), EM ∝ B̄^b (b 0.45–1.96), 가열률 H_T ∝ B̄^2 — stressed-field 소산과 MHD turbulence를 지지.

**다파장 F–B 관계**(§6.6): Démoulin(2004)의 ΔF ∝ B̄^1.1–1.2는 19개 파장 대역에 걸친다. T Tauri·적색왜성까지 확장되는 **보편적 flux-자기장 관계**(L_x ∝ Φ^1.15–1.19).

### Part VII: Appendix — Sunspot Classification Schemes / 부록: 흑점 분류 체계 (Appendix A, pp.79–80)

**English**: Two classification systems are used throughout the literature:

**Modified Zürich/McIntosh classification** (Waldmeier 1955; McIntosh 1990) uses three letters "Zpc":
- **Z (group class)**: A (single unipolar), B (bipolar without penumbra), C (one penumbra), D (longitudinal extent ≤ 10°), E (10–15°), F (> 15°), H (unipolar with penumbra);
- **p (principal-spot penumbra)**: x (none), r (rudimentary), s/a (small symmetric/asymmetric, ≤ 2.5°), h/k (large symmetric/asymmetric, ≥ 2.5°);
- **c (interior distribution)**: x (N/A), o (open), i (intermediate), c (compact).

**Mt. Wilson magnetic classification** (Hale et al. 1919; Künzel 1960):
- **α**: unipolar,
- **β**: bipolar with simple polarity division,
- **β-γ**: bipolar but no continuous line separates polarities,
- **γ**: complex, polarities irregularly distributed,
- **δ**: opposite-polarity umbrae within same penumbra — highest flare productivity class.

**한국어**: 문헌에서 사용되는 두 분류 체계:

**수정된 Zürich/McIntosh 분류**(Waldmeier 1955; McIntosh 1990)는 3문자 "Zpc"를 사용:
- **Z(군 등급)**: A(단극), B(무penumbra 양극), C(한 penumbra), D(경도 ≤ 10°), E(10–15°), F(> 15°), H(penumbra 있는 단극);
- **p(주요 흑점 penumbra)**: x(없음), r(초보적), s/a(작은 대칭/비대칭 ≤ 2.5°), h/k(큰 대칭/비대칭 ≥ 2.5°);
- **c(내부 분포)**: x(N/A), o(열림), i(중간), c(밀집).

**Mt. Wilson 자기 분류**(Hale et al. 1919; Künzel 1960):
- **α**: 단극,
- **β**: 단순 양극,
- **β-γ**: 양극이나 연속선이 극성을 나누지 못함,
- **γ**: 복잡, 극성이 불규칙 분포,
- **δ**: 같은 penumbra 내 반대 극성 umbra — 최대 flare 생산성.

## 3. Key Takeaways / 핵심 시사점

1. **Joy's law is a statistical, not universal, relation** — The rule-of-thumb γ ≈ 0.5·latitude (or γ = 32.1° sin b) works for large ARs but breaks for small bipoles (< 300 MSH) which may even invert the correlation (Tlatov et al. 2010). The underlying mechanism combines Coriolis torque on expanding Ω-loops with the initial tilt inherited from sub-photospheric toroidal geometry (Babcock 1961). / **Joy 법칙은 통계적 관계**이며 보편 법칙이 아니다 — γ ≈ 0.5·위도는 큰 AR에서는 성립하나 작은 bipole에서는 반전될 수 있다. 메커니즘은 팽창 Ω-loop에 대한 Coriolis + 표면 아래 toroidal 기하에서 상속된 초기 tilt의 조합이다.

2. **Flux distribution is scale-free over 7 orders of magnitude** — Parnell et al. (2009)'s single power law n(Φ) ∝ Φ^(-1.85) from 10¹⁶ to 10²³ Mx implies either one universal mechanism or that surface processes (fragmentation/coalescence) dominate the observed distribution. This is a major constraint on dynamo theory. / **자속 분포는 10⁷ 자릿수에 걸쳐 스케일 자유** — Parnell et al.(2009)의 n(Φ) ∝ Φ^(-1.85)는 단일 메커니즘이거나 표면 과정이 지배함을 의미. dynamo 이론의 주요 제약.

3. **Emergence is short, decay is long and multi-phase** — Typical emergence ≤ 5 days, but decay spans days-to-months because it is dominated by slow advective processes (ρv²/2 = B²/8π requires B < 615 G for convection to fragment the field). Flux cancellation, MMFs, supergranular dispersal, and toroidal disconnection (~5 months) all contribute. / **부상은 짧고, 쇠퇴는 길고 다단계** — 전형 부상 ≤ 5일, 쇠퇴는 수일-수개월. 자속 상쇄·MMF·supergranular 분산·toroidal 분리(~5개월)가 모두 기여.

4. **Flux cancellation builds flux ropes** — The van Ballegooijen & Martens (1989) mechanism converts potential field into a highly-sheared flux rope via PIL reconnection. Observationally up to 10%/day of total AR flux can be cancelled (Green et al. 2011: 34% in 3.5 days). This is the accepted origin of decay-phase filaments and sigmoids, and explains why CMEs persist long after flare activity ceases. / **자속 상쇄가 flux rope을 만든다** — van Ballegooijen & Martens 메커니즘이 PIL 재결합을 통해 potential 자기장을 강전단 flux rope로 변환. 관측적으로 총 AR 자속의 10%/day까지 상쇄 가능하며, flare 활동이 끝난 뒤에도 CME가 지속되는 이유를 설명한다.

5. **Differential rotation is an inefficient helicity source** — AR 7978's six-rotation helicity budget shows ΔH_diff rot ~ 8.3 × 10⁴² Mx² vs ΔH_emergence ~ 55–241 × 10⁴² Mx². Inherent flux-tube twist dominates coronal helicity injection. CME helicity ejection rate ~10⁴² Mx²/event. / **차등 자전은 비효율적 헬리시티 원천** — AR 7978 예산에서 차등 자전 ~8.3 vs 부상 ~55–241 × 10⁴² Mx². 고유 flux-tube twist가 지배적. CME당 ~10⁴² Mx² 배출.

6. **Coronal heating is magnetic-stress dominated** — The observed power-law H_T ∝ B̄^2 for AR 7978, combined with the near-linear ΔF ∝ B̄^{1.1–1.2} relation across 19 wavelengths, strongly favours dissipation of stressed, current-carrying fields and MHD turbulence over wave heating. This is consistent with stellar L_x ∝ Φ^{1.15} over 12 magnitudes. / **코로나 가열은 자기 응력 지배** — H_T ∝ B̄^2와 ΔF ∝ B̄^1.1–1.2는 응력장 소산과 MHD turbulence를 지지. 별 L_x ∝ Φ^1.15와 일치.

7. **Nesting truncates single-AR lifetimes but prolongs activity complexes** — ARs emerging within existing AR boundaries (Cassini 1729; Liggett & Zirin 1985: 10–22 times higher emergence rate inside existing AR) trigger intense local cancellation. Individual bipole lifetimes shorten, but the activity complex as a whole can persist for many rotations with compound magnetic complexity and enhanced flare/CME productivity. / **Nesting은 개별 AR 수명은 줄이지만 활동 복합체는 연장** — 기존 AR 경계 내 부상(Cassini 1729)은 강력한 국소 상쇄를 유발. 개별 bipole 수명은 짧아지나 활동 복합체는 여러 회전 지속, 복합 자기 복잡도와 강화된 flare/CME 생산성.

8. **AR 7978 is the Rosetta Stone of AR long-term evolution** — Because it was the only AR on-disc during the cycle 22/23 minimum for 6 rotations, it served simultaneously as: (i) test bed for photometric starspot models, (ii) probe of "sun-as-an-X-ray-star" variability, (iii) helicity budget closure case, (iv) coronal heating–B scaling law source, (v) flare-vs-CME evolutionary decoupling example. No other AR has been analysed so completely. / **AR 7978은 AR 장기 진화의 로제타 스톤** — 주기 22/23 극소기에 유일한 on-disc AR로 6 회전 지속하여 starspot 모델 시험대·sun-as-X-ray-star 탐침·헬리시티 예산·코로나 가열-B 스케일링·flare 대 CME 분리 사례로 동시 기능.

## 4. Mathematical Summary / 수학적 요약

**1. MHD Induction Equation (governing equation of decay)**
$$\frac{\partial\mathbf{B}}{\partial t} = \nabla\times(\mathbf{v}\times\mathbf{B}) + \eta\nabla^2\mathbf{B}$$
**Terms**: first term = advection of field by plasma flows; second term = ohmic diffusion (η = magnetic diffusivity). The magnetic Reynolds number R_m ≡ vℓ/η determines which dominates. For sunspot decay at ℓ = 3000 km, η = 300 m²/s, diffusion timescale ~ℓ²/η ~ 1000 yr — so decay must be advective, requiring B < ~615 G before convection (ρv²/2 ≈ B²/8π with ρ = 4.9×10⁻⁶ kg/m³, v = 1 km/s) can fragment the field.

**2. Joy's law (modern form)**
$$\gamma = 32.1^\circ \sin b \quad\text{(Stenflo \& Kosovichev 2012)}$$
or, in sine form (Wang & Sheeley 1991):
$$\sin\gamma = 0.48\sin\theta + 0.03$$
**Numerical examples**: b = 10° → γ ≈ 5.57°; b = 20° → γ ≈ 10.98°; b = 30° → γ ≈ 16.05°. Rule of thumb **sin γ ≈ 0.5 sin b** works for latitudes ≤ 20°.

**3. Wang & Sheeley flux–separation scaling**
$$\Phi(s) = 4\times10^{20}\,\text{Mx}\cdot s^{1.3}$$
where s is polarity separation in heliographic degrees. Example: s = 5° → Φ ≈ 3.22×10²¹ Mx; s = 10° → Φ ≈ 7.96×10²¹ Mx.

**4. Flux emergence rate for ephemeral regions** (Yang & Zhang 2014, ~3000 ERs from SDO/HMI)
$$\dot\Phi_{\text{ER}} = 2.6\times10^5\,\text{Mx/s}$$
Mean emergence duration: 50 min; peak flux 9.3×10¹⁸ Mx; separation velocity 1.1 km/s.

**5. Flux cancellation rate** — observed range
$$\dot\Phi_{\text{cancel}} \lesssim 0.10\,\Phi_{\text{AR}}\,/\text{day} \approx 10^{20}\text{--}10^{21}\,\text{Mx/day}$$
Specific cases: Green et al. (2011): 34% cancelled in 3.5 days; Baker et al. (2012): 20% in 2 days; Mackay et al. (2011): 8%/day rate.

**6. Compactness parameter** (Karachik et al. 2010)
$$r_{\text{eff}}^2 = \frac{1}{S}\sum_{i,j}\left[(x_{i,j}-\bar x)^2 + (y_{i,j}-\bar y)^2\right] B_z(x_{i,j},y_{i,j})$$
with S = Σ B_z. During decay, r²_eff typically doubles while Φ drops to 40–17% of initial.

**7. Disconnection from toroidal roots** (Schrijver & Title 1999)
$$t_{\text{AR}}[\text{days}] = 15\,\frac{\Phi}{10^{21}\,\text{Mx}}$$
Example: Φ = 2×10²² Mx (large AR) → t_AR ~ 300 days, but empirically ~5 months.

**8. Coronal magnetic helicity generation** (Berger 1984, 1988)
$$\frac{dH_r}{dt} = -2\oint_S (\mathbf{A}_p\cdot\mathbf{v})\,B_n\,dS$$
with A_p = vector potential of the potential field. Generates twist helicity (rotation of one polarity) and writhe helicity (relative translation of opposite polarities) — partially cancelling.

**9. Magnetic energy available**
$$E_{\text{mag}} = \int \frac{B^2}{8\pi}\,dV, \quad E_{\text{free}} = E_{\text{mag}} - E_{\text{potential}}$$
Mackay et al. (2011): free-energy injection rate (2.5–3)×10²⁵ erg/s for AR 8005; a CME removes ~10³¹–10³² erg.

**10. Decay parameter (linear spot area decay)**
$$\frac{dA}{dt} \approx -(0.2\text{--}0.5)\,\text{MSH/day} \quad \Leftrightarrow \quad \frac{d\Phi}{dt}\sim -10^{20}\,\text{Mx/day}$$
Typical decay rate for medium-size spot.

**11. Power-law flux distribution** (Parnell et al. 2009)
$$n(\Phi)\propto \Phi^{-1.85\pm0.14},\quad 10^{16}\le\Phi\le10^{23}\,\text{Mx}$$
Scale-free distribution spanning 7 orders in flux and 18 in frequency.

**12. Flux–B heating scaling** (Démoulin 2004)
$$\Delta F \propto \bar B^{b},\quad b\approx 1.1\text{--}1.2 \quad(\text{UV through SXR after }R(T)\text{ correction})$$
Coronal heating rate H_T ∝ B̄^b with b ≈ 2 — consistent with stressed-field dissipation, not wave heating.
