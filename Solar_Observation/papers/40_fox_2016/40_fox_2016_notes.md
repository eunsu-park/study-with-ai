# 40. The Solar Probe Plus Mission: Humanity's First Visit to Our Star / 솔라 프로브 플러스 미션 — 인류 최초의 태양 방문

---

## 1. Core Contribution / 핵심 기여

This paper is the canonical **mission overview** for NASA's Solar Probe Plus (SPP), renamed Parker Solar Probe (PSP) in May 2017 to honor Eugene N. Parker — the first time NASA named a mission after a living person. The paper synthesizes (i) the **science motivation** rooted in five decades of unanswered questions about coronal heating, solar wind acceleration, and energetic particle production; (ii) the **mission design** with 7 Venus Gravity Assists (VGAs) and 24 perihelion passes ranging from 35.7 R_S down to 9.86 R_S; (iii) the **spacecraft architecture** dominated by the carbon-carbon Thermal Protection System (TPS) that survives 475 Suns of irradiance (~649 kW/m²); and (iv) the **four instrument suites** — FIELDS (electromagnetic fields), ISIS (energetic particles), SWEAP (thermal solar wind), and WISPR (white-light imager) — selected by NASA in September 2010. Three top-level science objectives are explicitly stated and serve as the organizing framework for the entire mission: (1) trace the flow of energy that heats the corona and accelerates the solar wind; (2) determine the structure and dynamics of the plasma and magnetic fields at the sources of the solar wind; (3) explore mechanisms that accelerate and transport energetic particles.

이 논문은 NASA의 Solar Probe Plus(SPP, 2017년 5월 Parker Solar Probe로 개명 — NASA가 살아있는 인물의 이름을 미션에 부여한 최초 사례) 미션의 정전(canonical) 개요 논문이다. 다음 네 축을 종합한다: (i) 50년에 걸친 코로나 가열·태양풍 가속·고에너지 입자 생성에 관한 미해결 질문의 **과학적 동기**, (ii) 7번의 **금성 중력 보조(VGA)** 와 24번의 근일점 통과를 포함하는 **미션 설계**(35.7 R_S → 9.86 R_S 단계적 감소), (iii) 9.86 R_S에서 475 Suns(약 649 kW/m²)의 열 환경을 견디는 **carbon-carbon Thermal Protection System(TPS)** 중심 설계 철학, (iv) NASA가 2010년 9월에 선정한 **네 개의 관측 장비 스위트** — FIELDS(전자기장), ISIS(고에너지 입자), SWEAP(열적 태양풍), WISPR(백색광 이미저). 세 가지 최상위 과학 목표는 (1) 코로나를 가열하고 태양풍을 가속하는 에너지 흐름 추적, (2) 태양풍 발원 영역의 플라즈마·자기장 구조 결정, (3) 고에너지 입자의 가속·수송 메커니즘 탐구이며, 이 세 축이 미션 전체를 조직하는 골격이다.

## 2. Reading Notes / 읽기 노트

### Part I: Introduction & History / 도입과 역사 (Sec. 1, Box 1)

**English.** The introduction sets out three top-level facts: (a) SPP will sample the corona in-situ to reveal how it is heated and how the solar wind and SEPs are accelerated; (b) the seven-year nominal mission walks the perihelion down via seven VGAs to <10 R_S, "the closest any spacecraft has come to the Sun"; (c) total time inside 20 R_S is **937 hours**, inside 15 R_S is **440 hours**, and inside 10 R_S is **14 hours** — small but unprecedented. SPP orbits in the ecliptic plane, so it cannot directly sample the fast wind from polar coronal holes (Ulysses' domain); it compensates by spending so much time inside 20 R_S that latitudinal extensions of high-latitude coronal holes can be sampled. Near 35 R_S, twice per orbit, SPP enters a **quasi-corotation** interval — its angular speed approximately matches the Sun's rotation, allowing it to sample one flux tube radially before crossing into a new sector. Box 1 traces the historical evolution: the 1958 NRC Simpson Committee Report, eight major studies between 1962-2007, the 2003 NRC Decadal Survey, the 2005 design (SP2005) which used a Jupiter Gravity Assist for two polar passes at ~4 R_S, and the 2008 STDT redesign (McComas et al. 2008) into the current SPP concept. The redesign trades a slightly larger closest-approach distance (9.86 R_S vs. 4 R_S) for **24 orbits instead of 2** and **>2100 hours of close-Sun data instead of ~160**.

**한국어.** 도입부는 세 가지 핵심 사실을 제시한다: (a) SPP는 코로나에 직접 진입해 가열 메커니즘과 태양풍·SEP 가속 과정을 in-situ로 관측한다; (b) 7년 명목 미션 동안 7번의 금성 중력 보조로 근일점을 점진적으로 <10 R_S까지 낮추며, 이는 인류 우주선 사상 최근접이다; (c) 20 R_S 이내 누적 시간은 **937시간**, 15 R_S 이내는 **440시간**, 10 R_S 이내는 **14시간**이다. SPP는 황도면 궤도이므로 Ulysses처럼 극지 고속 태양풍을 직접 샘플링할 수는 없지만, 20 R_S 이내에서 매우 오랜 시간을 보내며 고위도 코로나 구멍의 적도 방향 확장을 측정한다. 35 R_S 부근에서는 궤도당 두 번 SPP가 태양 자전과 거의 같은 각속도를 가지는 **quasi-corotation 구간**에 진입해, 단일 flux tube를 방사 방향으로 길게 샘플링한다. Box 1은 1958년 NRC Simpson 보고서 → 1962-2007년 8회의 연구 → 2005년 SP2005 설계(목성 중력 보조, 2회 극궤도, ~4 R_S) → 2008년 STDT 재설계(McComas et al. 2008)로 이어지는 역사를 다룬다. 새 컨셉은 최근접 거리(4 → 9.86 R_S)를 약간 양보하는 대신 **2회 → 24회 궤도**, **~160시간 → >2100시간**으로 데이터 양을 13배 이상 늘린다.

### Part II: Science Objective 1 — Energy Flow / 과학 목표 1 — 에너지 흐름 (Sec. 2.1)

**English.** Objective 1 asks "*Trace the flow of energy that heats the solar corona and accelerates the solar wind*." Three sub-questions are posed:
- **2.1.1**: How is energy from the lower atmosphere transferred to and dissipated in the corona/wind?
- **2.1.2**: What processes shape the non-equilibrium velocity distribution functions (VDFs) seen throughout the heliosphere?
- **2.1.3**: How do coronal processes affect solar-wind properties in the heliosphere?

Two competing classes of heating mechanisms are debated: (a) **wave/turbulence dissipation** — Parker (1972) conjectured that "tangling" of magnetic field lines produces current sheets dissipating as nanoflares; Alfvén-wave nonlinear cascade (Velli et al. 1989, Verdini & Velli 2007) gives the open-field analogue; stochastic heating (Xia et al. 2013) and ion-cyclotron resonance (Axford & McKenzie 1992) are alternatives; (b) **reconnection** in jets, plumes, and at flux-tube boundaries.

Figs. 2-3 (from Verdini et al. 2009, Verdini & Velli 2007) show simulated Elsässer Z⁺/Z⁻ amplitudes and the rms δu, δb energies as functions of r/R_⊙. The Alfvén critical height (~12-13 R_S) is where wave reflection produces the inward (Z⁻) component beyond which all waves propagate outward. SPP will measure these Z± amplitudes directly, testing whether nonlinear cascade is sufficient.

For VDFs (2.1.2): observed eVDFs have three components — thermal core, suprathermal halo, and magnetic-field-aligned **strahl** (Rosenbauer et al. 1977, Maksimovic et al. 2005, Fig. 4). Proton VDFs show anisotropic cores with T_⊥ > T_∥, and a field-aligned beam at ~Alfvén speed. Helios/Ulysses/Wind data plot anisotropy vs. β_∥ near firehose/mirror instability thresholds (Bale et al. 2009, Matteini et al. 2007, Fig. 5). SPP will determine the heliocentric distance at which instability thresholds are reached and whether ion beam formation is evolutionary or coronal in origin.

For 2.1.3 (turbulence evolution): Fig. 6 shows magnetic field power spectra with break frequencies that move to lower frequency with increasing heliocentric distance — evidence of a non-linear cascade. SPP will measure spectra below the Alfvén point to see how turbulence structures form and evolve.

**한국어.** 첫 번째 목표는 "*코로나 가열과 태양풍 가속을 일으키는 에너지의 흐름 추적*"이다. 세 하위 질문(2.1.1-3)은 (a) 하부 대기에서 코로나/태양풍으로의 에너지 전달과 소산, (b) 비평형 속도분포함수(VDF)를 만드는 과정, (c) 코로나 과정이 헬리오스피어 속성에 미치는 영향을 묻는다.

가열 메커니즘은 두 부류가 경쟁한다: **파동·난류 소산**(Parker 1972의 nanoflare, Alfvén 비선형 캐스케이드, 확률적 가열, ion-cyclotron 공명) vs. **재결합**(jet, plume, flux-tube 경계). Fig. 2-3은 Elsässer Z⁺/Z⁻ 진폭과 δu, δb의 r 의존성을 보여주며, 알펜 임계 고도(~12-13 R_S) 부근에서 파동 반사가 일어나 Z⁻ 성분이 생겨난다. SPP는 이 Z± 진폭을 직접 측정해 비선형 캐스케이드가 코로나 가열에 충분한지 테스트한다.

VDF(2.1.2): 전자 VDF는 코어·할로·strahl 세 성분으로 구성되며(Fig. 4), 양성자 VDF는 T_⊥ > T_∥의 비등방 코어와 알펜 속도의 빔 성분을 보인다. Bale et al. (2009)은 firehose/mirror 불안정 임계 부근에서 비등방성이 제한됨을 보였다(Fig. 5). SPP는 비등방성이 임계에 도달하는 거리와 빔 형성이 코로나 기원인지 헬리오스피어 진화 산물인지를 결정한다. 2.1.3: Fig. 6의 자기장 파워 스펙트럼은 거리 증가에 따른 break frequency 이동을 보이며, SPP는 알펜 점 이하에서 처음으로 이를 측정한다.

### Part III: Science Objective 2 — Solar Wind Sources / 과학 목표 2 — 태양풍 발원 (Sec. 2.2)

**English.** Objective 2: "*Determine the structure and dynamics of the plasma and magnetic fields at the sources of the solar wind.*" Sub-questions:
- **2.2.1**: How does the source-region magnetic field connect to the photosphere and heliosphere?
- **2.2.2**: Are the sources steady or intermittent?
- **2.2.3**: How do observed coronal structures evolve into the solar wind?

Standard model: PFSS (Altschuler & Newkirk 1969, Schatten et al. 1969) maps photospheric field upward to a source surface; the slow wind originates near coronal-hole boundaries (Wang & Sheeley 1990) and may also come from helmet streamers (Sheeley 1997), pseudo-streamers, or a network of separatrices/quasi-separatrix layers (Antiochos et al. 2012). Composition (low-FIP enhancement, charge state of Fe/O) distinguishes slow-wind plasma from coronal-hole fast wind. SPP will sample fine-scale fast/slow boundaries close to the Sun and measure the helmet streamer reconnection products directly.

For 2.2.2: the slow wind is **bursty** and intermittent at 1 AU (Padhye et al. 2001), suggesting interchange reconnection between open and closed loops (Fisk 1996, Schwadron & McComas 2003). Even fast wind may have intermittent injection origins (chromospheric reconnection). SPP's near-Sun multi-orbit measurements will resolve sub-1-AU structures.

For 2.2.3: Fig. 7 shows fine-scale corona structures — pseudo-streamers, helmet streamers, polar plumes, prominence cavities. Plumes recur for days (DeForest et al. 2001), with footpoints showing high-frequency jetlets (Raouafi & Stenborg 2014). SPP combines magnetic, plasma, and composition data with WISPR imaging to track plume contributions to the wind. The quasi-corotation intervals decouple temporal and spatial variability.

**한국어.** 두 번째 목표는 "*태양풍 발원 영역의 플라즈마·자기장 구조와 동역학 결정*"이다. 표준 모델은 PFSS(Altschuler & Newkirk 1969)로 광구 자기장을 source surface로 사상하며, 저속풍은 coronal hole 경계(Wang & Sheeley 1990) 또는 helmet streamer/pseudo-streamer/separatrix 네트워크(Antiochos et al. 2012)에서 유래한다고 본다. 합성(low-FIP 강화, Fe/O charge state)이 저속풍과 고속풍을 구별한다. SPP는 fast/slow 경계를 태양 가까이에서 처음으로 직접 샘플링한다.

2.2.2: 저속풍은 1 AU에서 **bursty**·간헐적이며(Padhye et al. 2001) 개방·폐쇄 루프 간 interchange reconnection(Fisk 1996)을 시사한다. 고속풍도 chromospheric reconnection 기원일 수 있다. SPP의 다중 궤도 데이터는 1 AU 미만 미세 구조를 해소한다. 2.2.3: Fig. 7은 pseudo-streamer, helmet streamer, polar plume, prominence cavity 등 미세 구조를 보여준다. plume은 며칠에 걸쳐 재발하며(DeForest et al. 2001) 발자취에는 jetlet 활동이 있다(Raouafi & Stenborg 2014). SPP는 자기장·플라즈마·합성·WISPR 영상을 결합해 plume 기여를 추적하며, quasi-corotation 구간이 시·공간 변동을 분리한다.

### Part IV: Science Objective 3 — Energetic Particles / 과학 목표 3 — 고에너지 입자 (Sec. 2.3)

**English.** Objective 3: "*Explore mechanisms that accelerate and transport energetic particles.*" Sub-questions concern shock/reconnection/wave/turbulence roles, source populations and conditions, and transport in the heliosphere.

Particles propagating from the Sun to 1 AU undergo significant scattering, distorting time profiles and energy spectra. SPP measures SEPs in the 9.86-53.7 R_S range where propagation effects are minimized.

Fig. 8 sketches the canonical paradigm: SEPs originate in flares (resonant acceleration in turbulent fluctuations) and at CME-driven shock waves (diffusive shock acceleration, DSA). The shock front spirals into the heliosphere, with energetic particles streaming along magnetic field lines drape over the CME. Fig. 9 (ISEE-3 1978) shows ~28 keV magnetic field fluctuations upstream of a quasi-parallel shock — evidence of self-generated waves that scatter particles, enabling DSA closure.

Possible mechanisms:
- **DSA at CME shocks** (Lee 2005)
- **Statistical Fermi** (Jokipii 1971)
- **Transit-time damping** (Fisk 1976)
- **Stochastic acceleration** by MHD waves (Jokipii & Lee 2010)
- **Compression-region acceleration** (Giacalone et al. 2002)

For source populations: Fig. 10 shows the suprathermal tail (10⁻³-10 MeV/n) bridging bulk solar wind and SEPs/GCRs. SPP measurements close to the Sun observe the inner source of pickup hydrogen near origin (Schwadron et al. 2000), and constrain whether suprathermal seed populations come from remnant impulsive flares, direct flares, solar wind, or CME material. For transport: cross-field diffusion vs. direct connection — SPP combined with 1 AU spacecraft constrains both. Fig. 11 (Dröge et al. 2010) shows simulated 107 keV electron distribution at heliocentric 0.05 AU, demonstrating SPP's vantage near the acceleration sites.

**한국어.** 세 번째 목표는 "*고에너지 입자의 가속·수송 메커니즘 탐구*"이다. 1 AU에 도달한 SEP는 산란으로 시간 프로파일과 스펙트럼이 왜곡된다. SPP는 9.86-53.7 R_S 영역에서 SEP를 측정해 전파 효과를 최소화한다.

Fig. 8의 표준 패러다임: SEP는 (a) 플레어의 난류 공명 가속과 (b) CME 추진 충격파의 diffusive shock acceleration(DSA)로 생성된다. Fig. 9는 1978년 ISEE-3가 관측한 quasi-parallel 충격파 상류의 자기장 변동으로, 입자가 스스로 만든 파동이 자신을 산란시켜 DSA를 가능케 한다. 가능한 메커니즘은 DSA, 통계적 Fermi, transit-time damping, 확률적 MHD 파동 가속, 압축 영역 가속이다.

Fig. 10의 suprathermal tail(10⁻³-10 MeV/n)이 bulk 태양풍과 SEP/GCR을 잇는다. SPP는 pickup 수소의 inner source(Schwadron et al. 2000)를 기원 부근에서 직접 관측하며, suprathermal seed 인구의 출처(잔여 플레어, 태양풍, CME)를 합성으로 제약한다. Fig. 11은 0.05 AU에서 107 keV 전자 분포 시뮬레이션이며, SPP가 가속 영역 가까이에서 관측함을 보인다.

### Part V: SPP Observations & Instruments / 관측과 기기 (Sec. 3)

**English.** Section 3 introduces the four science investigations selected in September 2010:

| Investigation | Lead | What it measures |
|---|---|---|
| **FIELDS** | UC Berkeley (Bale) | DC + AC magnetic field (3 fluxgate + search-coil), 5 electric antennas, plasma waves, density fluctuations, radio emissions |
| **ISIS** (EPI-Hi + EPI-Lo) | SwRI (McComas) | Energetic electrons, protons, heavy ions: 10s keV to 100 MeV |
| **SWEAP** (SPAN A/B + SPC) | SAO (Kasper) | Thermal solar wind: e⁻, p⁺, He²⁺ velocity, density, temperature |
| **WISPR** | NRL (Howard) | White-light coronagraph: structures approaching/passing spacecraft |

There is also a theory/modeling investigation **HeliOSPP** led by Marco Velli. The science requirements are written in measurement terms (not instrument terms) so the mission is robust against any single instrument failure: minimum mission success requires ≥920 hours below 20 R_S and ≥14 hours below 10 R_S. Table 1 (with continuation Table 1') is the backbone — a matrix mapping each science question to measurement strategies and which of {plasma, magnetic field, plasma waves, energetic particles, large-scale structures} the strategy uses. Multiple instrument combinations can address each science question, providing redundancy.

**한국어.** 4개 과학 조사단이 2010년 9월에 선정되었다: FIELDS(전자기장, UCB), ISIS(고에너지 입자, SwRI), SWEAP(열적 태양풍, SAO), WISPR(백색광 영상, NRL). 추가로 HeliOSPP 이론·모델링(Marco Velli) 조사단이 있다. 과학 요구사항은 기기 단위가 아닌 측정 단위로 표현되어 단일 기기 고장에도 견고하다: 최소 미션 성공 조건은 20 R_S 이하 ≥920시간, 10 R_S 이하 ≥14시간이다. Table 1은 각 과학 질문 → 측정 전략 → 필요 측정 종류를 사상하는 매트릭스로, 다중 기기 조합으로 같은 질문을 풀 수 있게 한다.

### Part VI: Mission Design / 미션 설계 (Sec. 4)

**English.** *Trajectory.* SPP launches on a Venus-intercept trajectory (Delta IV Heavy + STAR-48B upper stage, C3 = 154 km²/s²). Six weeks after launch, the first VGA places SPP into an elliptical orbit with perihelion 35 R_S (0.16 AU) and aphelion ~1 AU. Six subsequent VGAs decrease perihelion in stages to 9.86 R_S. Launch window: 31 July - 18 August 2018. Fig. 13 shows the trajectory viewed from above the ecliptic with the seven Venus flybys; Fig. 14 shows the perihelion walkdown — distance to Sun vs. time — with each VGA visibly stepping the perihelion lower.

*Encounter and cruise phases.* Each orbit divides into a **solar encounter** (closer than 0.25 AU) where instruments operate at ~100% duty cycle and store data, and a **cruise/downlink** phase (>0.25 AU) where data is sent to Earth via Ka-band high-gain antenna and the spacecraft is positioned for navigation, trajectory correction, and Venus flybys. Communications are limited during encounter due to thermal pointing constraints.

Table 3 gives orbit time below thresholds. Highlights: Total time below 30 R_S = **2130.85 hr**; below 20 R_S = **937.58 hr**; below 15 R_S = **440.03 hr**; below 10 R_S = **14.85 hr** (in the final 3 orbits #22-24, each contributing ~4.95 hr at 9.86 R_S).

*Near-Sun environment.* At 9.86 R_S, surfaces facing the Sun receive 475 Suns ≈ 649 kW/m² (vs. 1366 W/m² at Earth). The Sun cannot be treated as a point source — the angular diameter is ~5.82°. The TPS's penumbra region reduces irradiance on solar arrays, and the array wing 70° incidence angle further lowers it to ~25 Suns at end-of-life. SPP must accommodate UV irradiance, charging, dust impacts, and radiation damage.

*Spacecraft.* SPP launch mass: 685 kg (with propellant). Height: ~3 m. TPS diameter: 2.3 m. The TPS is a **carbon-carbon composite/carbon-foam sandwich** with an alumina coating optimized for high α/ε ratio. The TPS coating + foam absorbs 6500 W of heat at minimum perihelion. Under the TPS, an actively cooled solar array (Solar Array Cooling System, SACS) with water as coolant pumps heat to four Cooling System Primary Radiators (CSPRs). The cooling system maintains the solar cells below 150°C at minimum perihelion. Solar arrays have primary + secondary sections with different cant angles; only the secondary is illuminated at perihelion (reaching ~25 Suns at EOL).

The spacecraft has 3-axis G&C with redundant star trackers, IMU, 7 Solar Limb Sensors (SLS) on brackets near the bottom deck, and 2 Digital Sun Sensors (DSS) on the anti-ram side. The SLS detects excursions from Sun-pointing within ~2° umbra margin. Pointing accuracy ≤0.1° under nominal conditions. Actuation: 4 reaction wheels + 12 hydrazine thrusters. Avionics: triple redundant single-board computer (prime + hot spare + backup spare) with a redundancy controller.

The TPS is an 8° packaging umbra in ram/anti-ram, 8.9° in ±y, providing ≥2° margin against the actual 5.82° umbra at perihelion. SLS provide additional warning of nearing umbra violation.

**한국어.** *궤도.* SPP는 Delta IV Heavy + STAR-48B 상단으로 발사되며 발사 에너지 C3=154 km²/s². 발사 6주 후 첫 VGA로 perihelion 35 R_S (0.16 AU)·aphelion ~1 AU 타원궤도에 진입한다. 이후 6번의 VGA로 perihelion을 9.86 R_S까지 단계적으로 낮춘다. 발사창은 2018년 7월 31일-8월 18일. Fig. 13은 황도면 위에서 본 궤도와 7번의 금성 flyby, Fig. 14는 perihelion walkdown(태양 거리 vs. 시간)을 보인다.

*Encounter 단계.* 각 궤도는 0.25 AU 이내의 **solar encounter** 단계(기기 100% 가동, 데이터 저장)와 0.25 AU 이상의 **cruise/downlink** 단계(Ka 밴드 고이득 안테나로 다운링크, 궤도 조정, Venus flyby)로 나뉜다. Encounter 중에는 열·자세 제약으로 통신이 제한된다.

Table 3 정량 요약: 30 R_S 이하 누적 시간 **2130.85시간**, 20 R_S 이하 **937.58시간**, 15 R_S 이하 **440.03시간**, 10 R_S 이하 **14.85시간**(최종 3 궤도 #22-24가 각각 ~4.95시간 기여).

*근태양 환경.* 9.86 R_S에서 태양 복사는 475 Suns ≈ 649 kW/m²(지구 대비 475배). 태양은 점광원이 아니며 각지름 5.82°. TPS의 penumbra는 태양 어레이 조도를 줄이고, 70° 입사각으로 EOL ~25 Suns까지 낮춘다. UV·하전·먼지 충돌·복사 손상도 고려해야 한다.

*우주선.* 발사 질량 685 kg, 높이 ~3 m, TPS 지름 2.3 m. TPS는 **carbon-carbon 복합재/carbon-foam 샌드위치**, 알루미나 코팅, 고 α/ε 비율로 최적화. TPS는 perihelion에서 6500 W 흡수. 그 아래에 능동 냉각식 태양 어레이(SACS, 물 냉각수)가 4개의 CSPR로 열을 방출하며, 태양전지 셀을 150°C 이하로 유지한다. 태양 어레이는 primary + secondary 섹션이 있고, perihelion에서는 secondary만 조명된다(EOL ~25 Suns). 우주선은 3축 안정화이며 redundant star tracker, IMU, 7개 Solar Limb Sensor(SLS), 2개 Digital Sun Sensor를 갖는다. SLS는 ~2° umbra margin 내에서 Sun-pointing 이탈을 감지한다. 자세 정확도 ≤0.1°. 4 reaction wheel + 12 hydrazine 추력기. 항공전자: 삼중화 SBC(prime + hot spare + backup spare).

TPS의 packaging umbra: ram/anti-ram 8°, ±y 8.9° — perihelion에서 실제 5.82° umbra에 대해 ≥2° margin 확보.

### Part VII: Technology Development / 기술 개발 (Sec. 4.3)

**English.** Six critical technologies developed specifically for SPP: (1) **TPS** — full-scale fabricated and tested in launch (vibration/acoustic) and thermal-vacuum environments more severe than expected within 10 R_S; (2) **solar arrays** — primary + secondary sections, full-size secondary section panel (27 cm × 65 cm) tested with flight-like water-cooled substrate at 25 Suns; (3) **Solar Array Cooling System (SACS)** — half-scale full-size unit demonstrated in thermal-vacuum chamber; dissipates 6500 W; uses water as coolant (chosen for high heat capacity, freezing-point management); (4) **Solar Limb Sensors** — protect against umbra violation; (5) **SWEAP SPC** — Faraday cup that extends beyond TPS and sees the same environment as the TPS sun-facing surface; (6) **FIELDS electric field antennas** — also extend beyond TPS. All technologies completed development by Preliminary Design Review (PDR), reaching the required Technology Readiness Levels.

**한국어.** SPP를 위해 개발된 6가지 핵심 기술: (1) **TPS** — 전체 크기 제작·발사 환경(진동·음향)·열진공 환경에서 시험; (2) **태양 어레이** — primary + secondary 분할, 풀사이즈 secondary 패널(27 × 65 cm)에 비행급 물 냉각 substrate 적용해 25 Suns 시험; (3) **SACS** — 풀사이즈 절반 유닛으로 6500 W 방열 검증, 물 냉매(고비열, 동결점 관리); (4) **Solar Limb Sensor** — umbra 위반 방지; (5) **SWEAP SPC** — TPS 너머로 돌출되는 Faraday cup, TPS 햇볕면과 같은 환경 노출; (6) **FIELDS 전기장 안테나** — TPS 너머 돌출. 모든 기술은 PDR까지 개발 완료.

### Part VIII: Mission Operations / 미션 운영 (Sec. 5)

**English.** SPP uses a **decoupled operations** concept: instrument teams operate their instruments independently of bus operations. Mission Operations Center (MOC) handles bus, while four Science Operation Centers (SOCs, one per investigation) handle instrument planning. Communications use NASA Deep Space Network (DSN) Ka-band for science downlink and X-band for housekeeping. Data flow: SOC creates Instrument Supplemented Command Files (ISCF) → MOC validates → spacecraft executes; spacecraft Solid State Recorder (SSR) sized for two consecutive orbits (some orbits cannot fully downlink in time). The Orbit Planning Team (OPT) creates Orbit Operations Templates (OOT) ~3 months ahead of each orbit. The Science Working Group (SWG) coordinates inter-instrument science activities. During cruise (>0.7 AU), the spacecraft is pointed 45° off-Sun to maintain temperatures and reduce heater power; <0.82 AU, attitude is between 0.7-0.76 AU off-Sun pointing for HGA Earth-pointing.

**한국어.** SPP는 **분리 운영(decoupled operations)** 컨셉: 기기 팀은 버스와 무관하게 기기를 운영한다. MOC는 버스를, 4개 SOC는 각 기기를 담당한다. 통신은 DSN Ka 밴드(과학 다운링크) + X 밴드(housekeeping). 데이터 흐름: SOC가 ISCF 작성 → MOC 검증 → 우주선 실행; SSR은 연속 2궤도 데이터를 저장할 크기(다운링크가 한 궤도분을 다 못 받는 경우 대비). OPT가 궤도 3개월 전 OOT 작성. SWG가 기기 간 과학 활동 조율. 0.7 AU 이상 cruise 중에는 45° off-Sun pointing으로 온도 유지·히터 전력 절감; 0.82 AU 미만에서는 HGA Earth-pointing을 위해 0.7-0.76 AU 자세 사용.

### Part IX: Worked Example — Reproducing the "475 Suns" Claim / 작동 예제: "475 Suns" 도출

**English.** A small, fully-traced calculation that any reader can repeat:

1. SPP minimum perihelion: $r_p = 9.86\,R_\odot$.
2. Convert to AU: $r_p = 9.86 \times 696{,}000\,\text{km} = 6.86\times10^6\,\text{km} = 0.0459\,\text{AU}$.
3. Solar irradiance at Earth: $S_\oplus = 1366\,\text{W/m}^2$.
4. Inverse-square scaling: $S(r_p) = 1366 \times (1/0.0459)^2 = 1366 \times 474.7 \approx 6.49 \times 10^5\,\text{W/m}^2$.
5. In units of solar constant: 474.7 ≈ **475 Suns** ✓ (matches Box 1).
6. Total power received by 2.3-m diameter TPS: area ≈ π × 1.15² = 4.15 m². Power = 4.15 × 6.49 × 10⁵ ≈ **2.7 MW**.
7. With α/ε = 0.6 alumina coating, the TPS dissipates at $T = (\alpha S/\varepsilon\sigma)^{1/4} \approx (0.6 \times 6.49\times 10^5 / 5.67\times 10^{-8})^{1/4} \approx 1700$ K.

The 6500 W of heat dissipated by the SACS (mentioned in Sec. 4.2) is the heat *that gets through* the TPS to the solar arrays — i.e., $\sim 0.0024$ of the 2.7 MW incident, demonstrating the TPS suppression factor of ~400× via reflection + reradiation.

**한국어.** 누구나 따라할 수 있는 작동 계산:

1. SPP 최근접 perihelion: $r_p = 9.86\,R_\odot$.
2. AU 환산: $r_p = 9.86 \times 696{,}000\,\text{km} = 0.0459\,\text{AU}$.
3. 지구의 태양 상수: $S_\oplus = 1366\,\text{W/m}^2$.
4. 역제곱 법칙: $S(r_p) = 1366 \times (1/0.0459)^2 \approx 6.49 \times 10^5\,\text{W/m}^2$.
5. 태양 상수 단위로 표현: ≈ **475 Suns** ✓ (Box 1과 일치).
6. 2.3 m 지름 TPS가 받는 총 출력: 면적 4.15 m² → 입사 출력 ≈ **2.7 MW**.
7. α/ε = 0.6 알루미나 코팅에서 TPS 정상 상태 온도 $T \approx 1700\,\text{K}$.

Sec. 4.2의 SACS가 방열하는 6500 W는 TPS를 *뚫고 들어오는* 열로, 입사 2.7 MW의 ~0.0024(약 0.24%)에 해당한다. 즉 TPS는 반사 + 재방출을 통해 약 400배의 열차단 효과를 발휘한다.

### Part X: Case Study — A Single Encounter Pass / 사례 연구: 한 번의 근접 통과

**English.** Consider a representative 9.86 R_S encounter (orbit #22-24). Using Kepler's equation (Section 4.7), the spacecraft spends:
- Below 30 R_S: ~102 hr (paper Table 3, ~102.40 per orbit)
- Below 20 R_S: ~57 hr (paper: 57.02)
- Below 15 R_S: ~35 hr (paper: 35.22)
- Below 10 R_S: ~5 hr (paper: 4.95)

During the 5 hours below 10 R_S, the spacecraft sweeps from inbound 10 R_S → 9.86 R_S perihelion → outbound 10 R_S. The angular range covered is $\sim 2 \times \arccos((1 - 9.86 R_S / a) / e)$ where $a, e$ are the orbital elements. With $r_p = 9.86\,R_S, r_a = 0.73$ AU, this is $\sim 30°$. SPP at 195 km/s sweeps ~3.5 million km of radial coverage and ~13 million km of in-track distance — sampling roughly one large active-region scale.

During this 5-hour window, FIELDS samples electromagnetic fields at up to several MHz cadence; SWEAP/SPC reads the proton/alpha distribution function every ~1 second; ISIS records energetic particle events. The Solid-State Recorder (sized for 2 orbits) accumulates roughly 100 GB of science telemetry per encounter. The downlink, constrained to ~50 hours per orbit at 0.7-0.76 AU via Ka-band HGA, must queue and prioritize this dataset across multiple weeks.

**한국어.** 9.86 R_S 근접 통과(궤도 #22-24) 한 번을 살펴보자. Kepler 방정식으로 우주선이 보내는 시간:
- 30 R_S 이하: ~102시간 (논문 Table 3: 102.40)
- 20 R_S 이하: ~57시간 (논문: 57.02)
- 15 R_S 이하: ~35시간 (논문: 35.22)
- 10 R_S 이하: ~5시간 (논문: 4.95)

10 R_S 이하 5시간 동안 우주선은 inbound 10 R_S → 9.86 R_S 근일점 → outbound 10 R_S을 지나간다. 각도 범위는 $2 \arccos((1 - 9.86R_S/a)/e) \approx 30°$. 195 km/s 속도로 3.5백만 km 반경 + 13백만 km 궤도 거리 — 대규모 활동 영역 하나의 크기에 해당하는 영역을 샘플링한다.

이 5시간 동안 FIELDS는 수 MHz 캐던스로 전자기장을, SWEAP/SPC는 약 1초 간격으로 양성자·알파 분포함수를, ISIS는 고에너지 입자 이벤트를 기록한다. SSR(2궤도 분 용량)은 통과당 약 100 GB의 과학 데이터를 누적한다. 0.7-0.76 AU에서 Ka 밴드 HGA로 궤도당 ~50시간만 다운링크 가능하므로, 이 데이터셋은 수 주간에 걸쳐 우선순위에 따라 전송된다.

### Part XI: Why Eight Suns of Power Margin? / 왜 8°의 packaging umbra인가?

**English.** Solar disk angular radius at 9.86 R_S: $\theta_\odot = \arctan(R_\odot / 9.86 R_\odot) = \arctan(1/9.86) = 5.79° \approx 5.82°$ (paper). The TPS provides 8° packaging umbra in ram/anti-ram, giving ≥2° margin. With G&C pointing accuracy ≤0.1°, this margin accommodates the worst-case fault scenarios (single thruster misfire, reaction wheel saturation) and provides a window for autonomous recovery before the umbra is violated. This 2° margin is *the* fundamental design constraint that determines the size of the TPS, the placement of all instruments, and the maximum allowable solar-array wing angle.

**한국어.** 9.86 R_S에서 태양 원반 각반경 $\theta_\odot = \arctan(1/9.86) = 5.79° \approx 5.82°$. TPS는 ram/anti-ram 8° packaging umbra를 제공해 ≥2° margin을 둔다. G&C 지향 정확도 ≤0.1°와 결합하여, 이 margin은 worst-case 고장 시나리오(추력기 오작동, 반작용 휠 포화)에서 자율 복구 시간을 확보한다. 이 2° margin은 TPS 크기, 모든 기기 배치, 태양 어레이 wing 각도의 최대값을 결정하는 *근본* 설계 제약이다.

## 3. Key Takeaways / 핵심 시사점

1. **Three orbital regimes define SPP science** — SPP's 24 orbits split scientifically into three perihelion regimes: orbits #1-3 at 35 R_S (broad survey), orbits #4-21 at 19-20 R_S (Alfvén-surface crossing), and orbits #22-24 at 9.86 R_S (deepest). Each regime answers a different question — the first probes wave reflection, the middle samples sub-Alfvénic flow, the last reaches the canonical "birthplace of the supersonic solar wind." / 24 궤도는 과학적으로 세 영역으로 나뉜다 — 35 R_S(개관), 19-20 R_S(알펜 면 통과), 9.86 R_S(최심부) — 각기 다른 과학 질문에 답한다.

2. **The Venus-walkdown trades distance for orbit count** — SPP's 9.86 R_S is farther than SP2005's 4 R_S, but 24 orbits replace 2 orbits, multiplying close-Sun data hours by ~13× (~160 hr → >2100 hr). This is a paradigm shift in mission design philosophy: temporal sampling > spatial extremum. / Venus-walkdown은 거리를 양보하고 궤도 수를 얻는 거래이며, 시간적 샘플링이 공간적 최극값보다 우선한다는 미션 설계 철학의 전환이다.

3. **The TPS is the enabling technology** — Without the carbon-carbon TPS surviving 475 Suns, the mission is impossible. The 8° packaging umbra with 2° margin against 5.82° solar disk is the fundamental geometric constraint that drives spacecraft layout, pointing accuracy (≤0.1°), and instrument boom design. / TPS는 미션을 가능케 하는 핵심 기술이며, 8° packaging umbra와 5.82° 태양 원반에 대한 2° margin이 모든 설계를 좌우한다.

4. **Decoupled operations enable science autonomy** — Solar encounter (≤0.25 AU) prevents real-time control: SOC pre-plans, instruments execute autonomously, data stored on SSR for cruise-phase downlink. SSR sized for two orbits provides margin for incomplete downlinks. This decoupling propagates throughout mission design (G&C, fault management, power allocation). / 분리 운영은 0.25 AU 이내의 실시간 제어 불가 환경에 대한 답이며, SOC 사전 계획 + SSR 두 궤도 분 저장이 핵심이다.

5. **Three science objectives are not independent** — Coronal heating (Obj. 1) provides energy; magnetic source structures (Obj. 2) channel that energy; particle acceleration (Obj. 3) is the kinetic outcome. SPP's instrument suite is co-designed so each measurement contributes to multiple objectives. Table 1's matrix structure is not a mere accounting device — it is the architecture of the mission. / 세 과학 목표는 독립적이지 않으며, 코로나 가열(에너지) → 자기장 구조(채널) → 입자 가속(결과)의 인과 사슬을 이룬다.

6. **Quasi-corotation is a unique observational mode** — Twice per orbit near 35 R_S, SPP angular speed matches the Sun's. The spacecraft samples one flux tube radially over an extended interval, decoupling temporal and spatial variability. This is a feature absent in any prior mission and is enabled by SPP's specific orbit. / Quasi-corotation 구간은 SPP의 고유한 관측 모드로, 시간적·공간적 변동을 분리한다.

7. **Robustness is built into the requirements** — Science requirements are stated as measurement requirements, not instrument requirements. Multiple instrument combinations satisfy each science question; loss of any single instrument still allows minimum mission success (≥920 hr below 20 R_S, ≥14 hr below 10 R_S). This is best-practice mission engineering for high-risk missions. / 견고성은 요구사항 자체에 내장되어 있으며, 측정 단위로 표현된 요구사항과 다중 기기 조합으로 단일 기기 고장에 대한 회복력을 확보한다.

8. **SPP is a heliophysics platform, not a single experiment** — Synergies with Solar Orbiter (ESA, launched 2020), ground-based observatories (DKIST), and existing 1 AU spacecraft (Wind, ACE, STEREO) are explicitly designed in. SPP's measurements gain interpretive power only when combined with remote sensing context — multi-asset heliophysics is the new normal. / SPP는 단독 실험이 아닌 헬리오피직스 플랫폼이며, Solar Orbiter·DKIST·1 AU 우주선과의 시너지가 설계에 명시되어 있다.

## 4. Mathematical Summary / 수학적 요약

### 4.1 Solar Irradiance Scaling / 태양 복사 스케일링

The inverse-square law for solar flux:

$$
S(r) = S_\oplus \left(\frac{1\,\text{AU}}{r}\right)^2
$$

where $S_\oplus = 1366\,\text{W/m}^2$ is the solar constant at Earth, and $r$ is heliocentric distance. At SPP's minimum perihelion $r = 9.86\,R_S = 0.0459\,\text{AU}$:

$$
S(9.86\,R_S) = 1366 \times \left(\frac{1}{0.0459}\right)^2 = 1366 \times 475 \approx 6.49 \times 10^5\,\text{W/m}^2
$$

This is the "475 Suns" environment quoted throughout the paper. / 즉 9.86 R_S에서 태양 상수의 475배.

### 4.2 Vis-Viva (Orbital Speed) / 궤도 속도

For an elliptical orbit with semi-major axis $a$, the speed at radial distance $r$ is:

$$
v(r) = \sqrt{G M_\odot \left(\frac{2}{r} - \frac{1}{a}\right)}
$$

With $G M_\odot = 1.327 \times 10^{20}\,\text{m}^3/\text{s}^2$. SPP's final orbit has perihelion $r_p = 9.86\,R_S \approx 6.86 \times 10^9\,\text{m}$ and aphelion roughly $r_a \approx 0.73\,\text{AU} \approx 1.09 \times 10^{11}\,\text{m}$, so $a = (r_p + r_a)/2 \approx 5.78 \times 10^{10}\,\text{m}$. Then:

$$
v_p = \sqrt{1.327 \times 10^{20} \left(\frac{2}{6.86\times 10^9} - \frac{1}{5.78\times 10^{10}}\right)} \approx 1.95 \times 10^5\,\text{m/s} = 195\,\text{km/s}
$$

This matches the Box 1 quote: "lower speed (195 km/s) of SPP at closest approach compared to that of SP2005 (308 km s⁻¹)". The lower speed gives more sampling time per perihelion. / 즉 SPP는 SP2005보다 perihelion에서 느려서 sampling 시간이 길다.

### 4.3 Stefan-Boltzmann Equilibrium for TPS Front Face / TPS 전면 열평형

Energy balance for a flat, gray surface with absorptivity $\alpha$ and emissivity $\varepsilon$:

$$
\alpha\, S(r) = \varepsilon\, \sigma\, T^4
$$

where $\sigma = 5.67 \times 10^{-8}\,\text{W/m}^2/\text{K}^4$. At perihelion $S = 6.49 \times 10^5\,\text{W/m}^2$. With α/ε ≈ 0.6 (alumina coating optimized to reflect):

$$
T = \left(\frac{0.6 \times 6.49 \times 10^5}{5.67 \times 10^{-8}}\right)^{1/4} \approx 1700\,\text{K}
$$

This is the front-face temperature of the TPS. Heat flow through the carbon-foam core is sized to deliver only nominal levels (~30°C) to the spacecraft body. / 전면 1700 K, 후면 30°C로 단열.

### 4.4 Alfvén Speed and Critical Surface / 알펜 속도와 임계면

$$
V_A = \frac{B}{\sqrt{\mu_0\, \rho}} = \frac{B}{\sqrt{\mu_0\, n\, m_p}}
$$

where $\mu_0 = 4\pi \times 10^{-7}\,\text{H/m}$, $n$ is proton number density, $m_p$ is proton mass. The Alfvén critical surface is the locus where $V_{sw}(r) = V_A(r)$. For typical near-Sun values $B \sim 10^3\,\text{nT}$ and $n \sim 10^4\,\text{cm}^{-3}$ at 10 R_S, $V_A \sim 220\,\text{km/s}$, comparable to early solar wind. / SPP는 이 면을 처음으로 직접 통과한다.

### 4.5 Parker Spiral / 파커 나선

For purely radial outflow $v_{sw}$ with footpoint rotating at angular speed $\Omega_\odot$, the heliospheric magnetic field has spiral geometry:

$$
\frac{B_\phi}{B_r} = -\frac{\Omega_\odot\, r \sin\theta}{v_{sw}}
$$

At 1 AU and ecliptic ($\theta = 90°$), with $\Omega_\odot = 2.7 \times 10^{-6}\,\text{rad/s}$ and $v_{sw} = 400\,\text{km/s}$, $B_\phi/B_r \sim -1$ (45° spiral). At SPP perihelion (r = 9.86 R_S = 6.86 × 10⁹ m), $|B_\phi/B_r| \sim 0.05$ — nearly radial field. / SPP perihelion에서 자기장은 거의 방사형.

### 4.6 Diffusive Shock Acceleration (DSA) Spectral Index / DSA 스펙트럼 지수

For a strong, parallel, non-relativistic shock with compression ratio $r_{cr}$:

$$
f(p) \propto p^{-q},\qquad q = \frac{3 r_{cr}}{r_{cr} - 1}
$$

For $r_{cr} = 4$ (strong shock), $q = 4$, giving differential intensity $j(E) \propto E^{-1.5}$ (non-relativistic). SPP measurements close to CME-driven shocks at $\le 20\,R_S$ test this scaling before propagation distortions. / 강한 충격에서 q=4 → j(E) ∝ E^{-1.5}.

### 4.7 Mission Time Budget / 미션 시간 예산

Total mission perihelion-time below thresholds (Table 3, summed over 24 orbits):

| Threshold | Cumulative time | Fraction of 7-yr mission |
|---|---|---|
| 30 R_S | 2130.85 hr | 3.5% |
| 20 R_S | 937.58 hr | 1.5% |

The 7-year mission has ~61,000 hours total; only **0.024%** is spent inside 10 R_S, but those 14.85 hours are the most scientifically valuable hours in the history of heliophysics. / 7년 중 10 R_S 이하는 14.85시간(0.024%)뿐이지만, 헬리오피직스 역사상 가장 가치있는 시간이다.

```
1958 ─── Parker: Solar wind theory (Parker 1958 ApJ)

2005 ─── Solar Probe (SP2005) design — Jupiter GA, 4 R_S, 2 polar passes
2006 ─── STEREO launched (twin spacecraft)
2008 ─── McComas et al. STDT — SPP redesign with 7 VGAs, 24 orbits, 9.86 R_S
2010 ─── Four instrument suites selected
►2016 ─── THIS PAPER — Fox et al. mission overview (Sp. Sci. Rev.)
2018 ─── PSP launched 12 Aug (mission renamed May 2017 to honor E.N. Parker)
2018 Nov ─── First perihelion at 35.7 R_S
2019 ─── Bale et al. Nature: switchbacks discovered
2019 ─── Kasper et al. Nature: super-Alfvénic plasma jets
2020 ─── ESA Solar Orbiter launched (companion mission)
2021 ─── PSP first crosses Alfvén critical surface
2024 Dec ─── PSP first 9.86 R_S perihelion (final closest approach)
2025+ ─── Synergy with DKIST ground-based observations
```
