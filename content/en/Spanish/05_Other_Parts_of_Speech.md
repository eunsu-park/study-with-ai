## 1. Pronouns

### A. Subject Pronouns

| Pronoun | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| yo / I |
| tú / you (informal) |
| él / he |
| ella / she |
| usted / you (formal) |
| nosotros/as / we |
| vosotros/as / you all (informal) |
| ellos / they (masculine) |
| ellas / they (feminine) |
| ustedes / you all (formal) |

### B. Reflexive Pronouns

| Subject | Reflexive Pronoun | Meaning |
|---------|------------------|---------|
| yo | me | myself |
| tú | te | yourself |
| él/ella/usted | se | himself/herself/yourself |
| nosotros/as | nos | ourselves |
| vosotros/as | os | yourselves |
| ellos/ellas/ustedes | se | themselves/yourselves |

**Examples:**
- Me levanto / I get up
- Te lavas / You wash yourself
- Se viste / He/She gets dressed
- Nos preparamos / We prepare ourselves

### C. Direct Object Pronouns

| Object | Pronoun | Meaning |
|--------|---------|---------|
| a mí | me | me |
| a ti | te | you |
| a él | lo | him, it (masc) |
| a ella | la | her, it (fem) |
| a usted | lo/la | you (formal) |
| a nosotros/as | nos | us |
| a vosotros/as | os | you all |
| a ellos | los | them (masc) |
| a ellas | las | them (fem) |
| a ustedes | los/las | you all |

**Examples:**
- Lo veo / I see it/him
- Te quiero / I love you
- La conozco / I know her
- Los compro / I buy them

### D. Indirect Object Pronouns

| Object | Pronoun | Meaning |
|--------|---------|---------|
| a mí | me | to me |
| a ti | te | to you |
| a él/ella/usted | le | to him/her/you |
| a nosotros/as | nos | to us |
| a vosotros/as | os | to you all |
| a ellos/ellas/ustedes | les | to them/you all |

**Examples:**
- Me da el libro / He gives the book to me
- Te escribo / I write to you
- Le digo / I tell him/her
- Nos compran regalos / They buy gifts for us

**Le/Les → Se Rule:**
When used with direct objects, le/les becomes se
- Se lo doy / I give it to him/her
- Se la muestro / I show it to him/her

### E. Possessive Pronouns

| Owner | Masculine Singular | Feminine Singular | Masculine Plural | Feminine Plural |
|-------|-------------------|------------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| yo | el mío | la mía | los míos | las mías |
| tú | el tuyo | la tuya | los tuyos | las tuyas |
| él/ella/usted | el suyo | la suya | los suyos | las suyas |
| nosotros/as | el nuestro | la nuestra | los nuestros | las nuestras |
| vosotros/as | el vuestro | la vuestra | los vuestros | las vuestras |
| ellos/ellas/ustedes | el suyo | la suya | los suyos | las suyas |

**Examples:**
- Este libro es el mío / This book is mine
- Esa casa es la tuya / That house is yours

### F. Demonstrative Pronouns

| Distance | Masculine Singular | Feminine Singular | Neuter | Masculine Plural | Feminine Plural |
|----------|-------------------|------------------|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Near | este / this | esta / this | esto / this | estos / these | estas / these |
| Middle | ese / that | esa / that | eso / that | esos / those | esas / those |
| Far | aquel / that | aquella / that | aquello / that | aquellos / those | aquellas / those |

**Neuter Demonstrative Pronouns:**
- esto / this (thing)
- eso / that (thing)
- aquello / that (thing) over there

**Examples:**
- ¿Qué es esto? / What is this?
- Eso es verdad / That's true

## 2. Conjunctions

### A. Coordinating Conjunctions

1. **y** (e) / and
   - Juan y María / Juan and María
   - *Note: e before i/hi words*
   - padre e hijo / father and son

2. **o** (u) / or
   - café o té / coffee or tea
   - *Note: u before o/ho words*
   - siete u ocho / seven or eight

3. **pero** / but
   - Es caro pero bueno / It's expensive but good

4. **sino** / but rather
   - No es rojo sino azul / It's not red but blue

5. **ni** / neither, nor
   - No tengo tiempo ni dinero / I have neither time nor money

### B. Cause/Reason Conjunctions

6. **porque** / because
   - Estudio porque quiero aprender / I study because I want to learn

7. **como** / since, as
   - Como llueve, me quedo / Since it's raining, I stay

8. **pues** / well, since
   - No puedo ir, pues estoy ocupado / I can't go, since I'm busy

9. **ya que** / since
   - Ya que estás aquí, ayúdame / Since you're here, help me

10. **puesto que** / since
    - Puesto que no hay tiempo, empecemos / Since there's no time, let's start

### C. Consequence Conjunctions

11. **así que** / so, therefore
    - Estoy cansado, así que voy a dormir / I'm tired, so I'll sleep

12. **por lo tanto** / therefore
    - No hay evidencia, por lo tanto es inocente / There's no evidence, therefore he's innocent

13. **por eso** / that's why
    - Llueve, por eso llevo paraguas / It's raining, that's why I carry an umbrella

14. **entonces** / then, so
    - ¿No vienes? Entonces voy solo / You're not coming? Then I'll go alone

### D. Purpose Conjunctions

15. **para que** / so that (+ subjunctive)
    - Te lo digo para que lo sepas / I tell you so you know

16. **a fin de que** / in order that (+ subjunctive)
    - Trabajo a fin de que mi familia viva bien / I work so my family lives well

### E. Conditional Conjunctions

17. **si** / if
    - Si llueve, no voy / If it rains, I won't go

18. **a menos que** / unless (+ subjunctive)
    - Voy a menos que llueva / I'll go unless it rains

19. **con tal de que** / provided that (+ subjunctive)
    - Te ayudo con tal de que me pagues / I'll help you if you pay me

20. **siempre que** / as long as
    - Siempre que estudies, aprobarás / As long as you study, you'll pass

### F. Concessive Conjunctions

21. **aunque** / although
    - Aunque llueve, voy / Although it's raining, I'm going

22. **a pesar de que** / despite
    - A pesar de que es difícil, lo intento / Despite it being difficult, I try

23. **sin embargo** / however
    - Es difícil, sin embargo lo haré / It's difficult, however I'll do it

24. **no obstante** / nevertheless
    - Estudió mucho, no obstante reprobó / He studied a lot, nevertheless he failed

### G. Temporal Conjunctions

25. **cuando** / when
    - Cuando llegues, llámame / When you arrive, call me

26. **mientras** / while
    - Mientras estudias, yo cocino / While you study, I'll cook

27. **antes de que** / before (+ subjunctive)
    - Llámame antes de que salgas / Call me before you leave

28. **después de que** / after
    - Llámame después de que llegues / Call me after you arrive

29. **hasta que** / until
    - Espero hasta que vengas / I'll wait until you come

30. **desde que** / since (time)
    - Desde que llegué, he estado ocupado / Since I arrived, I've been busy

31. **en cuanto** / as soon as
    - En cuanto llegue, te llamo / As soon as I arrive, I'll call you

32. **tan pronto como** / as soon as
    - Tan pronto como termine, salgo / As soon as I finish, I'm leaving

### H. Comparative Conjunctions

33. **como** / as, like
    - Es como su padre / He's like his father

34. **tan... como** / as... as
    - Es tan alto como su hermano / He's as tall as his brother

35. **más... que** / more... than
    - Es más alto que yo / He's taller than me

36. **menos... que** / less... than
    - Es menos caro que ese / It's less expensive than that one

### I. Explanatory/Additive Conjunctions

37. **es decir** / that is to say
    - Viene mañana, es decir, el lunes / He's coming tomorrow, that is, Monday

38. **además** / besides, furthermore
    - Es caro. Además, no me gusta / It's expensive. Besides, I don't like it

39. **también** / also
    - Hablo español y también inglés / I speak Spanish and also English

40. **tampoco** / neither, either
    - No como carne y tampoco pescado / I don't eat meat and I don't eat fish either

## 3. Prepositions

### A. Basic Prepositions

1. **a** / to, at
   - Voy a la escuela / I go to school
   - a las tres / at three

2. **de** / of, from
   - el libro de Juan / Juan's book
   - Soy de Corea / I'm from Korea

3. **en** / in, on, at
   - Estoy en casa / I'm at home
   - en la mesa / on the table
   - en enero / in January

4. **con** / with
   - Voy con mi amigo / I go with my friend

5. **sin** / without
   - café sin azúcar / coffee without sugar

6. **para** / for, in order to
   - Es para ti / It's for you
   - Estudio para aprender / I study to learn

7. **por** / for, by, through
   - por la mañana / in the morning
   - por favor / please
   - Gracias por todo / Thanks for everything

8. **sobre** / on, about
   - el libro está sobre la mesa / the book is on the table
   - hablamos sobre política / we talk about politics

9. **bajo** / under
   - bajo la mesa / under the table

10. **entre** / between, among
    - entre tú y yo / between you and me

11. **hacia** / toward
    - Voy hacia el norte / I go toward the north

12. **hasta** / until, up to
    - hasta mañana / until tomorrow
    - hasta las 5 / up to 5

13. **desde** / from, since
    - desde aquí / from here
    - desde ayer / since yesterday

14. **durante** / during
    - durante el verano / during summer

15. **tras** / after, behind
    - tras la puerta / behind the door

16. **contra** / against
    - contra la pared / against the wall

17. **según** / according to
    - según él / according to him

### B. Prepositional Phrases

18. **delante de** / in front of
    - delante de la casa / in front of the house

19. **detrás de** / behind
    - detrás de ti / behind you

20. **encima de** / on top of
    - encima de la mesa / on top of the table

21. **debajo de** / under
    - debajo de la cama / under the bed

22. **al lado de** / next to
    - al lado de la escuela / next to the school

23. **cerca de** / near
    - cerca de aquí / near here

24. **lejos de** / far from
    - lejos de la ciudad / far from the city

    - dentro de la caja / inside the box

    - fuera de la casa / outside the house

### F. Formation of -mente Adverbs

**Basic Rule:**
Feminine form of adjective + -mente = adverb

| Adjective (masculine) | Adjective (feminine) | Adverb | Meaning |
|---------------------|---------------------|--------|---------|
| lento | lenta | lentamente | slowly |
| rápido | rápida | rápidamente | quickly |
| tranquilo | tranquila | tranquilamente | quietly |
| silencioso | silenciosa | silenciosamente | silently |
| cuidadoso | cuidadosa | cuidadosamente | carefully |
| perfecto | perfecta | perfectamente | perfectly |
| completo | completa | completamente | completely |
| absoluto | absoluta | absolutamente | absolutely |
| total | total | totalmente | totally |
| general | general | generalmente | generally |
| normal | normal | normalmente | normally |
| especial | especial | especialmente | especially |
| principal | principal | principalmente | mainly |
| final | final | finalmente | finally |
| real | real | realmente | really |

**Adjectives ending in -e/-consonant:**
Add -mente directly
- fácil → fácilmente / easily
- difícil → difícilmente / with difficulty
- frecuente → frecuentemente / frequently
- probable → probablemente / probably
- posible → posiblemente / possibly
- horrible → horriblemente / horribly
- terrible → terriblemente / terribly
- simple → simplemente / simply
- feliz → felizmente / happily

**Two consecutive -mente adverbs:**
First adverb uses adjective form only, second gets -mente
- Habla clara y lentamente / He speaks clearly and slowly
- Lo hizo rápida y eficientemente / She did it quickly and efficiently

### I. Adverb Position Rules

**1. General rule: After verb**
- Habla español bien / He speaks Spanish well
- Corre rápidamente / He runs quickly

**2. For emphasis: Beginning of sentence**
- Nunca he visto eso / I have never seen that
- Siempre llega tarde / He always arrives late

**3. Compound tenses: Between haber and past participle**
- He comido bien / I have eaten well
- Ya he terminado / I have already finished

**4. Frequency adverbs: Between subject and verb**
- Siempre estudia / He always studies
- Nunca trabaja / He never works

**5. Modifying adjective/adverb: Right before**
- muy bien / very well
- bastante rápido / quite fast
- demasiado tarde / too late

## 5. Interrogatives

## Study Tips

1. **Pronoun Order**: Memorize the order: reflexive → indirect → direct
2. **Conjunctions by Category**: Learn by usage: time, cause, condition, etc.
3. **Preposition Usage**: Memorize complete common expressions
4. **Adverb Derivation**: Master adjective + mente rule
5. **Interrogative Practice**: Create questions daily
6. **Number Practice**: Say phone numbers, prices, etc. in Spanish
7. **Learn in Sentences**: Learn naturally within sentences rather than in isolation
8. **Interjection Practice**: Practice emotional expressions with proper intonation
9. **Discourse Marker Usage**: Practice natural connections in writing and speaking
