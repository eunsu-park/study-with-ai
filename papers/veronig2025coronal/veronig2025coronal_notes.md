# 86. Coronal Dimmings and What They Tell Us About Solar and Stellar Coronal Mass Ejections / 코로나 디밍과 태양·항성 CME

---

## 1. Core Contribution / 핵심 기여

**English**:
Veronig et al. (2025) present the first comprehensive Living Review of coronal dimmings — localized, impulsive decreases in coronal EUV and soft X-ray emission that accompany the lift-off and early expansion of a coronal mass ejection (CME). Building on nearly three decades of observational work beginning with SOHO/EIT and Yohkoh/SXT in the late 1990s, the review synthesizes dimming physics across three layers: (1) **observational phenomenology** — dimming main phase (impulsive intensity drop of ~80-90%, area growth $dA/dt$, typical areas $A \sim 10^{18}-10^{20}\,\mathrm{cm^2}$, lifetime ~3-12 hr), recovery phase, and the rare but diagnostically valuable pre-eruption dimmings; (2) a **new physics-driven categorization** that moves beyond the traditional "core/secondary" scheme to classify dimmings by the underlying magnetic flux system — flux-rope dimmings (stationary, shrinking, moving), strapping-flux dimmings, exterior dimmings, open-flux dimmings, and complex events; and (3) the **extension from Sun to stars**, where sun-as-a-star SDO/EVE data establish dimmings as a robust proxy for CMEs, and stellar observations with XMM-Newton (Proxima Centauri, AB Dor, AU Mic), HST/COS (ε Eridani), and TESS+ground-based spectroscopy (EK Dra) yield the first viable observational handle on stellar CMEs.

**한국어**:
Veronig 외(2025)는 코로나 디밍(coronal dimming)에 대한 최초의 포괄적 Living Review를 제공한다. 코로나 디밍은 CME의 이륙 및 초기 팽창 단계에서 EUV 및 soft X-ray 복사가 국소적·급격하게 감소하는 현상으로, 1990년대 후반 SOHO/EIT와 Yohkoh/SXT에서 발견된 이후 30년간의 관측·이론 성과가 이 리뷰에서 집약된다. 논문은 세 층위로 종합된다. (1) **관측 현상학** — dimming 주요 단계(~80-90% 강도 급감, 면적 성장률 $dA/dt$, 전형적 면적 $A \sim 10^{18}-10^{20}\,\mathrm{cm^2}$, 수명 3-12시간), 회복 단계, 드물지만 진단적으로 귀중한 eruption 이전 dimming. (2) **자속계(flux system) 기반 새로운 물리 분류** — 기존의 "core/secondary" 분류를 넘어 flux-rope dimming (stationary·shrinking·moving), strapping-flux dimming, exterior dimming, open-flux dimming, complex event로 분류. (3) **태양에서 항성으로의 확장** — SDO/EVE Sun-as-a-Star 관측으로 dimming이 CME의 견고한 프록시임이 증명되었고, Proxima Centauri, AB Dor, AU Mic (XMM-Newton), ε Eridani (HST/COS), EK Dra (지상 분광) 항성 관측으로 최초의 항성 CME 관측 수단을 확보하였다.

## 2. Reading Notes / 읽기 노트

### Part I: Introduction and History / 서론과 역사 (Sect. 1-2, pp. 3-7)

**English**:
The review opens by establishing coronal dimmings as one of the most important on-disk signatures of CMEs. Dimmings evolve during the *entire* life cycle of a CME — from quasi-steady pre-eruption expansion, through impulsive lift-off and main-phase mass evacuation, to the gradual post-event recovery of the corona. This makes them unique diagnostics that bridge CME triggering, magnetic connectivity, mass loss, and coronal replenishment.

Historically, the conceptual seed goes back to Waldmeier (1956), who identified "koronale Löcher" (coronal holes) as semi-permanent low-density regions. Skylab X-ray imaging in 1973 confirmed the nature of coronal holes and revealed "transient coronal holes" (Rust 1983), conceptually the same as what we now call coronal dimmings. Streamer blowouts (Sheeley et al. 1982) seen synoptically in K-coronagraph data provided early coronagraphic evidence of CME-driven mass removal. Soft X-ray imaging by Yohkoh/SXT (from 1991) and EUV full-disk imaging by SOHO/EIT (from 1995) enabled the first systematic studies: Hudson et al. (1996) noted the X-ray dimmings, Sterling and Hudson (1997) tied them to CME mass loss and flux-rope footprints, and Thompson et al. (1998) documented the iconic twin dimming of SOL1997-05-12. The advent of SDO (2010) with AIA (12-s cadence, 6 EUV channels) and EVE (disk-integrated EUV spectra) transformed the field.

**한국어**:
리뷰는 코로나 디밍을 CME의 가장 중요한 원반상 특징 중 하나로 자리매김한다. Dimming은 CME의 **전 생애주기**에 걸쳐 진화한다 — 사전 eruption의 준정상 팽창부터 충격적 이륙과 주요 단계의 질량 배출, 이후 긴 회복 단계까지. 이로 인해 dimming은 CME 촉발, 자기 연결성, 질량 손실, 코로나 재충전을 모두 아우르는 독특한 진단 도구가 된다.

역사적 뿌리는 Waldmeier (1956)의 "koronale Löcher"(coronal holes) 발견이다. 1973년 Skylab X-ray 영상으로 coronal hole의 본질이 확인되고 "transient coronal hole"(Rust 1983) 개념이 도입되었는데, 이것이 현재의 coronal dimming과 개념적으로 같다. Sheeley et al. (1982)의 streamer blowout 관측, Yohkoh/SXT(1991-)의 soft X-ray 이미징, SOHO/EIT(1995-)의 EUV 전원반 이미징이 결합되어 체계적 연구가 시작되었다: Hudson et al. (1996)은 X-ray dimming을 보고, Sterling and Hudson (1997)은 이를 CME 질량 손실과 flux rope footprint로 해석, Thompson et al. (1998)은 SOL1997-05-12 쌍둥이 dimming(twin dimming)의 고전적 사례를 기록했다. SDO 발사(2010) — AIA(12초 카덴스, 6 EUV 채널)와 EVE(원반 적분 EUV 분광) — 는 이 분야를 변혁시켰다.

### Part II: Observations / 관측 (Sect. 3, pp. 7-36)

#### 3.1 Overview / 개요

**English**:
The canonical dimming evolution (Fig. 3 of the paper) has three phases:
- **Pre-eruption dimming** (rare): slow, gradual emission drop starting 10 min - few hours before flare onset. Signatures of quasi-steady flux rope expansion (Qiu & Cheng 2017; Wang et al. 2019).
- **Main phase**: impulsive onset coincident with CME lift-off and flare impulsive phase. Intensity drops 80-90% in 10-30 min. Area grows at rate $dA/dt$.
- **Recovery phase**: gradual intensity recovery over ~3-12 hr (mean 7.4 hr; Reinard & Biesecker 2008), either by area shrinkage or brightness increase inside the dimming.

Dimmings are traditionally classified morphologically as **core (twin) dimmings** — deep (up to 90% drop), compact, rooted at opposite magnetic polarities, marking flux-rope footpoints — and **secondary dimmings** — broader, shallower, less impulsive, mapping the expansion of overlying field.

**한국어**:
논문의 Fig. 3에 제시된 정형적 dimming 진화는 세 단계로 이루어진다:
- **Pre-eruption dimming** (드물게 관측): 플레어 시작 10분~수 시간 전에 시작하는 완만한 강도 감소. 준정상 flux rope 팽창의 징후 (Qiu & Cheng 2017; Wang et al. 2019).
- **Main phase**: CME 이륙 및 플레어 충격기와 동시에 충격적으로 시작. 10-30분 내 강도 80-90% 급감. 면적은 $dA/dt$ 비율로 성장.
- **Recovery phase**: 3-12시간에 걸친 점진적 회복(평균 7.4시간; Reinard & Biesecker 2008). 면적 축소 또는 내부 밝기 증가를 통해.

전통적으로 형태학적으로 **core(twin) dimming** — 깊고(최대 90%) 좁으며 반대 극성 자기장에 뿌리를 두고 flux rope footpoint를 표시 — 과 **secondary dimming** — 넓고 얕으며 덜 충격적이고, 상부 자기장의 팽창을 반영 — 으로 구분한다.

#### 3.2 Detection Methods / 검출 방법

**English**:
Core detection techniques are based on difference or ratio imaging:
- **Base-difference (BD)**: $\Delta I = I(t) - I(t_0)$, where $t_0$ is pre-event. Emphasizes transient changes.
- **Running-difference (RD)**: $\Delta I = I(t) - I(t-\Delta t)$. Highlights fast changes but misses gradual growth.
- **Logarithmic base-ratio**: $\log[I(t)/I(t_0)]$, used by Dissauer et al. (2018a). Captures both deep core and shallow secondary dimmings.
- **Persistence maps** (Thompson & Young 2016): each pixel's minimum intensity over event duration. Reveals full spatial extent excluding flare loops.
- Automated algorithms: NEMO (Podladchikova & Berghmans 2005; 2012), SolarDemon (Kraaikamp & Verbeeck 2015, real-time), Dissauer et al. (2018a) log-base-ratio thresholding. SolarDemon combines RD + PRD masks (Fig. 5).

**한국어**:
핵심 검출 기법은 차분/비율 영상에 기초한다:
- **Base-difference (BD)**: $\Delta I = I(t) - I(t_0)$ — 사전 이벤트 영상 대비 차. 일시적 변화 강조.
- **Running-difference (RD)**: $\Delta I = I(t) - I(t-\Delta t)$ — 빠른 변화 강조하나 점진적 성장은 놓침.
- **Logarithmic base-ratio**: $\log[I(t)/I(t_0)]$ (Dissauer et al. 2018a). 깊은 core와 얕은 secondary 모두 포착.
- **Persistence map** (Thompson & Young 2016): 사건 기간 동안 각 픽셀의 최소 강도. 플레어 loop 제외한 전체 공간 범위 표시.
- 자동화 알고리즘: NEMO (Podladchikova & Berghmans 2005, 2012), SolarDemon (Kraaikamp & Verbeeck 2015, 실시간), Dissauer et al. (2018a) log-base-ratio 임계치. SolarDemon은 RD + PRD 마스크 결합(Fig. 5).

#### 3.3 Main Dimming Phase / 주요 디밍 단계

**3.3.1 Global Properties**. **English**:
Dimmings appear in all SDO/AIA coronal channels (171, 193, 211 Å most prominent; also 335 Å, weakly in 94/131 Å), confirming multi-thermal nature spanning ~0.7-10 MK. Intensity drops of 80-90% occur over ~10 min; dimmings persist at reduced levels for >10 hr in core regions (Fig. 7, Vanninathan et al. 2018). Dissauer et al. (2018b) studied 62 events and derived log-normal distributions (Fig. 10): dimming area $A \sim 10^9$-$10^{11}\,\mathrm{km^2}$ ($\mu_{\log A} = 5.37, \sigma = 0.96$), unsigned magnetic flux $\Phi \sim 10^{19}$-$10^{22}\,\mathrm{Mx}$ ($\mu_{\log \Phi} = 2.86, \sigma = 1.11$), mean flux density 20-300 G ($\mu = 4.11, \sigma = 0.61$). Total dimming duration (Reinard & Biesecker 2008): mean $8 \pm 0.4$ hr, mostly 3-12 hr. Veronig et al. (2021) EVE: $7.1 \pm 3.1$ hr.

**3.3.1 전역적 성질**. **한국어**:
Dimming은 모든 SDO/AIA 코로나 채널(171, 193, 211 Å에서 가장 두드러짐; 335 Å, 94/131 Å에서는 약하게)에 나타나 ~0.7-10 MK 범위의 다온도 성질을 확인시킨다. 강도는 ~10분 만에 80-90% 급감하고, core 영역에서는 10시간 이상 감소 상태가 지속된다(Fig. 7, Vanninathan et al. 2018). Dissauer et al. (2018b)의 62개 이벤트 분석(Fig. 10)은 로그정규 분포를 밝혔다: 면적 $A \sim 10^9$-$10^{11}\,\mathrm{km^2}$ ($\mu_{\log A} = 5.37$), 자속 $\Phi \sim 10^{19}$-$10^{22}\,\mathrm{Mx}$, 평균 자속 밀도 20-300 G. 총 dimming 지속 시간(Reinard & Biesecker 2008): 평균 $8 \pm 0.4$시간, 대부분 3-12시간. Veronig et al. (2021) EVE: $7.1 \pm 3.1$시간.

**3.3.2 Outflows in Dimming Regions**. **English**:
EIS spectroscopy reveals persistent blue shifts (upflows) in dimming regions. Harra & Sterling (2001) with SOHO/CDS: ~30 km/s in Fe XVI, ~100 km/s in O V. Harra et al. (2007), Tian et al. (2012) with Hinode/EIS: double-component Gaussian fits show stationary + fast-outflow (50-150 km/s) components. Veronig et al. (2019): outflow densities up to $\log(N_e/\mathrm{cm^{-3}}) \approx 9$. These outflows likely contribute to the solar wind and are transient analogs of coronal-hole fast-wind streams.

**3.3.2 디밍 영역의 유출류**. **한국어**:
EIS 분광 관측은 dimming 영역에서 지속적 청색 이동(상향류)을 보인다. Harra & Sterling (2001)의 SOHO/CDS: Fe XVI에서 ~30 km/s, O V에서 ~100 km/s. Harra et al. (2007), Tian et al. (2012)의 Hinode/EIS: 이중 가우시안 적합 결과 정상 + 고속 유출(50-150 km/s) 성분. Veronig et al. (2019): 유출류 밀도 $\log(N_e/\mathrm{cm^{-3}}) \approx 9$까지. 이 유출류는 태양풍에 기여하며, coronal-hole 고속풍의 일시적 상사물로 해석된다.

**3.3.3 Plasma Parameters**. **English**:
DEM analysis (Tian et al. 2012; Vanninathan et al. 2018): dimming caused by density depletion, not temperature change. Fig. 15 DEM curves show EM reduction at $\log(T/K) = 6.0$-$6.3$ but ~constant peak temperature. Fig. 16: total EM drops ~90%, density by >60%, temperature by <10% within ~10 min of CME lift-off. Density diagnostics from Fe XII 186.88/195.12 Å and Fe XIII 203.82/202.04 Å line pairs: $\log(N_e/\mathrm{cm^{-3}})$ drops from 8.89 to 8.67.

**3.3.3 플라즈마 파라미터**. **한국어**:
DEM 분석(Tian et al. 2012; Vanninathan et al. 2018): dimming은 밀도 감소에 기인, 온도 변화 아님. Fig. 15의 DEM 곡선은 $\log(T/K) = 6.0$-$6.3$에서 EM 감소하나 피크 온도는 거의 일정. Fig. 16: 총 EM ~90%, 밀도 >60% 감소, 온도는 <10% 감소 (CME 이륙 ~10분 내). Fe XII, Fe XIII 진단선: $\log(N_e/\mathrm{cm^{-3}})$이 8.89 → 8.67.

#### 3.4 Recovery Phase / 회복 단계

**English**:
Dimming recovers by (a) area shrinkage (Kahler & Hudson 2001 Yohkoh/SXT), (b) brightness increase inside the region (Attrill et al. 2008 SOHO/EIT), or (c) both. Mean recovery timescale ~4.8 ± 0.3 hr (Reinard & Biesecker 2008). Two-step recovery in ~25% of events. Core dimming brightness can remain at reduced level for >10 hr, indicating the erupted flux rope is still magnetically connected to the Sun (evidence: bi-directional electron beams in ICME).

**한국어**:
Dimming 회복은 (a) 면적 축소 (Kahler & Hudson 2001 Yohkoh/SXT), (b) 영역 내부 밝기 증가 (Attrill et al. 2008 SOHO/EIT), 또는 (c) 두 가지 조합으로 이루어진다. 평균 회복 시간 ~4.8 ± 0.3 시간 (Reinard & Biesecker 2008). 약 25% 이벤트에서 2단계 회복. Core dimming은 10시간 이상 감소 상태 유지 → eruption된 flux rope이 여전히 태양에 자기적으로 연결됨 증거(ICME 내 양방향 전자 빔).

#### 3.5 Pre-eruption Dimmings / Eruption 이전 디밍

**English**:
Gopalswamy et al. (1999, 2006): weak coronal dimming ~2 hr before SOL1998-04-27 flare. Qiu & Cheng (2017), Wang et al. (2019): twin pre-eruption dimmings up to 5 hr before M1.9 flare (SOL2012-06-14). 304 Å dimmings trace flux rope footprints. 20% intensity drop. Expansion velocity estimates ~few km/s (Fig. 20). Magnetic twist $\langle\tau\rangle \sim 2$ prior to eruption (Wang et al. 2019 from 304 Å-derived flux rope boundaries). Analytical estimates (Eq. 3-4 of paper): for isothermal expansion at ~few km/s, observable dimming in 211 Å at $L/L_0 = 1.5$ with initial $T_0 = 1.5$ MK.

**한국어**:
Gopalswamy et al. (1999, 2006): SOL1998-04-27 플레어 ~2시간 전 약한 dimming. Qiu & Cheng (2017), Wang et al. (2019): SOL2012-06-14 M1.9 플레어 5시간 전 쌍둥이 사전 dimming. 304 Å dimming이 flux rope footpoint를 추적. 강도 20% 감소. 팽창 속도 ~몇 km/s (Fig. 20). Wang et al. (2019)은 304 Å dimming 경계로부터 eruption 전 자기 비틀림 $\langle\tau\rangle \sim 2$ 추정. 해석적 추정(식 3-4): 등온 팽창 ~몇 km/s에서 $T_0 = 1.5$ MK 초기 조건으로 $L/L_0 = 1.5$일 때 211 Å에서 관측 가능한 dimming 예측.

### Part III: Relations to CMEs, Flares, ICMEs / CME·플레어·ICME와의 관계 (Sect. 4, pp. 36-49)

**English**:
- **Dimming-CME association rate**: 84-97% of CMEs have associated dimmings (Dissauer 2018b: 87% of 71 halo CMEs; Veronig 2021: 84% of M5+ eruptive flares in EVE; 97% in spatially resolved AIA). Dimming-associated CMEs are faster: mean 964 km/s vs. 498 km/s for non-dimming (Reinard & Biesecker 2009).
- **Dimming-CME mass**: Mass loss $M = \delta N \cdot S \cdot L \cdot m_p$ from density × area × depth. Observed dimming mass is 20-60% of total CME mass (Jin 2009, Tian 2012 from EIS spectra), up to 50% of 4.2 × 10^15 g total CME mass (Zhukov & Auchère 2004). Correlations $r \sim 0.6$-$0.85$ between dimming area/brightness and CME mass (Dissauer 2019; Chikunova 2020 off-limb).
- **Dimming-CME kinematics**: Strong correlation with CME max velocity ($r = 0.67$-$0.68$; Fig. 26). Dimming impulsive growth coincides with CME acceleration phase (<40 min for 50% of events).
- **Dimming-flare**: Static dimming parameters correlate with SXR fluence ($r \sim 0.7$); dynamic parameters (max brightness change rate $|dI_{cu,diff}/dt|$) correlate with GOES peak flux. Flare reconnection flux $\Phi_{ribbon} \approx \Phi_{dimming}$ (Qiu et al. 2007).
- **Timing**: $|\Delta t| < 5$ min for flare-dimming onset in 50% of events; mean $-1.5 \pm 9.9$ min (CME onset ~4 min after dimming onset).
- **Dimming-ICME**: Dimming flux $\Phi_d$ comparable to ICME magnetic cloud toroidal flux $\Phi_t$ within a factor ~2 (Webb 2000, Qiu 2007, Temmer 2017). $\Phi_d \lesssim \Phi_p$ (poloidal flux) in all 9 events of Qiu 2007.

**한국어**:
- **Dimming-CME 연관율**: CME의 84-97%가 dimming을 동반 (Dissauer 2018b: 71 halo CME의 87%; Veronig 2021: EVE에서 M5+ 플레어의 84%; AIA 공간분해에서 97%). Dimming 동반 CME는 더 빠름: 평균 964 km/s vs. 498 km/s (Reinard & Biesecker 2009).
- **Dimming-CME 질량**: 질량 손실 $M = \delta N \cdot S \cdot L \cdot m_p$. 관측된 dimming 질량은 전체 CME 질량의 20-60% (Jin 2009, Tian 2012 EIS), 최대 50% (Zhukov & Auchère 2004; $4.2 \times 10^{15}$ g CME에서). Dimming 면적/밝기와 CME 질량 간 상관계수 $r \sim 0.6$-$0.85$ (Dissauer 2019; Chikunova 2020 off-limb).
- **Dimming-CME 운동학**: CME 최대 속도와 강한 상관 ($r = 0.67$-$0.68$; Fig. 26). Dimming 충격 성장이 CME 가속기와 일치 (50% 이벤트에서 <40분).
- **Dimming-플레어**: 정적 dimming 파라미터와 SXR fluence 상관 $r \sim 0.7$; 동적 파라미터(최대 밝기 변화율)와 GOES 피크 flux 상관. 플레어 재결합 flux $\Phi_{ribbon} \approx \Phi_{dimming}$.
- **타이밍**: 플레어-dimming onset 시간차 $|\Delta t| < 5$분 (50% 이벤트); 평균 $-1.5 \pm 9.9$분 (CME onset은 dimming 4분 후).
- **Dimming-ICME**: Dimming flux $\Phi_d$는 ICME magnetic cloud toroidal flux $\Phi_t$와 2배 이내로 일치 (Webb 2000, Qiu 2007, Temmer 2017).

### Part IV: New Categorization / 새로운 분류체계 (Sect. 5, pp. 49-78)

**English**:
The review's key conceptual contribution. Traditional core/secondary classification is observational but lacks physical clarity. The authors propose categorization based on the **magnetic flux system** involved:

1. **Flux-rope dimmings** — formed at MFR footpoints. Three subtypes:
   - *Stationary* (5.1.1): footpoints fixed, flux rope straps at its feet.
   - *Shrinking* (5.1.2): direct interaction of erupting flux with itself, progressive footprint shrinkage.
2. **Strapping-flux dimmings** (5.2) — from lifting of overlying ambient flux that strapped the MFR down.
3. **Moving flux-rope dimmings** (5.3) — MFR leg migrates during eruption.
4. **Exterior dimmings** (5.4) — at the periphery, involving closed flux exterior to the immediate source region.
5. **Open-flux dimmings** (5.5) — rooted in pre-existing open field; reconnected into newly opened field.
6. **Complex dimming events** (5.6) — combined strapping/exterior, circular-ribbon events, breakout configurations, sympathetic eruptions.

Reconnection plays a central role: all categories except simple flux-rope lift involve reconnection of some kind (between erupting flux and surrounding closed or open flux). The categorization directly connects dimming observations to MHD models.

**한국어**:
리뷰의 핵심 개념적 기여. 기존 core/secondary 분류는 관측적이지만 물리적 명료성 부족. 저자들은 **관여한 자속계**를 기준으로 분류 제안:

1. **Flux-rope dimming** — MFR 발끝(footpoint)에서 형성. 두 하위 유형:
   - *Stationary* (5.1.1): 발끝 고정, flux rope가 발끝에서만 묶임.
   - *Shrinking* (5.1.2): eruption flux의 자기 상호작용, 발끝 점진적 축소.
2. **Strapping-flux dimming** (5.2) — MFR를 억제하던 상부 주변 flux의 상승으로 형성.
3. **Moving flux-rope dimming** (5.3) — eruption 중 MFR 다리가 이동.
4. **Exterior dimming** (5.4) — 주변부, 즉시 source 영역 외곽의 닫힌 flux 관여.
5. **Open-flux dimming** (5.5) — 기존 open field에 뿌리; 새로 열린 field로 재결합.
6. **복합 dimming 이벤트** (5.6) — strapping/exterior 결합, circular-ribbon 이벤트, breakout 구성, 동조(sympathetic) eruption.

재결합이 중심 역할: 단순 flux-rope 이륙을 제외한 모든 범주가 eruption flux와 주변 닫힌/열린 flux 사이 재결합 수반. 이 분류는 dimming 관측과 MHD 모델을 직접 연결한다.

### Part V: Simulations / 시뮬레이션 (Sect. 6, pp. 78-89)

**English**:
MHD simulations reproduce dimming observations. The CORHEL framework (Downs et al.) and similar thermodynamic MHD models produce synthetic EUV images including dimming signatures. Example 1: eruption of a flux rope in a bipolar active region shows dimming formation at flux-rope footprints and expansion of overlying strapping field → secondary dimming. Example 2: circular-ribbon eruption (breakout-like) shows dimmings at null-point reconnection regions. Simulations confirm the causal chain: MFR rise → reconnection → density evacuation → dimming.

**한국어**:
MHD 시뮬레이션이 dimming 관측을 재현. CORHEL 프레임워크(Downs 외) 및 유사 열역학 MHD 모델이 dimming 신호를 포함한 합성 EUV 이미지 생성. 예 1: 쌍극 활동 영역에서 flux rope eruption → flux-rope 발끝 dimming 형성, 상부 strapping field 팽창 → secondary dimming. 예 2: circular-ribbon eruption (breakout 유형) → null-point 재결합 영역 dimming. 시뮬레이션은 인과 사슬을 확인: MFR 상승 → 재결합 → 밀도 배출 → dimming.

### Part VI: From Sun to Stars / 태양에서 항성으로 (Sect. 7, pp. 89-106)

**English**:
The pivot from solar to stellar CMEs. The fundamental challenge: stellar CMEs are virtually undetectable by direct Thomson-scattering coronagraphy (angular size ~1 milli-arcsec for an M dwarf at 5 pc), and Type II radio bursts have yielded no convincing stellar detections. Alternative proxies are required.

**7.2 Sun-as-a-Star observations**:
SDO/EVE disk-integrated EUV spectra (Mason 2014, 2016; Harra 2016; Veronig 2021) reveal coronal dimmings in Fe IX–XII lines (log T/K = 5.8-6.2) after X-class flares. Temperature-dependent signatures: at high ionization (Fe XXI, Fe XXIV) the flare dominates; at lower ionization dimming is revealed after flare decays. Veronig et al. (2021): 44 large flares (GOES ≥M5), 84% of eruptive flares show dimming in EVE 150-250 Å broadband; false-alarm rate 17%; conditional probability $P(\mathrm{CME|Dim}) = 0.97$. Fig. 56 shows the spectacular X5.1 SOL2012-03-07 dimming, dominant in 170-210 Å range.

**7.3 Post-flare stellar dimmings**:
Veronig et al. (2021) searched XMM-Newton, Chandra, EUVE archives: 21 dimmings on 13 stars. Half on three stars: AB Dor (K0V, rapid rotator), AU Mic (M0V, young), Proxima Cen (M5.5Ve). Fig. 57 shows two Proxima events: 36% and 56% depth, 3 and 4.5 hr duration. Key comparison (Fig. 58):
- **Stellar dimming depths**: 5-55% (mean 22.7%, σ = 13.2%)
- **Solar dimming depths**: 0.5-5% (mean 2.18%, σ = 1.12%)
- **Duration**: stellar 1-10 hr (lower estimates; some truncated by subsequent flares)
- Stellar dimmings are ~10× deeper than solar — consistent with higher magnetic activity and CMEs with larger mass fractions relative to stellar EUV/X-ray output.

Loyd et al. (2022) extended the method to HST/COS FUV on ε Eridani (K2V): 81±5% decrease in Fe XXI (log T = 7.3), 16±8% in Fe XII (log T = 6.2). Estimated CME mass $10^{15.2}$ g. Equations 5-9 of the paper formalize the CME mass estimate:
$$\delta_{max} = F_{CME}/F_{pre}, \quad F = \int_V G(T,n_e) n_e n_H dV, \quad m = \frac{\mu \delta_{max} F_{pre}}{n G(T,n)}$$

Namekata et al. (2024) on EK Draconis (G1.5V young solar-type): TESS + Hα spectroscopy reveals prominence eruption at 510 km/s during superflare; 10% X-ray dimming on the faster of two eruptions — first confirmed CME signature on a solar-type star via this methodology.

**7.4 Simulations of stellar CMEs**:
Alvarado-Gómez et al. (2018, 2019, 2020): global MHD shows that a dipole field of 75 G can fully suppress CMEs up to $3 \times 10^{32}$ erg initial energy (Fig. 53) — explaining the scarcity of stellar CMEs (the "magnetic confinement" problem). Jin et al. (2020): simulates stronger stellar CME (5× solar surface flux density); CME speed ~3000 km/s; coronal dimming signatures shifted to higher temperature lines (Fe XV 284 Å, Fe XVI 335 Å). Lynch et al. (2019): Kappa¹ Ceti Carrington-scale superflare/CME simulation using Zeeman Doppler Imaging magnetogram (Fig. 61) — shows distinct pre-eruption dimming (expansion) + eruption dimming + post-eruption flare arcade signatures in integrated light curves.

**한국어**:
태양에서 항성 CME로의 전환. 근본적 도전: 직접 Thomson scattering coronagraph로는 항성 CME 관측 불가(5 pc의 M 왜성에서 원반 각크기 ~1 마이크로초), Type II 라디오 버스트도 설득력 있는 항성 검출 없음. 대안 프록시가 필요.

**7.2 Sun-as-a-Star 관측**:
SDO/EVE 원반 적분 EUV 분광(Mason 2014, 2016; Harra 2016; Veronig 2021)은 X급 플레어 후 Fe IX–XII 선(log T/K = 5.8-6.2)에서 코로나 dimming 발견. 온도 의존 신호: 고이온(Fe XXI, XXIV)에서는 플레어가 지배, 저이온에서는 플레어 후 dimming이 드러남. Veronig et al. (2021): GOES ≥M5 대규모 플레어 44개, 이륙 플레어의 84%가 EVE 150-250 Å에서 유의한 dimming; 거짓 경보율 17%; 조건부 확률 $P(\mathrm{CME|Dim}) = 0.97$. Fig. 56: X5.1 SOL2012-03-07의 극적인 dimming, 170-210 Å에 집중.

**7.3 플레어 후 항성 dimming**:
Veronig et al. (2021)은 XMM-Newton, Chandra, EUVE 아카이브 검색: 13개 별에서 21개 dimming. 절반이 세 별에서: AB Dor (K0V, 빠른 자전), AU Mic (M0V, 젊음), Proxima Cen (M5.5Ve). Fig. 57: Proxima의 두 사건 — 36%, 56% 깊이, 3, 4.5시간 지속. 핵심 비교(Fig. 58):
- **항성 dimming 깊이**: 5-55% (평균 22.7%, σ = 13.2%)
- **태양 dimming 깊이**: 0.5-5% (평균 2.18%, σ = 1.12%)
- **지속 시간**: 항성 1-10시간 (하한 추정)
- 항성 dimming은 태양보다 **~10배 깊음** — 높은 자기활동 및 항성 EUV/X-ray 출력 대비 CME 질량 비중이 큼과 일치.

Loyd et al. (2022)는 HST/COS FUV로 ε Eridani (K2V) 관측: Fe XXI에서 81±5% 감소, Fe XII에서 16±8%. CME 질량 $10^{15.2}$ g 추정. 논문 식 5-9가 공식화:
$$\delta_{max} = F_{CME}/F_{pre}, \quad F = \int_V G(T,n_e) n_e n_H dV, \quad m = \frac{\mu \delta_{max} F_{pre}}{n G(T,n)}$$

Namekata et al. (2024)는 EK Draconis(G1.5V 젊은 태양형 별) 관측: TESS + Hα 분광으로 슈퍼플레어 중 510 km/s 필라먼트 eruption, 더 빠른 eruption에 10% X-ray dimming 동반 — 이 방법론으로 태양형 별에서 최초 확인된 CME 신호.

**7.4 항성 CME 시뮬레이션**:
Alvarado-Gómez et al. (2018, 2019, 2020): 전역 MHD로 75 G 쌍극 field가 최대 $3 \times 10^{32}$ erg 초기 에너지 CME 완전 억제 가능(Fig. 53) — 항성 CME 희소성의 "자기 억제" 문제 설명. Jin et al. (2020): 더 강한 항성 CME 모사(5배 태양 표면 flux 밀도); CME 속도 ~3000 km/s; dimming 신호가 고온선(Fe XV 284 Å, Fe XVI 335 Å)으로 이동. Lynch et al. (2019): Zeeman Doppler Imaging 자기장도를 이용한 Kappa¹ Ceti Carrington-급 슈퍼플레어/CME 모사(Fig. 61) — eruption 전 dimming(팽창) + eruption dimming + 사후 플레어 아케이드 신호를 적분 광도곡선에 뚜렷이 보임.

## 3. Key Takeaways / 핵심 시사점

1. **Dimmings are density-driven, not thermal** / **디밍은 밀도 감소에서 비롯되며 열적 변화가 아니다** — Multi-wavelength observations (Thompson 1998, Vanninathan 2018, Tian 2012) and DEM analysis confirm that dimming is caused by plasma evacuation (density drops 50-70%), not by heating/cooling. Peak temperature stays nearly constant. This validates mass-loss interpretations and justifies mass estimates from $M = \delta N \cdot S \cdot L \cdot m_p$.
   다파장 관측과 DEM 분석은 dimming이 플라즈마 배출(밀도 50-70% 감소)에 기인하며 가열/냉각이 아님을 확인. 피크 온도 거의 일정. 이로써 $M = \delta N \cdot S \cdot L \cdot m_p$ 질량 추정이 정당화됨.

2. **Dimmings trace flux rope footprints** / **디밍은 플럭스 로프 발끝을 추적한다** — Twin core dimmings at opposite polarities mark MFR footpoints (Sterling & Hudson 1997). Magnetic flux $\Phi_d$ within dimmings matches toroidal flux of in-situ ICME within factor 2 (Qiu 2007, Temmer 2017). This makes dimmings a rare tool to infer MFR magnetic properties without in-situ measurement.
   반대 극성의 쌍둥이 core dimming은 MFR 발끝을 표시. Dimming 자속 $\Phi_d$는 in-situ ICME의 toroidal 자속과 2배 이내 일치. In-situ 측정 없이 MFR 자기 특성을 추론할 수 있는 드문 도구.

3. **Dimming dynamics correlate with CME acceleration** / **디밍 동역학은 CME 가속기와 상관된다** — Dynamic parameters ($dA/dt$, $d\Phi/dt$, max brightness change rate) correlate with GOES SXR peak ($r \sim 0.6$-$0.7$) and CME max velocity ($r \sim 0.67$-$0.82$). Static parameters (total area, brightness) correlate with flare fluence ($r \sim 0.7$). Dimmings thus reflect both the reconnection dynamics and the CME energetics.
   동적 파라미터는 GOES SXR 피크 및 CME 최대 속도와 상관($r \sim 0.67$-$0.82$), 정적 파라미터는 플레어 fluence와 상관($r \sim 0.7$). Dimming은 재결합 동역학과 CME 에너지 모두 반영.

4. **New flux-system categorization unifies observations and models** / **새로운 자속계 분류가 관측과 모델을 통합한다** — The authors' physics-driven classification (flux-rope/strapping/exterior/open-flux) replaces the limited core/secondary dichotomy. Each category corresponds to specific reconnection geometries in MHD models and specific observational signatures (morphology, timing, flare ribbon relation). This bridges observation-theory-simulation.
   저자들의 물리 중심 분류(flux-rope/strapping/exterior/open-flux)는 제한적인 core/secondary 이분법을 대체. 각 범주는 MHD 모델의 특정 재결합 기하 및 특정 관측 신호에 대응. 관측-이론-시뮬레이션을 연결.

5. **Pre-eruption dimmings reveal CME triggering** / **Eruption 전 디밍은 CME 촉발을 드러낸다** — Rare but valuable: 304 Å pre-eruption dimmings (Qiu & Cheng 2017, Wang et al. 2019) map MFR footprints before eruption. Enable estimates of MFR twist $\langle\tau\rangle \sim 2$, expansion speed (~few km/s), vertical electric currents — currently the only way to diagnose pre-eruption MFR magnetic structure from Earth-side observations.
   희귀하나 귀중한 데이터: 304 Å eruption 전 dimming이 MFR 발끝 추적. MFR 비틀림 $\langle\tau\rangle \sim 2$, 팽창 속도(~몇 km/s), 수직 전류 추정 가능 — 현재 Earth-side 관측으로 사전 MFR 자기 구조 진단의 유일한 방법.

6. **Stellar CMEs are now observable via dimmings** / **항성 CME는 이제 디밍으로 관측 가능하다** — 21 stellar dimmings detected on 13 stars (AB Dor, AU Mic, Proxima, ε Eri, EK Dra). Depths 5-55%, 10× deeper than solar. CME mass estimates $\sim 10^{15}$ g. This provides the first viable observational handle on stellar CME rates, critical for exoplanet habitability assessment.
   13개 별에서 21개 항성 dimming 검출 (AB Dor, AU Mic, Proxima, ε Eri, EK Dra). 깊이 5-55%, 태양보다 10배 깊음. CME 질량 $\sim 10^{15}$ g 추정. 항성 CME 빈도 관측의 최초의 유효 수단 — 외계행성 거주가능성 평가에 중요.

7. **Magnetic confinement suppresses stellar CMEs** / **자기 억제가 항성 CME를 억누른다** — Alvarado-Gómez simulations: dipole field ≥75 G can fully suppress CMEs up to $3 \times 10^{32}$ erg. Explains paradox of abundant stellar flares vs. scarce stellar CMEs. Strong stellar magnetic fields trap eruptions, producing "failed eruptions" that may still generate weak thermal or expansion-driven dimmings.
   Alvarado-Gómez 시뮬레이션: ≥75 G 쌍극 field가 $3 \times 10^{32}$ erg까지 CME 완전 억제. 풍부한 항성 플레어 대비 희소한 항성 CME 역설 설명. 강한 항성 자기장이 eruption을 가두어 "실패한 eruption" 생성 — 여전히 약한 열적·팽창 dimming 가능.

8. **Dimmings are space weather early warning** / **디밍은 우주 환경 조기 경보 신호이다** — On-disk dimmings appear tens of minutes before CMEs enter coronagraph field of view. SolarDemon (Kraaikamp & Verbeeck 2015) provides real-time detection. Future: Vigil mission (L5, 2031) will enable off-Sun-Earth-line EUV imaging of Earth-directed events. Dimmings can distinguish Earth-directed halo CMEs from backsided events via non-radial expansion signatures (Möstl 2015, Chikunova 2023, Jain 2024).
   원반 dimming은 CME가 coronagraph에 진입하기 수십 분 전에 나타남. SolarDemon(Kraaikamp & Verbeeck 2015)이 실시간 검출 제공. 향후 Vigil 미션(L5, 2031)이 지구 지향 이벤트의 off-Sun-Earth-line EUV 이미징 제공. Dimming은 비방사상 팽창 신호로 지구 지향 halo CME과 후방 이벤트 구별.

## 4. Mathematical Summary / 수학적 요약

### (1) Dimming intensity fractional drop / 디밍 강도 상대적 감소
The observed intensity in an optically-thin EUV line relative to pre-event:
$$\frac{\Delta I}{I_0} = \frac{I(t) - I_0}{I_0}$$
Typical values: $\Delta I/I_0 \sim -80\%$ to $-90\%$ in 193 Å core dimmings; $\sim -10\%$ to $-30\%$ in secondary dimmings.

### (2) Coronal mass loss / 코로나 질량 손실
From density change × volume:
$$\boxed{M = \delta N \cdot S \cdot L \cdot m_p}$$
- $\delta N$: electron density change (cm⁻³), typically $10^8$-$10^9$
- $S$: dimming area (cm²), typically $10^{18}$-$10^{20}$
- $L$: dimming depth (cm), approximated as $L \approx \sqrt{S}$ or from scale height models
- $m_p$: proton mass ($1.67 \times 10^{-24}$ g)
- Example: $\delta N = 5 \times 10^8$ cm⁻³, $S = 10^{19}$ cm², $L = 3 \times 10^9$ cm → $M \approx 2.5 \times 10^{13}$ g.
- Typical mass loss: 20-60% of white-light CME mass.

### (3) EM decrease from DEM analysis / DEM 분석에서 EM 감소
Emission measure over LOS:
$$EM(T) = \int n_e^2(T, s) \, ds, \quad EM_{total} = \int DEM(T) \, dT$$
Dimming causes EM drop at $\log(T/K) = 6.0$-$6.3$ while peak temperature changes by <10%. $EM_{dim}/EM_{pre} \approx 0.1$-$0.5$ in core dimmings.

### (4) Dimming area growth rate / 디밍 면적 성장률
$$\frac{dA}{dt}, \qquad \frac{d\Phi}{dt} = \int_A B_z \, dA$$
Impulsive phase: sharp peak in $dA/dt$ lasting 10-120 min. Correlates with CME acceleration phase and GOES SXR derivative.

### (5) Magnetic flux in dimming / 디밍의 자기 flux
$$\Phi_{+} = \int_{A_+} B_z \, dA, \quad \Phi_{-} = \int_{A_-} |B_z| \, dA, \quad \Phi_d \approx \frac{\Phi_+ + \Phi_-}{2}$$
Typical values $\Phi_d \sim 10^{19}$-$10^{22}$ Mx, balanced across polarities. Relation to ICME: $\Phi_d \lesssim \Phi_p$ (poloidal flux).

### (6) Exponential recovery / 지수 회복
$$I(t) = I_{min} + (I_0 - I_{min}) \left[1 - \exp\left(-\frac{t - t_2}{\tau_{rec}}\right)\right]$$
with $\tau_{rec} \approx 4.8 \pm 0.3$ hr. Two-step recovery (steeper early + gradual late) in ~25% of events.

### (7) Expansion model (Aschwanden 2016, §3.5) / 팽창 모델
For self-similar or linear expansion at polytropic index $\eta$:
$$\frac{T}{T_0} = \left(\frac{L_0}{L}\right)^{\alpha \eta}, \qquad \frac{I_\lambda}{I_{\lambda,0}} \approx \left(\frac{L}{L_0}\right)^{1-2\alpha} \frac{R_\lambda(T)}{R_\lambda(T_0)}$$
- $\alpha = 1$: linear LOS expansion
- $\alpha = 3$: self-similar isotropic expansion
- $\eta = 0$: isothermal; $\eta = 2/3$: adiabatic (monoatomic)
- $R_\lambda(T)$: instrumental response function
- Prediction: for $T_0 = 1.5$ MK and $L/L_0 = 1.5$, AIA 211 Å shows deepest dimming.

### (8) Stellar CME mass estimate (Loyd et al. 2022) / 항성 CME 질량
Fractional dimming depth:
$$\delta_{max} = \frac{F_{CME}}{F_{pre}}$$
Optically thin emission:
$$F = \int_V G(T, n_e) \, n_e n_H \, dV \approx G(T,n) n^2 V$$
CME mass:
$$\boxed{m = \frac{\mu \, \delta_{max} \, F_{pre}}{n \, G(T,n)} = \frac{\mu \, \delta_{max} \, EM_{pre}}{n}}$$
- Linear relation $m \propto \delta_{max}$ (stellar)
- vs. $m \propto \delta_{max}^{1/2}$ for solar (Mason 2016, which assumes fixed dimming volume)
- For ε Eri (Loyd 2022): $\delta_{max} = 0.81$ in Fe XXI → $m \approx 10^{15.2}$ g.

### (9) Dimming depth comparison solar vs. stellar / 태양 대 항성 디밍 깊이
| Type | $\delta_{max}$ | Duration | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Solar (EVE 150-250 Å) | 0.5-5% (μ=2.18%) | 4-12 hr (μ=7.12 hr) | Veronig 2021 |
| Stellar (XMM 0.2-2 keV) | 5-55% (μ=22.7%) | 1->10 hr (μ=4.41 hr) | Veronig 2021 |
| ε Eri (HST/COS Fe XXI) | 81±5% | ~3 hr | Loyd 2022 |
| EK Dra (X-ray post-flare) | ~10% | ~hrs | Namekata 2024 |

Stellar dimmings are ~10× deeper, reflecting higher magnetic activity and larger CME-to-quiescent-emission ratios on active cool stars.

### (10) Worked example: CME mass from dimming / 작업 예시: 디밍 기반 CME 질량
Consider the SOL2011-02-13 M6.7 event (Dissauer 2019, Fig. 9 of the paper):
- Observed dimming area at end of impulsive phase: $A = 5 \times 10^{10}\,\mathrm{km^2} = 5 \times 10^{20}\,\mathrm{cm^2}$
- Unsigned magnetic flux: $\Phi \approx 6 \times 10^{21}$ Mx
- Density change from DEM/EIS analysis: $\delta N \approx 3 \times 10^8$ cm⁻³
- Depth assumption: $L \approx \sqrt{A} \approx 2.2 \times 10^{10}$ cm
- Mass estimate: $M \approx 3 \times 10^8 \times 5 \times 10^{20} \times 2.2 \times 10^{10} \times 1.67 \times 10^{-24}$ g $\approx 5.5 \times 10^{15}$ g
- This matches the white-light CME mass from LASCO within a factor ~2.

실제 예: SOL2011-02-13 M6.7 이벤트에서 dimming 면적 $5 \times 10^{20}\,\mathrm{cm^2}$, 밀도 변화 $3 \times 10^8$ cm⁻³, 깊이 $\sim \sqrt{A}$ 가정 시 CME 질량 $\sim 5.5 \times 10^{15}$ g으로 LASCO 백광 CME 질량과 2배 이내 일치.

### (11) Worked example: stellar Proxima Cen dimming / 작업 예시: Proxima Cen 항성 디밍
From Fig. 57 of the paper (Veronig 2021), Proxima Cen 2017-09-07 event:
- XMM-Newton 0.2-2 keV count rate drop: $\delta_{max} \approx 0.36$
- Pre-flare X-ray luminosity: $L_X \approx 4 \times 10^{26}$ erg/s (Proxima quiescent)
- Duration: $\sim 3$ hr
- Using Loyd (2022) formula with typical Fe XII emissivity $G(T,n) \sim 10^{-25}$ erg·cm³/s and $n \sim 10^{10}$ cm⁻³:
  $$m \approx \frac{\mu \delta_{max} F_{pre}}{n G(T,n)} \sim 10^{15}\text{-}10^{16}\,\mathrm{g}$$
- This is comparable to largest solar CMEs ($\sim 10^{16}$ g) even though Proxima is much less luminous. The deeper fractional dimming compensates.

Proxima Cen 2017-09-07 이벤트: XMM-Newton 0.2-2 keV에서 $\delta_{max} \approx 0.36$, 지속 3시간, CME 질량 $\sim 10^{15-16}$ g 추정 — Proxima 광도가 훨씬 낮음에도 가장 큰 태양 CME에 필적.
