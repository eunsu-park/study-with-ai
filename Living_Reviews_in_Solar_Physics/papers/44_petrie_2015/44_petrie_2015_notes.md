# 44. Solar Magnetism in the Polar Regions / 태양 극지역의 자기장

---

## 1. Core Contribution / 핵심 기여

### English
Petrie (2015) is the canonical Living Review on the Sun's polar magnetic fields. The polar fields are defined as the large-scale (|b| > ~60°), approximately unipolar magnetic field distributions at the heliographic poles. Although individually weak on average (flux density ~5 G at cycle minimum, peaking ~10 G right at the pole), they (i) dominate the global coronal magnetic structure during most of the solar cycle via the axial dipole moment, (ii) supply the bulk of the interplanetary magnetic field (IMF) through polar coronal holes, and (iii) act as the seed of the next activity cycle in Babcock–Leighton dynamo models. The review synthesizes four decades of full-disk line-of-sight (LOS) magnetogram observations (MWO since 1960s, NSO Kitt Peak since 1974, WSO since 1976, SOLIS/VSM from 2003, SDO/HMI from 2010) together with the revolutionary Hinode SOT/SP vector observations (from 2006) that first resolved the small-scale "kilogauss" flux patches embedded within the weak polar background.

### Korean / 한국어
Petrie (2015)는 태양 극자기장에 관한 표준 Living Review이다. 극자기장은 헬리오그래픽 극점 부근 (|b| > 약 60°)의 대규모 단극 자기장 분포를 의미한다. 평균적으로 약한 편이지만 (극소기 flux density 약 5 G, 극점 최대 약 10 G), (i) 축 dipole 모멘트를 통해 주기 대부분 기간의 전역 코로나 구조를 지배하고, (ii) 극 coronal hole을 통해 행성간 자기장 (IMF) 대부분을 공급하며, (iii) Babcock–Leighton 발전기 모델에서 다음 활동주기의 씨앗이 된다. 본 리뷰는 40년간의 전디스크 line-of-sight 자기그램 관측 (MWO 1960년대, NSO Kitt Peak 1974–, WSO 1976–, SOLIS/VSM 2003–, SDO/HMI 2010–)과 함께 소규모 "킬로가우스" 자속 농집부를 최초 분해한 혁신적 Hinode SOT/SP 벡터 관측 (2006–)을 종합한다.

The review proceeds in three thematic sections: (§2) observations — high-resolution Hinode maps, radial-field test, synoptic vector maps, pole-filling interpolation, and the magnetic butterfly diagram spanning cycles 21–24; (§3) kinematic flux-transport models — Babcock (1961) phenomenology, Leighton (1964, 1969) diffusion model, the role of meridional flow (Sheeley et al. 1985; Wang et al. 1989), evolving "synchronic" synoptic maps (Worden & Harvey 2000; ADAPT), magnetoconvective coupling (Schrijver 2001), and the anomalously weak cycle 23 polar fields; and (§4) the polar fields' influence on the solar atmosphere and heliosphere — global PFSS structure, coronal holes, solar wind speed via the Wang–Sheeley–Arge model, polar faculae as century-scale proxies, high-latitude filaments, and prominence eruptions/CMEs.

본 리뷰는 3개 주제 섹션으로 진행된다: (§2) 관측 — 고해상도 Hinode 맵, 방사형 가정 검증, synoptic 벡터 맵, 극 pole-filling 보간, cycles 21–24 자기 butterfly diagram; (§3) 운동학적 flux-transport 모델 — Babcock (1961) 현상학, Leighton (1964, 1969) 확산 모델, meridional flow의 역할 (Sheeley et al. 1985; Wang et al. 1989), 진화하는 "synchronic" synoptic 맵 (Worden & Harvey 2000; ADAPT), magnetoconvective coupling (Schrijver 2001), 비정상적으로 약한 cycle 23 극자기장; (§4) 극자기장이 태양 대기·heliosphere에 미치는 영향 — 전역 PFSS 구조, coronal hole, Wang–Sheeley–Arge 모델을 통한 태양풍 속도, 세기적 대리지표로서 polar faculae, 고위도 filament, prominence eruption/CME.

## 2. Reading Notes / 읽기 노트

### Part I: Observations of the Polar Magnetic Field / 극자기장 관측 (pp. 7–34)

#### §2.1 High-resolution Hinode observations / 고해상도 Hinode 관측 (pp. 7–13)

**English.** Polar-field measurement is challenging because (i) the B0 tilt angle between the solar rotation axis and the ecliptic is only 7.25°, so optimal viewing angles at the north/south pole occur just once per year (8 September and 6 March respectively), (ii) foreshortening degrades effective spatial resolution toward the limb, and (iii) intrinsic polar fields are much weaker than active-region fields. Prior to Hinode, polar vector observations were limited to ground-based snapshots with variable seeing. Tsuneta et al. (2008) used Hinode SOT/SP on 2007 March 16 (B0 ≈ −7°, south pole fully visible) to produce the first high-resolution, unambiguously-inverted polar vector map (Figure 1). The image revealed numerous kilogauss-strength, coherently unipolar vertical flux patches, each coincident with a bright polar facula in the continuum. Crucially, the distribution of the inclination angle was bimodal: peaks at <25° (vertical) and >65° (horizontal) relative to local normal. The strong patches were vertical while weaker, small patches were horizontal — this bimodality allowed Ito et al. (2010) to resolve the usual 180° azimuth ambiguity intrinsic to Zeeman measurements.

**Key numbers**: Total polar flux above 70° latitude = 5.6×10²¹ Mx (nominal filling factor) to 2.5×10²² Mx (filling factor set to 1). If spread uniformly, average strength = 3.1–13.9 G. After 50% stray light correction → ~10 G average, consistent with lower-resolution WSO/NSO estimates. Shiota et al. (2012) extended to 2007–2012: large (>10¹⁷ Mx) patches were polarity-unbalanced (same sign as polar cap) and cycle-dependent, while small (<10¹⁷ Mx) mixed-polarity patches were cycle-invariant. Year-by-year Hinode data (2008–2012) show that the polar cap net flux decreased faster in the north than the south during the rise of cycle 24 — consistent with the earlier reversal of the north polar field.

**Korean.** 극자기장 측정은 다음 이유로 어렵다: (i) 태양 회전축과 황도면의 B0 기울기 각이 7.25°에 불과하여 북/남극의 최적 관측 각은 연 1회만 (각각 9월 8일, 3월 6일) 발생; (ii) foreshortening이 림 쪽 유효 공간해상도를 저하; (iii) 내재적 극 자기장이 active region 자기장보다 훨씬 약함. Hinode 이전 극 벡터 관측은 seeing이 일정치 않은 지상 snapshot에 국한되었다. Tsuneta et al. (2008)은 Hinode SOT/SP로 2007년 3월 16일 (B0 ≈ −7°, 남극 완전 가시) 최초의 고해상도, 모호하지 않은 inversion 결과를 얻은 극 벡터 맵 (Figure 1)을 제작하였다. 영상은 다수의 킬로가우스 강도, 일관되게 unipolar한 수직 flux 패치를 드러냈고, 각 패치는 continuum에서의 밝은 polar facula와 일치했다. 중요한 점은 inclination 각도 분포가 이중봉 (bimodal)이었다는 것: <25° (수직)과 >65° (수평)에서 각각 봉우리. 강한 패치는 수직, 약하고 작은 패치는 수평이었고, 이 이중성 덕분에 Ito et al. (2010)이 Zeeman 측정의 일반적 180° 방위 모호성을 해결할 수 있었다.

**핵심 수치**: 위도 70° 이상 총 극자속 = 5.6×10²¹ Mx (nominal filling factor) ~ 2.5×10²² Mx (filling factor=1). 균일 분포 가정 시 평균 강도 = 3.1–13.9 G. 50% stray light 보정 후 → 평균 약 10 G, 저해상도 WSO/NSO 추정치와 일치. Shiota et al. (2012)는 2007–2012로 확장: 큰 (>10¹⁷ Mx) 패치는 polarity가 극관과 같은 부호로 편향되어 cycle 의존적, 작은 (<10¹⁷ Mx) 혼합 polarity 패치는 cycle 불변. 2008–2012년 연도별 Hinode 데이터는 cycle 24 상승기 동안 북극 순 자속이 남극보다 빠르게 감소했음을 보였고, 이는 북극 자기장의 이른 반전과 일치.

#### §2.2 Are polar fields radial? Topknot distribution / 극자기장은 방사형인가? 톱노트 분포 (pp. 13–17)

**English.** Svalgaard et al. (1978) devised an elegant test: bin WSO LOS measurements by the heliocentric angle ρ and plot against cos ρ. For a purely radial field, B_LOS = B_r · cos ρ, yielding a rhombus-shaped plot. The data fit this pattern, and — combining this with the annual B0-angle variation — they fitted a cos^n θ latitudinal profile to 1 year of WSO data, finding B_p cos^n θ with **n = 8, B_pole ≈ 10 G at the pole** (with flux density at 55° of ~6 G). Wang & Sheeley Jr (1988) confirmed using Wilcox + Mt. Wilson PFSS models: n = 7.75, B_p = 5.7 G. This sharply concentrated "topknot" (much narrower than a dipole, n=1) became the standard representation.

$$
B_r(\theta) = B_\text{pole}\, \cos^n\theta, \qquad n \approx 8\text{–}10
$$

Petrie & Patrikeeva (2009) refined the analysis by bundling flux elements in time-series of SOLIS magnetograms and solved the linear system:
$$
B_r \cos\rho_1 + B_\theta \sin\rho_1 = c_1 + c_2 L,\qquad B_r \cos\rho_2 + B_\theta \sin\rho_2 = c_3 + c_4 L
$$
at colatitude L. Photospheric fields were nearly radial poleward of ±65° (tilt histogram peak at 1.8 ± 10.8°), while chromospheric fields (SOLIS Ca II 8542 Å) were significantly tilted (5.5 ± 35°) — reflecting canopy expansion. Final fit: **n = 8.8, B_pole = −5.3 G (north); n = 9.7, B_pole = 5.8 G (south)** from 2003–2008 SOLIS data. Rudenko (2004) criticized the rhombus approach — a non-radial field with sign-change across the limb could mimic the same pattern — but the Petrie & Patrikeeva bundle analysis bypassed this.

**Korean.** Svalgaard et al. (1978)의 우아한 테스트: WSO LOS 측정을 헬리오중심각 ρ로 binning하여 cos ρ에 대해 플롯. 순수 방사형이면 B_LOS = B_r · cos ρ → 마름모 모양. 데이터는 이 패턴을 따랐고, 연주기 B0-각 변화와 결합하여 1년치 WSO 데이터에 cos^n θ 위도 프로파일 맞춤 → **n=8, B_pole ≈ 10 G**. Wang & Sheeley Jr (1988): WSO + MWO PFSS로 n=7.75, B_p=5.7 G 확인. 이 급격히 집중된 "톱노트" (dipole n=1 보다 훨씬 좁음)가 표준 표현으로 자리잡았다.

Petrie & Patrikeeva (2009)는 SOLIS 시계열에서 flux element를 번들로 묶어 위 선형계를 풀어, 광구 자기장은 ±65° 이상에서 거의 방사형 (tilt 1.8 ± 10.8°), 반면 채층 (SOLIS Ca II 8542 Å)은 상당히 기울어짐 (5.5 ± 35°) — canopy 팽창 반영. 최종 fit: **n=8.8, B_pole=−5.3 G (north); n=9.7, B_pole=5.8 G (south)** (2003–2008 SOLIS). Rudenko (2004)는 rhombus 접근을 비판 (림에서 부호 전환하는 non-radial field가 같은 패턴을 만들 수 있음)했지만 Petrie & Patrikeeva 번들 분석은 이를 회피.

#### §2.3–§2.4 Vector synoptic maps and pole-filling / 벡터 synoptic 맵과 극 pole-filling (pp. 18–22)

**English.** Gosain et al. (2013) produced vector synoptic Carrington maps from SOLIS/VSM — B_r, B_θ, B_φ — but polar latitudes remained blank because high-latitude fields (~5 G) fall below the Stokes inversion reliability threshold of SOLIS/VSM (1″ pixel). Hinode has demonstrated feasibility but not in routine synoptic mode. LOS-only synoptic maps (GONG, SOLIS, HMI, WSO) remain the workhorse for modeling the large-scale polar field because they reach down to ~1 G with optimal B0 angles.

The **pole-filling problem**: with all Earth-based magnetographs confined to the ecliptic plane and B0 = 7.25°, one pole is invisible half the year. Missing pixels must be filled. Liu et al. (2007) compared seven techniques (cubic spline, polynomial surface fitting, topknot fit B_p cos^n θ, Schrijver 2001 flux-transport) and found that 2D polynomial surface fitting combined with temporal interpolation is best. Sun et al. (2011) refined this with B0-optimized annual measurements and low-order polynomial time interpolation; their hybrid maps improved Wang–Sheeley–Arge (WSA) predictions of solar wind speed and IMF polarity versus ACE in-situ data compared to 1-D interpolation (Figure 12).

**Korean.** Gosain et al. (2013): SOLIS/VSM으로부터 B_r, B_θ, B_φ 벡터 synoptic Carrington 맵 제작. 그러나 극지역은 공백으로 남았다 (고위도 ~5 G 자기장이 SOLIS/VSM의 1″ 픽셀 Stokes inversion 신뢰도 이하). Hinode가 가능성을 보였으나 routine synoptic은 미달성. LOS-only synoptic 맵 (GONG, SOLIS, HMI, WSO)이 대규모 극자기장 모델링의 주역 (최적 B0 각에서 ~1 G 도달).

**극 pole-filling 문제**: 지상 자기그래프는 황도면 근처에 있고 B0=7.25°이므로 연중 절반은 한쪽 극이 보이지 않음. Liu et al. (2007)은 7가지 기법 (cubic spline, 다항식 곡면 fitting, 톱노트 fit B_p cos^n θ, Schrijver flux-transport 등)을 비교해 2D 저차 다항식 곡면 fitting + 시간 보간이 최선이라 결론. Sun et al. (2011)은 이를 B0-최적 연간 측정 + 저차 다항식 시간 보간으로 정제; 그들의 하이브리드 맵은 Wang–Sheeley–Arge (WSA)의 태양풍 속도 및 IMF polarity 예측을 ACE in-situ 데이터 대비 1D 보간보다 크게 향상시켰다 (Figure 12).

#### §2.5–§2.6 Radial-field PFSS and chromospheric maps / 방사형 가정 PFSS와 채층 맵 (pp. 21–27)

**English.** Wang & Sheeley Jr (1992) showed that simply dividing LOS data by cos ρ before running PFSS produces dramatically better coronal-hole and streamer-belt predictions than raw LOS data. The radial-field method's larger polar flux correctly reproduces the dominant polar coronal holes visible in He I 10830 Å synoptic maps (Figure 13), and the flatter K-coronal streamer brightness (Figure 14) matches SOLWIND observations. This vindicated the radial-field assumption at the photosphere. Jin et al. (2013) alternatively used chromospheric Ca II 8542 Å LOS data from SOLIS/VSM to produce hybrid radial-field maps after subtracting meridional-component averages; their chromospheric PFSS extrapolations were modestly superior to GONG photospheric maps but showed systematic polar-field overestimation. Overall, photospheric radial + LOS remains the standard boundary for coronal models.

**Korean.** Wang & Sheeley Jr (1992)는 PFSS 실행 전 LOS 데이터를 cos ρ로 나누는 단순 radial-field 방법이 raw LOS 데이터보다 극적으로 더 나은 coronal hole 및 streamer belt 예측을 산출함을 보였다. Radial-field의 더 큰 극자속은 He I 10830 Å synoptic 맵의 지배적 polar coronal hole을 정확히 재현 (Figure 13)하고 평탄한 K-코로나 streamer 밝기 (Figure 14)가 SOLWIND 관측과 일치. 이는 광구 수준에서 방사형 가정을 입증했다. Jin et al. (2013)은 대안으로 SOLIS/VSM 채층 Ca II 8542 Å LOS 데이터로부터 meridional 성분 평균 제거 후 하이브리드 방사형 맵 제작; 그들의 채층 PFSS 외삽은 GONG 광구 맵보다 약간 우수했으나 극자기장 과대추정 경향. 종합적으로 광구 radial + LOS가 여전히 코로나 모델 표준 경계.

#### §2.7 Magnetic butterfly diagram / 자기 butterfly diagram (pp. 27–34)

**English.** The Kitt Peak butterfly diagram (Figure 17) covers cycles 21–24, showing:
1. **Spörer's law** — active-field emergence at ±30° at cycle start, drifting equatorward;
2. **Polar-field reversal** near activity maximum, *not* simultaneously at both poles;
3. **Poleward surges** at ±50°–60° between active and polar latitudes as streaks of one dominant (trailing) polarity; surge width ≈ 1–1.5 yr, the meridional flow travel time;
4. **"Rush to the poles"** — steady migration of decayed trailing-polarity flux that builds the polar cap;
5. **No equatorward high-latitude counter-cell** — flux transport above ±50° is exclusively poleward (Howe et al. 2013).

The latitude centroids of positive and negative flux in each hemisphere (Figure 18) swap positions each cycle — a signature of Joy's law tilt. Petrie (2012) found that the annual-averaged high-latitude (±50°) poleward stream fluxes correlate with (active-region flux) × (centroid latitude displacement), and these with the ±63°–70° annual polar-field changes. Around 2002–2003 (cycle 23 maximum), the two centroids converged, the net flux contribution from active regions shrank, and the polar fields ceased to strengthen — a likely root cause of the cycle 23 weak polar fields. Cycle 23/24: the north polar field reversed first, then a 2013 negative-flux surge threatened it; the south polar field was fed steady negative flux and reversed robustly. As of writing (2015), the polar fields remained highly asymmetric.

Figure 19: 10-day averages of north (red) and south (blue) polar fields at Kitt Peak (top, 63°–70°, radial) and WSO (bottom, ~55°–pole, LOS). Since the cycle 23 reversal, the polar fields have been only ~60% as strong as before — a record-low level contributing to the 2008–2009 anomalous minimum.

**Korean.** Kitt Peak butterfly diagram (Figure 17)은 cycles 21–24를 포괄하며 다음을 보여준다:
1. **Spörer 법칙** — 주기 시작 시 ±30°에서 active field emergence, 이후 적도 방향 이동;
2. **극자기장 반전** 활동 극대기 부근, 두 극이 *동시는 아님*;
3. **극쪽 돌진 (poleward surge)** ±50°–60°에서 한 지배적 (trailing) polarity 의 줄기; 폭 약 1–1.5년 (meridional flow 이동 시간);
4. **"Rush to the poles"** — 쇠퇴한 trailing polarity 자속의 지속적 이동으로 극관 형성;
5. **적도방향 고위도 counter-cell 없음** — ±50° 이상 자속 수송은 전적으로 극쪽 (Howe et al. 2013).

각 반구의 양/음 자속 위도 centroid (Figure 18)는 주기마다 위치가 뒤바뀜 — Joy's law tilt의 징표. Petrie (2012): 연평균 고위도 (±50°) poleward stream 자속이 (active region 자속)×(centroid 위도 변위)와 상관, 이것이 ±63°–70° 연평균 극자기장 변화와 상관. 2002–2003년경 (cycle 23 극대기), 두 centroid가 수렴하고 active region 순 기여가 감소, 극자기장 성장 정지 — cycle 23 약한 극자기장의 주요 원인. Cycle 23/24: 북극 먼저 반전, 2013년 음자속 surge가 위협; 남극은 꾸준한 음자속 공급으로 견고히 반전. 2015년 집필 시점, 극자기장은 여전히 비대칭.

Figure 19: Kitt Peak (상, 63°–70°, radial)와 WSO (하, ~55°–극, LOS) 북 (적)·남 (청) 극자기장 10일 평균. Cycle 23 반전 이후 극자기장은 이전 60% 수준 — 2008–2009년 이례적 극소기에 기여한 기록적 저값.

### Part II: Kinematic Flux-Transport Models / 운동학적 flux-transport 모델 (pp. 35–59)

#### §3.1 Babcock's phenomenological model / Babcock 현상학 모델 (pp. 36–38)

**English.** Babcock (1961) built a qualitative 9-stage cycle diagram (Figure 20):
(a) minimum dipole field → (b) differential rotation winds up internal toroidal flux → (c) buoyant rise → (d,e) flux emerges as bipolar active regions (Hale's rule, Joy's law tilt) → (f) meridional expansion + decay → (g) meridional flow carries trailing-polarity remnants poleward, reversing the polar cap → (h) some flux diffuses equatorward via subsurface flow → (i) new polar dipole of opposite sign. This cartoon married the Zeeman-effect observations of cycle-19 polar reversal with Parker's α-effect ideas, and remains the backbone of Babcock–Leighton dynamo theory. Babcock noted the lack of model stability: a weak cycle need not be followed by weak polar fields, foreshadowing the cycle-23 puzzle.

**Korean.** Babcock (1961)은 정성적 9단계 주기 다이어그램 (Figure 20) 구축:
(a) 극소기 dipole field → (b) differential rotation으로 내부 toroidal flux 감김 → (c) 부력 상승 → (d,e) 쌍극 active region으로 emergence (Hale 법칙, Joy's law tilt) → (f) meridional 팽창 + 쇠퇴 → (g) meridional flow가 trailing-polarity 잔재를 극쪽으로 수송, 극관 반전 → (h) 일부 자속은 지하 flow로 적도쪽 확산 → (i) 반대 부호의 새 극 dipole. 이 개념도는 cycle-19 극 반전의 Zeeman 효과 관측을 Parker α-effect와 결합, Babcock–Leighton 발전기 이론의 뼈대로 남아있다. Babcock은 모델의 불안정성을 지적 — 약한 주기가 약한 극자기장으로 이어지지 않을 수 있음, cycle 23 퍼즐을 암시.

#### §3.2 Leighton's numerical kinematic model / Leighton 수치 운동학 모델 (pp. 38–39)

**English.** Leighton (1964) quantified Babcock's cartoon via a random-walk diffusion model of supergranular convection. Large-scale field evolves via the solution in spherical harmonics with time constants decreasing with multipole order ℓ. The lowest mode (dipole, ℓ=1) has decay time ≈ 5 yr ≈ half a solar cycle. Leighton (1969) added differential rotation and predefined bipolar emergence with Joy's law tilt. The model reproduced:
- Equatorward migration of active latitudes (butterfly wings);
- Poleward migration of the radial-field zero contour before reversal;
- 90° phase lag between polar-field and activity cycles (Sheeley 1964);
- Stretched, tilted butterfly "wings" (Figure 21) from the combined action of differential rotation shear and diffusion.

**Korean.** Leighton (1964)은 Babcock 개념도를 supergranular 대류의 random walk 확산 모델로 정량화. 대규모 자기장은 구면조화함수 해로 진화, multipole 차수 ℓ이 증가함에 따라 시간상수 감소. 최저 모드 (dipole, ℓ=1) 감쇠시간 ≈ 5년 ≈ 반주기. Leighton (1969)은 differential rotation과 Joy's law tilt를 가진 사전정의 쌍극 emergence를 추가. 모델은 다음을 재현:
- Active 위도의 적도방향 이동 (butterfly 날개);
- 반전 전 radial-field zero contour의 극쪽 이동;
- 극자기장–활동 주기 간 90° 위상 지연 (Sheeley 1964);
- Differential rotation shear와 확산의 결합으로 인한 늘어지고 기울어진 butterfly "날개" (Figure 21).

#### §3.3 Meridional flow and Babcock–Leighton models / Meridional flow와 Babcock–Leighton 모델 (pp. 39–42)

**English.** Observational detection of poleward surface meridional flow (Duvall 1979; Komm et al. 1993; Hathaway et al. 1996) at ~20 m/s revolutionized flux-transport modeling. Sheeley et al. (1985), Wang et al. (1989, 1991) showed that:
- Diffusion *alone* spreads flux too broadly (produces near-dipolar topknot with n ≈ 1);
- Observed n ≈ 8–11 requires poleward meridional flow of ~10 m/s;
- The major poleward surges of 1980–1982 required accelerated flow and enhanced emergence rates;
- Differential rotation alone has *no effect* on the axisymmetric component including the axial dipole moment — only diffusion + meridional flow reduce a bipole's dipole moment after emergence.

Wang & Sheeley Jr (1991) also showed that the dipole contribution from a tilted bipole at latitude ~15° asymptotes at ~0.11 G axial dipole strength for v ≈ 10 m/s, κ = 600 km²/s (Figure 23). Modern surface flux-transport (SFT) and ADAPT data-assimilation models (Arge et al. 2011, 2013) include Kalman-filter updates with daily magnetograms and provide robust real-time polar-field estimates.

**Korean.** Poleward 표면 meridional flow ≈ 20 m/s 관측 (Duvall 1979; Komm et al. 1993; Hathaway et al. 1996)은 flux-transport 모델링을 혁신. Sheeley et al. (1985), Wang et al. (1989, 1991):
- 확산 *만*으로는 자속이 너무 넓게 퍼짐 (거의 dipole 형태, n ≈ 1);
- 관측된 n ≈ 8–11은 ~10 m/s poleward meridional flow 필요;
- 1980–1982 거대 surge는 가속된 flow와 증가된 emergence rate 필요;
- Differential rotation 만으로는 axisymmetric 성분 (axial dipole 포함)에 *영향 없음* — 확산 + meridional flow 만이 쌍극의 dipole moment를 감소.

Wang & Sheeley Jr (1991): 위도 ~15°의 기울어진 쌍극 dipole 기여가 v ≈ 10 m/s, κ = 600 km²/s에서 axial dipole 강도 ~0.11 G로 점근 (Figure 23). 현대 표면 flux-transport (SFT) 및 ADAPT 데이터 동화 모델 (Arge et al. 2011, 2013)은 일일 자기그램과 Kalman 필터 업데이트로 robust한 실시간 극자기장 추정 제공.

#### §3.4–§3.5 Synchronic maps, magnetoconvective coupling / Synchronic 맵, magnetoconvective 결합 (pp. 42–50)

**English.** Worden & Harvey (2000) produced the first "synchronic" synoptic map — a full-surface snapshot at one instant — using differential rotation, meridional flow, supergranular diffusion, and continuous small-scale background flux input (1.8 G RMS). Schrijver (2001) added collision + fragmentation + magnetoconvective coupling (Schrijver 1989): active-region flux is more resistant to dispersal than weak network flux. Schrijver & Title (2001) applied this to active stars; Figure 26 shows that a star 30× more active than the Sun develops a nested polarity pattern around each polar cap that persists because the trailing flux takes longer to diffuse to and cancel the old polar flux. In the solar case, the trailing-polarity flux reaches the pole in ~1.4 yr (≪ 11 yr cycle), so solar polar reversals take only a fraction of a cycle — a single polarity dominates the polar cap for most of the cycle.

**Korean.** Worden & Harvey (2000)은 최초의 "synchronic" synoptic 맵 (한 순간 전표면 snapshot)을 differential rotation, meridional flow, supergranular 확산, 지속 소규모 배경 자속 입력 (1.8 G RMS)으로 제작. Schrijver (2001)은 충돌 + 파쇄 + magnetoconvective coupling (Schrijver 1989) 추가: active region 자속이 약한 network 자속보다 분산에 더 저항. Schrijver & Title (2001)은 active 별에 적용; Figure 26은 태양보다 30배 활동적인 별이 각 극관 주변에 중첩된 polarity 패턴을 형성하고 지속됨을 보임 (trailing 자속이 기존 극자속을 확산·상쇄하는 데 더 오래 걸리기 때문). 태양의 경우 trailing-polarity 자속이 ~1.4년 (≪ 11년 주기) 만에 극에 도달, 따라서 태양 극 반전은 주기의 일부분만 걸리고 단일 polarity가 극관의 대부분 기간을 지배.

#### §3.6 Unusual cycle 23 minimum / 비정상적 cycle 23 극소기 (pp. 50–59)

**English.** Cycle 23 peaked with sunspot number 120.8 (vs 164.5 for cycle 21 and 158.5 for cycle 22) — 27% weaker than cycle 21. But the cycle 23/24 minimum polar fields were ~40% weaker (Svalgaard & Cliver 2007; Hoeksema 2010). The minimum had 265 and 261 spotless days in 2008 and 2009, matched only by 1878, 1901, 1913. Candidate explanations:

1. **Slower meridional flow** would delay poleward transport and reduce polar field — but Wang et al. (2009) required 17 m/s (vs 14.5 m/s for cycle 22), Jiang et al. (2013) required a 55% flow increase. Observationally, Ulrich (2010) and Basu & Antia (2010) found *no* significant flow change in cycle 23 — this explanation is contested.

2. **Reduced Joy's law tilt** — Dasi-Espuig et al. (2010) (later corrected to be weaker) and Jiang et al. (2013) proposed an anti-correlation between tilt and cycle amplitude. McClintock & Norton (2013) found it only in the southern hemisphere. Independently supported only partially.

3. **Converging flux centroids** — Petrie (2012) showed that the positive and negative active-region flux centroids converged in each hemisphere around 2003, making their net poleward-transport contribution small. This convergence implies reduced poloidal-field generation even without changes in flow speed or tilt — consistent with the unchanging polar fields observed between 2002–2006 despite significant active-region flux.

4. **Meridional-flow perturbations near activity bands** (Jiang et al. 2010; Sun et al. 2015) — near-surface inflows toward active latitudes reduce bipole tilt and thus the polar-field contribution. Amplitude correlates with cycle strength.

DeRosa et al. (2012) showed that the low-order odd axisymmetric multipoles (ℓ=1 dipole, ℓ=3 octupole) follow the polar fields, while low-order even multipoles and high-order multipoles follow the sunspot cycle (Figure 31). The long-term stability of the primary family (antisymmetric) vs secondary family (symmetric) in a simple Babcock–Leighton dynamo shows that small asymmetries in bipole emergence or meridional flow can produce the observed hemispheric asymmetries (Figure 32).

**Korean.** Cycle 23는 sunspot number 120.8로 극대 (cycle 21 164.5, cycle 22 158.5 대비) — cycle 21보다 27% 약함. 그러나 cycle 23/24 극소기 극자기장은 40% 더 약했다 (Svalgaard & Cliver 2007; Hoeksema 2010). 2008, 2009년 spotless day 265, 261일로 1878, 1901, 1913년 이후 최다. 가설:

1. **느려진 meridional flow** — poleward 수송 지연, 극자기장 감소 예상. 그러나 Wang et al. (2009)은 17 m/s (cycle 22의 14.5 m/s 대비), Jiang et al. (2013)은 55% 증가를 요구. 관측적으로 Ulrich (2010), Basu & Antia (2010)는 cycle 23에서 *유의미한* flow 변화 없음을 발견 — 이 설명은 논쟁 중.

2. **축소된 Joy's law tilt** — Dasi-Espuig et al. (2010) (이후 약하게 수정) 및 Jiang et al. (2013)이 tilt와 cycle 진폭의 반상관 제시. McClintock & Norton (2013)은 남반구에서만 확인. 부분적 지지.

3. **수렴하는 flux centroid** — Petrie (2012): 2003년경 각 반구에서 양/음 active-region flux centroid가 수렴하여 순 poleward 수송 기여가 작아졌음을 보임. 이 수렴은 flow 속도 또는 tilt 변화 없이도 poloidal-field 생성 감소를 의미 — 2002–2006년 동안 상당한 active-region 자속에도 극자기장이 변화하지 않았던 관측과 일치.

4. **활동대 근처 meridional-flow 섭동** (Jiang et al. 2010; Sun et al. 2015) — active 위도 방향 근표면 inflow가 쌍극 tilt를 축소하여 극자기장 기여 감소. 진폭은 주기 강도와 상관.

DeRosa et al. (2012): 저차 odd axisymmetric multipole (ℓ=1 dipole, ℓ=3 octupole)은 극자기장을 따르고, 저차 even multipole과 고차 multipole은 sunspot 주기를 따름 (Figure 31). 단순 Babcock–Leighton 발전기의 primary family (반대칭) vs secondary family (대칭) 장기 안정성은 쌍극 emergence나 meridional flow의 작은 비대칭이 관측된 반구 비대칭을 산출할 수 있음을 보임 (Figure 32).

### Part III: Polar Fields and the Solar Atmosphere / 극자기장과 태양 대기 (pp. 60–89)

#### §4.1 Effects on global coronal structure / 전역 코로나 구조에 미치는 영향 (pp. 60–63)

**English.** The axial dipole (ℓ=1, m=0) and axial octupole (ℓ=3, m=0), dominated by the polar fields, correlate at cc=0.97 with the polar field strength (Petrie 2013). High-order odd/even non-axisymmetric multipoles (ℓ > 4 hexadecapole) correlate with sunspot number. Thus the polar fields dominate the large-scale coronal structure — the streamer belt position, heliospheric current sheet (HCS) tilt, and low-latitude coronal holes — for most of the cycle. At solar maximum, when polar fields reverse, non-axisymmetric components dominate and the streamer belt becomes highly warped ("hairy-ball" PFSS plots, Figure 33). During the long cycle 23/24 minimum, the axisymmetric dipole and octupole were ~40% weaker than the previous minimum; the HCS tilt remained high until ~2009 (Figure 35), producing a more complex coronal minimum with more low-latitude coronal holes.

**Korean.** 극자기장이 지배하는 axial dipole (ℓ=1, m=0)과 axial octupole (ℓ=3, m=0)은 극자기장 강도와 cc=0.97 상관 (Petrie 2013). 고차 odd/even non-axisymmetric multipole (ℓ > 4 hexadecapole)은 sunspot 수와 상관. 따라서 극자기장이 주기 대부분 기간의 대규모 코로나 구조 — streamer belt 위치, heliospheric current sheet (HCS) 기울기, 저위도 coronal hole — 를 지배한다. 태양 극대기, 극자기장 반전 시, non-axisymmetric 성분이 지배하고 streamer belt가 심하게 왜곡됨 ("hairy-ball" PFSS 플롯, Figure 33). 긴 cycle 23/24 극소기 동안 axisymmetric dipole과 octupole이 이전 극소기보다 ~40% 약했고, HCS 기울기가 ~2009년까지 높게 유지 (Figure 35) — 더 복잡한 코로나 극소기와 더 많은 저위도 coronal hole 산출.

#### §4.2 Coronal holes / 코로나 홀 (pp. 63–66)

**English.** Coronal holes are low-density, unipolar open-field regions. Three classes (Harvey & Recely 2002): polar (|b| > 60°), isolated low-latitude (active-region remnants), and transient (post-CME). Polar coronal holes source the fast solar wind (~700 km/s). Cycle 22/23 minimum: polar holes covered 7.9–8.1% (north) and 6.9–7.1% (south) of solar surface. Cycle 23/24 minimum: polar holes covered only 4.4–4.7% (north) and 5.3–6.0% (south) — a 40–45% decrease in the north, 15–25% in the south (de Toma 2011). Low-latitude holes covered 2–3% during cycles 21–22 but the extended cycle 23 minimum had more low-latitude structure, consistent with the weaker polar fields. Polar coronal holes appear bright in 17 GHz microwave imaging (Nobeyama radioheliograph), and the polar microwave butterfly diagram (Gopalswamy et al. 2012) shows the same ~50% diminution during cycle 23/24 minimum — independent confirmation of the weak polar fields.

**Korean.** Coronal hole은 저밀도·단극·open field 영역. 3가지 (Harvey & Recely 2002): polar (|b| > 60°), isolated 저위도 (active region 잔재), transient (post-CME). Polar coronal hole은 fast solar wind (~700 km/s) 공급. Cycle 22/23 극소기: polar hole이 태양 표면의 7.9–8.1% (북), 6.9–7.1% (남) 차지. Cycle 23/24 극소기: 4.4–4.7% (북), 5.3–6.0% (남)으로 감소 — 북 40–45%, 남 15–25% 감소 (de Toma 2011). 저위도 hole은 cycle 21–22 동안 2–3%였으나 cycle 23 장기 극소기에는 더 많은 저위도 구조 — 약한 극자기장과 일치. Polar coronal hole은 17 GHz 마이크로파 이미징 (Nobeyama radioheliograph)에서 밝게 보이고, 극 마이크로파 butterfly diagram (Gopalswamy et al. 2012)도 cycle 23/24 극소기 동안 동일한 ~50% 감소를 보여 약한 극자기장 독립 확인.

#### §4.3 Coronal hole structure and solar wind / 코로나 홀 구조와 태양풍 (pp. 66–73)

**English.** Wang & Sheeley Jr (1990) introduced the expansion-factor concept:
$$
f_s = (R_0/R_s)^2 \, B^p(R_0)/B^p(R_s)
$$
Low f_s (flux tube expands little from R_0 to R_s = 2.5 R_☉) → fast wind; high f_s → slow wind. Arge & Pizzo (2000) extended this to the WSA model: wind speed depends on f_s *and* the angular distance to the nearest coronal-hole boundary. WSA reproduces monthly-averaged wind speed well (Figure 39) and remains operational today. Ulysses polar orbits (1992–2008):
- **Orbit 1 (cycle 22/23 min)**: simple bimodal wind — fast/tenuous at high lat from polar coronal holes, slow/variable at low lat. Band of variability confined to equator.
- **Orbit 2 (cycle 23 max)**: complex structure at all latitudes — streamers, CMEs, coronal holes everywhere.
- **Orbit 3 (cycle 23/24 min)**: structure simpler but HCS more tilted; band of variability extended to higher lat than cycle 22/23 minimum.

Smith & Balogh (2008): r²B_r was independent of latitude at both minima (consistent with radial expansion of open flux), but the cycle 23/24 average (2.3 nT·AU²) was only 64% of the cycle 22/23 value (3.6 nT·AU²) — direct confirmation of weak polar fields. Fast wind in orbit 3 was 3% slower, 17% less dense, 20% less mass flux than orbit 1.

**Korean.** Wang & Sheeley Jr (1990): 팽창 인자 도입. 작은 f_s (R_0→R_s=2.5 R_☉ 사이 flux tube가 조금만 팽창) → fast wind; 큰 f_s → slow wind. Arge & Pizzo (2000)는 이를 WSA 모델로 확장: wind 속도는 f_s와 가장 가까운 coronal hole 경계까지 각거리에 의존. WSA는 월평균 wind 속도를 잘 재현 (Figure 39)하고 현재도 운용 중. Ulysses 극 궤도 (1992–2008):
- **Orbit 1 (cycle 22/23 극소기)**: 단순 이중 바람 — 고위도 fast/tenuous (polar coronal hole 기원), 저위도 slow/variable. 변동 band가 적도로 국한.
- **Orbit 2 (cycle 23 극대기)**: 모든 위도에서 복잡 — streamer, CME, coronal hole 도처.
- **Orbit 3 (cycle 23/24 극소기)**: 구조는 더 단순하나 HCS 더 기울고, 변동 band가 cycle 22/23 극소기보다 더 고위도로 확장.

Smith & Balogh (2008): r²B_r은 두 극소기 모두 위도 독립 (open flux 방사 팽창과 일치), 그러나 cycle 23/24 평균 (2.3 nT·AU²)은 cycle 22/23 값 (3.6 nT·AU²)의 64% — 약한 극자기장 직접 확인. Orbit 3 fast wind는 orbit 1 대비 3% 느림, 17% 희박, 20% 질량 flux 감소.

#### §4.4–§4.6 Polar faculae, filaments, prominence/CME / Polar faculae, filament, prominence/CME (pp. 73–89)

**English.** **Polar faculae** are bright intergranular accumulations of kG flux associated with the large, unipolar patches of §2.1. Sheeley Jr (1964) found polar faculae numbers lag the sunspot cycle by 90° — consistent with Babcock–Leighton. MWO data since 1906 and Makarov back to 1870 provide a century-scale polar-field proxy. Muñoz-Jaramillo et al. (2013) found that (sunspot area) × (Joy's-law tilt) correlates with subsequent polar flux at r=0.67–0.74 (p=0.99), but sunspot area alone does not correlate — underscoring the centrality of Joy's law tilt. Polar flux during minimum correlates with following cycle amplitude at r≈0.6, supporting Babcock–Leighton.

**Polar filaments** delineate neutral lines. Two polar crowns (primary + secondary) migrate poleward together at the meridional-flow rate; the primary crown disappears as the polar field reverses. McIntosh (2003) mapped polar-crown migration in Hα. Gaizauskas et al. (1983, 2001) traced "complexes of activity" (~10²³ Mx superclusters) whose decay fed poleward surges of flux that formed filament channels near the polar cap — connecting active-region flux to polar-field reversal.

**Prominence eruptions and CMEs**: approximately 60% of CMEs are associated with filament/prominence eruptions. High-latitude filament eruptions are especially important during cycle maximum, contributing to polar field reversals. Polar-field changes (field reversal) are connected to the "rush to the poles" of Hα-emitting material.

**Korean.** **Polar faculae**는 §2.1의 크고 단극인 kG flux 패치와 관련된 밝은 intergranular 집적체. Sheeley Jr (1964): polar faculae 수는 sunspot 주기보다 90° 지연 — Babcock–Leighton과 일치. 1906년부터 MWO 데이터, Makarov는 1870년까지 세기적 극자기장 대리 지표 제공. Muñoz-Jaramillo et al. (2013): (sunspot 면적) × (Joy's-law tilt)이 후속 극자속과 r=0.67–0.74 (p=0.99) 상관, 그러나 sunspot 면적 단독은 무상관 — Joy's law tilt의 중심성 강조. 극소기 극자속은 다음 주기 진폭과 r≈0.6 상관, Babcock–Leighton 지지.

**Polar filament**는 neutral line을 따름. 두 polar crown (primary + secondary)이 meridional flow 속도로 함께 극쪽 이동; primary crown은 극자기장 반전과 함께 사라짐. McIntosh (2003): Hα에서 polar crown 이동 매핑. Gaizauskas et al. (1983, 2001): "complex of activity" (~10²³ Mx supercluster) 의 쇠퇴가 자속의 poleward surge를 공급, 극관 근처 filament channel 형성 — active region 자속과 극자기장 반전을 연결.

**Prominence eruption과 CME**: CME의 약 60%가 filament/prominence eruption과 연관. 고위도 filament eruption은 극대기에 특히 중요, 극자기장 반전에 기여. 극자기장 변화는 Hα 방출 물질의 "rush to the poles"와 연결.

## 3. Key Takeaways / 핵심 시사점

1. **Polar fields are weak but geometrically dominant / 극자기장은 약하지만 기하학적으로 지배적**
   — At solar minimum the average polar flux density is only ~5 G (peaking ~10 G at the pole), yet because of its unipolarity over large spatial scales it supplies the axial dipole that controls the global coronal structure during most of the cycle. / 극소기 평균 극 flux density는 ~5 G (극점 최대 ~10 G)에 불과하지만, 대규모 단극 분포로 인해 주기 대부분 기간의 전역 코로나 구조를 제어하는 축 dipole을 제공한다.

2. **The topknot B_p cos^n θ with n ≈ 8–10 is a flux-transport signature / 톱노트 B_p cos^n θ (n ≈ 8–10)은 flux-transport 징표**
   — Pure diffusion produces n ≈ 1 (dipole), while observed polar-field concentration requires poleward meridional flow of ~10 m/s competing with κ ≈ 600 km²/s supergranular diffusion. The topknot's sharpness is therefore direct evidence for meridional circulation. / 순수 확산은 n ≈ 1 (dipole)을 산출, 관측된 극자기장 집중은 κ ≈ 600 km²/s supergranular 확산과 경쟁하는 ~10 m/s poleward meridional flow를 요구. 톱노트의 날카로움은 meridional 순환의 직접 증거.

3. **Hinode revealed the polar field is bimodal / Hinode가 극자기장의 이중성을 드러냄**
   — Two populations coexist: large (>10¹⁷ Mx) cycle-dependent kilogauss vertical patches matching polar faculae (determining polar cap polarity), and small (<10¹⁷ Mx) cycle-invariant mixed-polarity horizontal patches. Only the large vertical patches matter for the global cycle. / 두 population 공존: 큰 (>10¹⁷ Mx) cycle 의존 kG 수직 패치 (polar faculae와 일치, 극관 polarity 결정), 작은 (<10¹⁷ Mx) cycle 불변 혼합 polarity 수평 패치. 전역 주기에는 큰 수직 패치만 중요.

4. **Babcock–Leighton mechanism — Joy's law is the key / Babcock–Leighton 기작 — Joy's law이 핵심**
   — The 90° phase lag between polar fields and activity is produced by the decay of tilted bipolar regions whose following (trailing) polarity is preferentially transported poleward. Muñoz-Jaramillo et al. (2013) showed (sunspot area) × (Joy's tilt) correlates with polar flux at r≈0.7, while sunspot area alone does not — the tilt is the essential physics. / 극자기장과 활동 사이 90° 위상 지연은 기울어진 쌍극 region의 쇠퇴에 의해 생성, 이때 following (trailing) polarity가 선택적으로 극쪽 수송됨. Muñoz-Jaramillo et al. (2013): (sunspot 면적)×(Joy's tilt)은 극자속과 r≈0.7 상관, 그러나 sunspot 면적 단독은 무상관 — tilt가 본질적 물리.

5. **Cycle-23 weak polar fields are an open puzzle / Cycle 23 약한 극자기장은 미해결 퍼즐**
   — 40% reduction in polar field strength vs 27% reduction in sunspot number. Competing explanations: slower meridional flow (Wang 2009; Jiang 2013 require 15–55% increase — inconsistent with helioseismology), reduced Joy's tilt (weakly supported), converging flux centroids (Petrie 2012, cleanly observed), or activity-band flow perturbations (Jiang 2010; Sun 2015). Most likely a combination. / 극자기장 강도 40% 감소 vs sunspot 수 27% 감소. 경쟁 가설: 느려진 meridional flow (Wang 2009; Jiang 2013은 15–55% 증가 요구 — helioseismology와 불일치), 축소된 Joy's tilt (약한 지지), 수렴하는 flux centroid (Petrie 2012, 깔끔한 관측), 활동대 flow 섭동 (Jiang 2010; Sun 2015). 복합 가능성 높음.

6. **Polar fields source most of the interplanetary magnetic field / 극자기장이 행성간 자기장 대부분을 공급**
   — Ulysses measurements of r²B_r showed latitude-independent values at both polar orbits, but cycle 23/24 average (2.3 nT·AU²) was 64% of cycle 22/23 (3.6 nT·AU²), directly tracking the polar-field weakening. This is the connection between the solar dynamo and space weather: weaker polar fields → weaker IMF → less shielding from galactic cosmic rays. / Ulysses r²B_r 측정은 두 극 궤도에서 위도 독립 값을 보였으나 cycle 23/24 평균 (2.3 nT·AU²)은 cycle 22/23 (3.6 nT·AU²)의 64%로 극자기장 약화 직접 추적. 이는 태양 발전기와 우주환경의 연결: 약한 극자기장 → 약한 IMF → 은하 우주선 차폐 감소.

7. **PFSS models require radial-field boundary / PFSS 모델은 radial-field 경계 필요**
   — Wang & Sheeley (1992) demonstrated that dividing LOS measurements by cos ρ before PFSS extrapolation yields dramatically better coronal hole and streamer-belt predictions — confirming that the photospheric field is nearly radial and validating the radial-field assumption for global modeling. / Wang & Sheeley (1992): PFSS 외삽 전 LOS 측정을 cos ρ로 나누면 극적으로 더 나은 coronal hole과 streamer belt 예측 — 광구 자기장이 거의 방사형이며 전역 모델링 radial-field 가정 타당성 확인.

8. **Polar faculae provide a century-long precursor record / Polar faculae는 세기적 전조 기록 제공**
   — MWO data since 1906 (Muñoz-Jaramillo et al. 2012) and Makarov's Pulkovo record back to 1870 provide polar-field proxies well pre-dating magnetographs. These data show polar field at minimum correlates with the next cycle amplitude at r ≈ 0.6 — making polar-field observations the single most reliable long-range cycle-amplitude predictor. / 1906년부터 MWO (Muñoz-Jaramillo et al. 2012), Makarov의 Pulkovo 기록 1870년부터 자기그래프 이전 극자기장 대리 지표 제공. 이 데이터는 극소기 극자기장이 다음 주기 진폭과 r ≈ 0.6 상관 — 극자기장 관측이 단일 최고 신뢰성 장기 주기 진폭 예측 지표.

## 4. Mathematical Summary / 수학적 요약

### Polar field topknot distribution / 극자기장 톱노트 분포

$$
B_r(\theta) = B_\text{pole}\, \cos^n \theta
$$
- **θ**: colatitude (0 at pole, π/2 at equator) / 콜라티튜드 (극 0, 적도 π/2)
- **B_pole**: field strength at the pole / 극점 field 강도
- **n**: concentration index; observed n ≈ 8–10 / 집중 지수; 관측값 n ≈ 8–10
- Observed (Petrie & Patrikeeva 2009): B_pole = −5.3 G, n = 8.8 (north); B_pole = +5.8 G, n = 9.7 (south) / 관측치 (Petrie & Patrikeeva 2009): north B_pole=−5.3 G, n=8.8; south B_pole=+5.8 G, n=9.7

### Heliocentric-angle geometry for LOS projection / LOS 투영을 위한 헬리오중심각 기하

$$
\cos\rho = \sin B_0 \sin b + \cos B_0 \cos b \cos(l-l_0)
$$
$$
B_\text{LOS} = B_r \cos\rho + B_\theta \sin\rho\cos\phi_\theta + B_\phi \sin\rho\sin\phi_\phi
$$
For a purely radial field: B_LOS = B_r cos ρ.
- **ρ**: heliocentric angle between local radial vector and LOS / 지역 radial 벡터와 LOS 사이 헬리오중심각
- **B_0**: tilt angle of solar rotation axis (7.25°) / 태양 회전축 기울기 각 (7.25°)
- **b**: heliographic latitude of observed point / 관측점 헬리오그래픽 위도
- **l, l_0**: longitude, central meridian longitude / 경도, 중앙자오선 경도

### Zeeman line-of-sight measurement sensitivity / Zeeman LOS 측정 감도

$$
\Delta \lambda_B = \frac{e \lambda^2 g_\text{eff}}{4\pi m_e c^2}\, B_\parallel
$$
where B_∥ = |B| cos(θ_B) is the LOS component and θ_B is the angle between the magnetic vector and the LOS. For a radial field at heliographic latitude b, θ_B ≈ ρ so:
$$
B_\parallel \approx |B|\cos\rho \approx |B|\sin(b)\quad\text{(central meridian, annual avg B}_0\text{)}
$$
showing why polar signal requires the B0 tilt (sin b factor).
- **e, m_e, c**: electron charge, mass, speed of light / 전자 전하, 질량, 광속
- **g_eff**: effective Landé g factor / 유효 Landé g 인자
- **λ**: wavelength of the spectral line (e.g., 630.2 nm Fe I) / 스펙트럼 선 파장 (예: 630.2 nm Fe I)

### Polar cap flux integration / 극관 자속 적분

$$
\Phi_\text{cap}(\theta_0) = \int_0^{2\pi}\!\!\!\int_0^{\theta_0} R_\odot^2 B_r(\theta)\sin\theta\, d\theta\, d\phi = 2\pi R_\odot^2 B_\text{pole}\int_0^{\theta_0}\cos^n\theta\sin\theta\, d\theta
$$
$$
= 2\pi R_\odot^2 B_\text{pole}\,\frac{1-\cos^{n+1}\theta_0}{n+1}
$$
With R_☉ = 6.96×10¹⁰ cm, B_pole = 5 G, n = 9, θ_0 = 30° (≈0.524 rad, lat ≥ 60°):
$$
\Phi_\text{cap} = 2\pi (6.96\times10^{10})^2 \cdot 5 \cdot \frac{1-\cos^{10}(30°)}{10} \approx 4.4\times10^{22}\text{ Mx}
$$
Consistent with Hinode-based estimates 5.6×10²¹–2.5×10²² Mx for lat > 70°.

### Surface flux-transport equation / 표면 flux-transport 방정식

$$
\frac{\partial B_r}{\partial t} = -\frac{1}{R_\odot\sin\theta}\frac{\partial}{\partial\theta}\big[\sin\theta\, v(\theta) B_r\big] + \frac{\kappa}{R_\odot^2\sin\theta}\frac{\partial}{\partial\theta}\!\left[\sin\theta\frac{\partial B_r}{\partial\theta}\right] + S(\theta,\phi,t)
$$
- Advection term: poleward meridional flow v(θ) ≈ 10–20 m/s / 이류항: poleward meridional flow
- Diffusion term: κ ≈ 250–600 km²/s supergranular / 확산항: supergranular
- Source term S: Babcock–Leighton tilted-bipole emergence / 원천항 S: Babcock–Leighton 기울어진 쌍극 emergence

### Axial dipole moment / 축 dipole 모멘트

$$
D_{\text{axial}} = \frac{3}{4\pi}\int B_r(\theta,\phi)\cos\theta\, d\Omega
$$
Wang & Sheeley Jr (1991): for a single tilted bipole of flux Φ_0 = 2×10²² Mx at latitude 15°, asymptotic D_axial ≈ 0.11 G after ~5 yr evolution under v=10 m/s, κ=600 km²/s.

### Wang–Sheeley expansion factor / Wang–Sheeley 팽창 인자

$$
f_s = \left(\frac{R_0}{R_s}\right)^2 \frac{|B^p(R_0)|}{|B^p(R_s)|}
$$
Typical solar wind speed look-up (Wang & Sheeley 1990 Table 1): f_s = 3 → v ≈ 650 km/s (fast); f_s = 50 → v ≈ 350 km/s (slow). Arge & Pizzo (2000) WSA:
$$
v_{sw} = v_\text{fast} - \frac{v_\text{fast}-v_\text{slow}}{(1+f_s)^\alpha}(1-0.8 e^{-(\omega_b/\omega_0)^\beta})^3
$$
with ω_b the angular distance to the coronal-hole boundary.

### Open flux and IMF / Open flux와 IMF

$$
B_r(r=r_E) = \frac{1}{4\pi r_E^2}\oint_{R_s}|B_r| \, dA
$$
assuming radial expansion from source surface R_s to 1 AU. Typical cycle 22/23 minimum: 3 nT; cycle 23/24 minimum: 2 nT (Wang et al. 2009).
